Hello Friends!

This is an invitation to join a networking list for people who speak at
conferences, give workshops, or otherwise make presentations to the public
about homeschooling; and for conference and workshop planners and support
group contacts to discuss and coordinate promotion of homeschooling through
conferences, seminars, workshops and other events.

Our goal is to encourage dialogue between those who want to speak and give
presentations on homeschooling and those who provide the conferences,
workshops, and other forums to reach homeschoolers. This list includes an
ever-widening circle of writers, authors, columnists, speakers, conference
coordinators, support group leaders and contact persons.

TO JOIN THIS GROUP:

simply go to our site at:
http://www.egroups.com/subscribe/HEM-Conferences
and click the "JOIN" button

There's been some very interesting discussion already, which you can read
at the listsite above.

To post a message to this list, address it to HEM-Conferences@egroups.com

Please feel free to forward this message to other homeschooling lists!
