Okay, anyone who's known me from this and other lists, know how much
I beleive in treading lightly on this earth, using organic food,
natural cleaners and working for the balance.

WELL! I've been staying at a hotel for the past two weeks and my son
and I discovered the microwave. Okay, I will never buy one, or use it
more than a 2 - 3 times a year BUT, can you imagine how we felt when
we popped popcorn???? I mean, what an amazing thing!! I'm in shock,
I'm overwhelmed with disbelief!!!

WOW! I must say for people who use pre-packaged foods and modern
conveniences such as this.. geez.. life must be so much *easier*!!!

Cooking and preparing and shopping at obscure places not only hurts
our budget dramatically, it also eats away time I would like to spend
with my family... I know I won't change my ways.. but

Wowza!!!

kolleen
