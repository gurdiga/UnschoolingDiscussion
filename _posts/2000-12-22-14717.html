Another idea for "keeping" artwork is to take pictures of it. Especially if
you have a digital camera, you can keep photos of artwork without taking up
so much room. That way you do have room to keep the really special ones.
And don't forget to frame really nice ones to keep forever. Dad has some
"original" artwork framed and hanging is his office :-)

I love taking pictures of my little artist standing next to or holding
his/her work of art, it puts more history and perspective into the art when
you reminisce.
