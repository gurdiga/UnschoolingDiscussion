work program I would think. Does she have younger ones to use for leverage?

Hi,
The welfare dept/food stamps usually requires something like a job search.
here, in East Prov., RI - every friday, I went to job search training - really it was 2 - 9 women meeting and talking about how hard it is i.e very poor public transportation, no jobs, no cumputer training - etc.
We needed to fill out a form
we would list 5 places we went to during the week.
Usuaslly, I would hit a mall w/ Brianna
grab 5 applications, fill them out,
leave them.
On the state form; there is a place for "outcome"
I would write in
Left app. w/ clerk
or
will drop off app. next week.

That was it.

It's important to realize that the sysytem sucks, but the people usually mean well.

Janis
