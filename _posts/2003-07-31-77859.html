Our day has finally come! We're moving tomorrow. Christopher is the
only one that is not nervous, he's ready to go. It's amazing how much
he has changed since we found unschooling and joined this list. It's
amazing how much we as parents are changing....slowly<g>

Just wanted to say thank-you to everyone on the list for all of your
wisdom and interesting conversations. We're going to take a break now
and regroup as a family and hope to be back sometime late August.

One last thing before I do go and the subject gets old. I wanted to
apologize for posting a link to a RUL. For the part I was on there, I
did not see no dislike of this list or of Sandra. Had I known the
story or had seen what went on before, I would have never posted the
link that caused so much nonsense. I love this list and the people
here and would never want anything bad to be said about either. For
those that are still mailing me, putting blame on me for all of this
starting, please realize that I wouldn't be on RUL, defending Sandra
nor this list if I didn't feel so strongly about them, I just
wouldn't waste my time.

Have a nice summer everyone!

Kim
