Hi my name is Tina,

I homeschool my 11 year old son in Utah. We recently began a project
that I would like to share with you.

Homeschooler's World Quilt

To be a part of the project each family or group creates a quilt
packet that tells about where they are from. The quilt packet consist
of a quilt square, brochure and a postcard. Exchange your quilt
packets with other members to create your own unique quilt of
culture. To become part of the project sign up on the yahoo-groups
page and exchange your quilt packets with other families and groups
from around the world. Currently there are 50 families involved,
including England, Saudi Arabia,Sweden, New Zealand and Australia....
come join us on this fun exchange of culture.

See pictures of some of the squares created by participating families-
<http://www.creativewebpagemagik.com/quiltpics.html>http://www.creativewebpagemagik.com/quiltpics.html

Yahoo-groups main page( you can sign up here)
-<http://groups.yahoo.com/group/HomeschoolersWorldQuilt>http://groups.yahoo.com/group/HomeschoolersWorldQuilt


Tina & Brandon
