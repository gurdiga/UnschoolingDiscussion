A new, statewide email list has been created on yahoo for Louisiana
Unschoolers! If you live in or around Louisiana and are an unschooler, or are
interested in learning more about unschooling, please check out our list.
There are already lots of links for book recommendations, essays and
articles, resources and much more...not to mention some very cute photos of Louisiana
Unschooler kids : ) After joining, please feel free to add your own favorite
links and photos.
Our newest member, Valerie Fitzenreiter from Lake Charles, authored the book,
The Unprocessed Child. Valerie radically unschooled her daughter, Laurie,
from birth (Laurie is presently up in Baton Rouge getting her Ph.D.). They will
both be coming to New Orleans to speak to Louisiana Unschoolers in December
at a mini-conference we are in the process of organizing!

The list is for unschoolers in the state of Louisiana and interested
Mississippians to make connections and find support with like-minded people; plan and
attend field trips around the state and share resources. Come join us!

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LAUnschoolers/
