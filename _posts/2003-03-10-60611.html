One caution is that you don't want to tell him that he has to learn
about the history of animals or the geography of animals, or do
reports to show you what he has learned. Don't make unit studies up
or anything. Just give him fun stuff to do that he will enjoy, let
him follow his passions, and know in your heart that he will learn
what he needs, when he needs. it. :)
