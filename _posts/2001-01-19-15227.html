hi tami,

i'm 15 now, but when i was younger i used to read "american girl" and
"girl's life." though i wouldn't call them intellectually stimulating,
they're not as beauty and fashion-oriented.

i'm currently addicted to BUST, which is an indie mag for girls -- though
it's geared for a liberal 17-and-up audience. it's very politically-focused,
feminist, and has a lot of underground media, so i'm not sure if that's what
you're looking for. it can be raunchy, but it's outrageously witty and
intelligent. you can check it out at www.bust.com.

hope that helps you out!
- katy
