Hey y'all,

Have you seen the new homeschooling magazine, Life Learning? It's edited by
perhaps the best homeschool editor in the land, and she's from Canada --
Wendy Priesnitz. The regulars who will be writing for it include John Gatto.

go to lifelearningmagazine.com

The Jul/Aug issue has a passage I like particularly:
Quote: When people can interact with art on their own terms, they learn to
trust -- and enjoy -- their own perceptions of art. They also learn to trust
and depend on themselves for their own education. endquote.

Ned Vare
