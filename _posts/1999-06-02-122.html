I have a very sick 11 year old upstairs right now and I got to thinking
about something...

This same child missed 20 days of school the first half of this year (our
attempt to get away from the rigidity of homeschooling that we didn't need
anyway!) because she was sick so often.

This is the first time she's been sick since Feb. when we started
homeschooling again.

Since she has less exposure to all those germs, she's been much healthier!

She's also not getting punched in the face anymore either.

I think reasons of health are some of the best reasons that our children are
better off at home!!

Kim from Pa
