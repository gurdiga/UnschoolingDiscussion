Well when I wrote in to ask about the whole chicken pox thing, I hadn't mentioned anything to my husband yet. So when I mentioned I was thinking of exposing the kids, he asked if I was crazy!! LOL.

So we had a discussion about it and when I said it might be better for them to get it now, he asked how I was sure they would ever get it at all. Well I couldn't be really.

So he called the 3 younger children in and explained what we were talking about and asked them what they wanted to do. None of them wanted it now. And when I mentioned it might be worse if they got it later, they all responded the same way my husband had. How did I know they would get it at all? After all, I never had it.

So it's settled. Life goes on and we see what happens.

Thanks for all the great thought provoking posts.
