Elizabeth Roberts

1 Aug 2004, at 12:42pm

I thought I had made a posting about this last night,
but it seems that for some reason it didn't go
through. I wanted to share what happened.

The kids didn't go with me. Megan and Gracie were
napping; Sarah and Logan decided not to go at the last
minute. I missed them, and wish they had come. They'd
have enjoyed it.

After I did my part, I left the group to go to the
potty. I was stopped by a gentleman who asked for my
name to run with my picture in the paper. He asked if
I was the CG spouse who'd been mentioned, and I said I
was. It turns out that the gentleman is the Public
Affairs Officer for Activities NY. We began talking,
and next thing I knew he gave me the opportunity to
write about the event for the newspaper! He also
mentioned having other events he's supposed to cover
but he's so busy that it's difficult, and if I'd like
to, I can attend and write about them as well!

Elizabeth
