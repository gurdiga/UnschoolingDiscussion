Here is a recipe or flubber:
In one bowl mix - 3/4 Cup warm water
1 cup glue
any food coloring you want

In another bowl mix - 2 teaspoons borax
1/2 Cup warm water

Then pour bowl number 1 into number 2. Then just reach in and pick up
flubber.
Then try playing with this on a slightly wet counter! Whoeeeeee...it
goes like crazy!
