I just wanted to thank you for giving me some food for thought on country

Thought I'd add my two cents on the food allergies. My3yo has eczema, red
cheeks and behavior changes caused by allergies to milk and wheat! Then my
1yo projectile vomits (as in 8 times in one hour!) from soy and eggs!!! Do
you realize how much stuff out there has these products? UGH!!! So we have
become VERY organic lately! The one thing I have learned is that I can
trace 3 of the 4 allergies back to my diet when I was pregnant with them!!!
I have also stumbled upon this concept in a few books! So, if any of you
are dealing with food allergies and considering having more children, watch
your diet when you're pregnant. The biggest thing I learned was to not eat
the same foods all the time and not eating only craved foods! You ought to
see me watching for the right child to grab the soy milk and the other to
grab the cow's milk in our house! No vacations or visits to other houses
for dinner for awhile! We're just getting the hang of this.
