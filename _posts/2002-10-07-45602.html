All I can say about Gatorade is what I know from experience. We have had
every imaginable color in this house of it. My Sierra loves to try them all
and see which ones she likes better. I drink it and prefer the blue and
light green (not the lime one) and also the fierce purple. Alyssa, my
two-year old likes it too. Needless to say, anything she drinks or eats
eventually gets spilled!! I have had it on her skin and clothes. Blue,
fierce grape and fierce berry. I've never had it stain her skin or clothes
to where I couldn't get it out. Meaning couldn't get it out of her clothes,
it stained when it happened but washed out.
