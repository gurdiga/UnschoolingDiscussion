You have four more days to register for the Live and Learn Unschooling
Conference to get the Early Bird discount! The deadline is June 1. You can pay
online through Paypal on the website, or register either by mail or online and send
a check. Go to LiveandLearnconference.org for more information.

The conference takes place at the Peabody Marriott in Massachusetts, just
north of Boston August 27-29. Speakers include keynote Anne Ohman, author Barbara
Sher (Wishcraft and other books), Home Education magazine columnist Sandra
Dodd, author Valerie Fitzenreiter (The Unprocessed Child), and others.

Funshops, open to all ages from child to adult, include Who are the Witches?,
Making fairies, Wire Jewelry, Acting and Improvisation, Origami, and American
Sign Language.

There'll be a storyteller, a concert, a theater presentation, and a Talent
Show open to all participants. We'll also have a screening of a feature length
movie made by homeschooled teens.

But the most important reason to come is Community! So far, we have over 200
people registered, from 29 states and as far away as California and Washington
state and British Columbia. We're going to have a blast!

Please forward this to anyone you think might be interested!

Kathryn Baptista, Conference Coordinator

Come to the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference August 27-29 in Peabody, MA!
For more information, go to www.LiveandLearnConference.org
