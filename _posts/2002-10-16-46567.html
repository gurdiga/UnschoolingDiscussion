Hi my name is Heather and I want to tell everyone about a BRAND NEW,
AWARD WINNING, educational series available to the
homeschool/unschool market called "Under The Waves".

"Under The Waves" is a marine educational series that is currently
being produced in the Cayman Islands by Executive Producer & Olympic
Gold Medal swimmer, Mike Barrowman. Mike has combined his love of
swimming, and the ocean, to teach children about all the neat stuff
that lives "Under The Waves". Each fun, zany, half hour is packed
with great underwater footage, lots of interesting sea creatures, and
a review session that keeps children wanting to learn and see more.

"Under The Waves" was awarded Best Educational Series at the
Yellowstone Film Festival held last month. In addition it has also
received a prestigious Telly Award, rewarding excellence in
children's broadcasting, as well as endorsements from Kids First and
The Dove Foundation for quality children's and family programming.

For more information take a look at www.UnderTheWaves.TV
