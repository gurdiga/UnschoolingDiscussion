anything he loved in the fridge, so I drove to Wendy's, three miles away , right

Then I noticed I was almost home. So was the food, still on the seat next to

it was, that I had fulfilled my urge to go there.

So I turned around, drove BACK a mile and some, and gave him his lunch.
