NHEN's New Homeschooler Encouragement Newsletter (N-H-E-N) is looking
for homeschoolers who are willing to share some advice on the recurring
question:

"What do you do when you have a relatives (or maybe even a spouse) who
aren't happy about the decision to homeschool?"

With each issue, N-H-E-N  helps new homeschoolers connect with parents
from all over the country who have experienced some of the
same obstacles. This is an easy way to reach out and help someone who's
having a rough time. Simply send your responses to N-H-E-N@... as
soon as possible.
 
And, if you know of homeschool websites with articles that address this
issue, please send us that link too.
 
Thanks!
Sue Patterson
N-H-E-N  Editor
