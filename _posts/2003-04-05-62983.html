Sunshyne Powell

5 Apr 2003, at 11:02am

Hello all! My name is Sunny, and I was schooled as a child. I promise,
I'm getting better...lol My husband and I are looking into
homeschooling. Well, a combination of home- and un- schooling to be
more accurate. Our little one, Orin, learns better when he moves at his
own pace. He would probably benefit greatly from unschooling, but I
need slightly more structure, at least until I've finished deprogramming
myself from the years of schooling I went through. So, for now we're
doing a mix of the two - letting him choose what he wants to learn at
the moment, and me helping him learn with worksheets, field trips,
games, etc. At least I know by now that anything can be a learning
experience, if you approach it the right way.

For example, little 4 year old Orin is, at this moment, learning the
basics of architecture, art, and classical music. Okay, in reality he
is building a house out of a cardboard box and packing paper. He's
already gathered crayons to decorate it when he's done, and Fantasia is
playing on the TV not 3 feet from him. ;-)

We are just starting out on the home/un schooling path, although it
feels like we've been doing this all along anyway. We are in NW
Florida, and thankfully have already met one other unschooling family
(and hope to meet more soon).

I guess that's the basics for now. I'm sure you all will learn more
about us as the years go by...lol

Sunny
