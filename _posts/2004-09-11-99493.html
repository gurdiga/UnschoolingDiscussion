Where do you store art supplies (or science kits or
whatever) so they are easy to reach (but not for a
toddler) and in plain view without being messy?
===
We put 2 five shelf bookcases (from home depot) into a double door closet in
the 'computer room'. Games and future craft kits, etc. are stored on the
higher shelves (with a step ladder folded up next to it) and all the art supplies
you could think of, and lots of paper, are stored on the lower shelves. If
you had a toddler you could just shut the closet doors to keep them out of it?
