I have two signed John Holt books. They weren't inscribed to me, I found
them at book sales but I have so liked to look at his little signature
and imagine that book in his hands. That's book AND author worship I
admit to.<g>

One is "Teach Your Own" and inside he wrote "to Priscilla good luck John
Holt".
The other is Escape From Childhood and he wrote "to John Chinn thanks &
good luck peace John Holt May 3, 1974"
No punctuation except the little comma after May 3. He didn't string it
together like that he wrote each separate thought on it's own line:

to John Chinn
thanks & good luck
peace
John Holt
May 3, 1974

Those books have good ju ju for me. <g>
