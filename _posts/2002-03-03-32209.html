Well, my son and I slid right off the road today and went down a steep
embankment. Of course, we landed in a nice deep swamp of mud and it froze
around us while waiting for the tow truck...neither of us is hurt, just
shook up as the truck almost went upside down on us! Yes, we were both
wearing our seat belts, something I do not always do but did today!

I am sore and just thankful that is all that is wrong. The truck running
board on my son's side will need work along with the mirror on his side but
other than that, there is not much damage! You should see the hole we made
in the muck. Took out some small trees too.

Mathieu is still talking about it and will for some time yet.
