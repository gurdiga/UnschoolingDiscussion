I think at least one time with our children we've all delayed something with
the best of intentions and haven't followed through. It's when we do it more
and more without realizing what it does to the chld that it becomes a
problem. I'm not sure if it has to do with how we were raised or what. I
know my husband had a habit of promising or saying later tot he kids with
all the best intentions, but something would come up to make it not so. I
pointed it out to him and even still have to remind him sometimes. He wasn't
even realizing what was going on. He would also have a habit of saying if
you do this than this will happen and not follow through. The kids got wise
to that quick and he learned his own lesson on that one!!!

He had a crappy childhood with no one really caring so maybe that had
something to do with it. Now he's so aware of just what words can do.
