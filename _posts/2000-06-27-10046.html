Due to camaraderie and interest expressed by several people on the
Unschooling.com list I have decided to form an e-loop letter (which
could become an e-list) list for hs parents who are challenged
themselves by diseases such as MS, Lupus, Fybromyalgia and any other
form of physically limiting disease or disability (wheel chair bound,
dealing with cancer, kidney disease etc).
The reason for this being that only someone in an identical or similar
situation will have any idea of the trial and tribulations that become
part of our daily lives and affect our hs'ing life as well.

This loop will be a safe place to vent, moan, seek comfort and express
our joys, fears and the triumphs we make over our various obstacles and
stumbling blocks.

This loop will not be moderated but anything hateful or mean spirited
will be not be tolerated.

Nothing will be off topic as everything colours our lives and influences
how we homeschool/unschool.

Please pass this on to anyone in the homeschooling/unschooling community
you think may benefit from such a venue and have them e-mail me
personally if they wish to be included.

I hope to have it up and running by the middle of July.

Debbie Klement (Buzz)
Hamilton, Ontario
