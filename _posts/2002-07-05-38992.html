HSC, the California-wide homeschooling group that I belong to has a
website with a lot of wonderful selected educational links.

These should be pretty easy to find from the main page:

http://www.hsc.org

I remember that one of the links is to the Exploratorium, a fabulous
science museum in San Francisco. From there, there are many more great
science links.

It's probably easier to find stuff on the web if you use Google to
search for specific terms, like "Trigonometry" or even more specific
terms like "arctangent".
