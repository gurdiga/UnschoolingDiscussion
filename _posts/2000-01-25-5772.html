I must have deleted John's post about higher education, but want to
respond. I have a friend whose father insisted they learn a trade first,
so she went to beauty school. Later, she earned a degree in psychology.
Now she is staying at home with her three year old daughter and cutting
hair, doing perms, etc. in her home while her daughter is there with her.
I have her cut my hair. It is so much easier to get a haircut when I
can bring my kids along and not have to worry about them being in the
way. Plus, she gets to keep all the money - doesn't have to share it
with a shop.
So here is my fantasy: I'm an expert in auto mechanics and I do tune-ups
& oil changes in people's own garage. They know that my children will be
with me. Or if they prefer, they can come to my house and their kids can
play with mine while I do the job.
