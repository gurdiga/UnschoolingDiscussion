Okay, I have something for you to tell them:

My whole life, I have hated Pecan Pie. Couldn't stand the stuff. But, I
believe that it is good to retry foods periodically to remember WHY you
don't like them. So today, I tried Pecan Pie again. Great Stuff! I
love it now. Over the years, my tastes have changed a lot. I now eat
loads of things I didn't like when I was younger. So, my message to all
the young ones out there: it's okay not to like certain foods. But try
them every once in a while anyway, just to make sure!
