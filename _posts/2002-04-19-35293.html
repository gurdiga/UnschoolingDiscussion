That was my dream as a kid. I wanted to live in the library. We had an
old, old library in our town; heaven knows who built it and when, but it had
5 floors, but it was split level on EVERY level. So this little old place
had 9 floors of books, all about 5 or 6 steps apart. As a teenager I used
to walk to the library, grab a stack of books and go hid behind one of the
pillars between the stacks on the top floor and listen to the rain on the
roof above me while I read. Such a great place.

They built a nice new library a couple years ago. My Mom took us to visit
it the last time we were there. It was so sterile and proper library-ish.
I hated it. Old dreams die hard.

brenda
