Hi Ann,
I am currently living in VA, just over the NC border but am a native of
Charlotte. I grew up there & lived just north of there in Mooresville for
10 yrs before moving to VA. We are trying to get back, my husband is
job-hunting now.

Although I have not actually homeschooled in NC I know that the laws are
easy. You must keep attendance records and test each year, but you keep
those in a file rather than turn them in. Also, you only need a high school
diploma and you only file once.

I've also sent out "feelers" for unschoolers since we hope to be moving
SOON. There is a group in Gastonia which is about 20 minutes from
Charlotte. There is a group in Winston Salem & one in Greensboro. There
may be more, maybe in the Raleigh area.

Charlotte is a great place, still has a small town feeling although it's
growing daily! There is so much we miss now that we are in a very rural
area. Luckily my family still live there so we get back often.

Let me know if you need more info....you can write off-list if you like & I
will help all I can. Good luck with your move.
Cindy
