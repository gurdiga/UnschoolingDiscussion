There is an unschool in Seattle, WA and in Portland, OR (of which
I am a staff member). Portland is right on the Washington /
Oregon border.

Seattle, WA:
The Clearwater School
http://www.clearwater.org/

Portland, OR
Cascade Valley School
http://www.casval.org

Both of these schools are modelled after Sudbury Valley School in
Framingham, MA.
http://www.sudval.org/

These schools follow the writings of Daniel Greenberg, John Holt,
Grace Llewellyn, and John Taylor Gatto.
