If you have to keep records, I think it is nice to keep track of stuff in
more "real" terms - rather than schoolish terms. If you keep your records
this way, you don't have to be thinking all the time in terms of school
subjects, you just have to go to your records once a month and translate a
few things into the subjects you need.

I'm going to upload a file for recordkeeping into the files area of this
list - or ask Joyce to do it if it doesn't let nonmoderators. It is called
"Recordkeeping for Unschoolers".
