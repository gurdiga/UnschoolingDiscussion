Hi my name is Jay and I have a feeling that I have been on this group
before but would like to introduce my self. We that is Martin and
the boys Axel 8 and Craig 6 are lving in Brittany at the moment but
are moving possible back to uk. We found the system here no good for
our unschooling children. We have a dream

OUR DREAM.


We are living in a village that has a large building with a social
centre including a shop, an alternative healing centre and café.
There are therapy rooms, a dance/theatre workshop, and a library. Up
stairs is a large room, which has lectures, demonstrations and indoor
fairs. At times this room is section off for small gatherings. There
are community cars. Part of the land is used for growing food; there
is reed bed water filtration, a communal garden and managed
woodland. All aspects of the land management are organic. The
majority of children are home educated and part of the Social centre
is used as a learning centre for both adults and children. Learning
is considered an ongoing process and anything from learning to read
to astrophysics is being studied at any one time.


Courses are held by people who have a passion for their subject and
want to share it with others. These courses are open to anyone; some
get the courses for free. The subjects range from land management to
cooking on a budge, from sculpture to decorating & alternative
therapies. These courses are advertise internationally and a deal is
set up with local people to have course participants staying with
them for a fee. There is a respite facility for families or
individuals to come and stay and try some of the courses and sample
some alternative therapies.


Martin has a thriving massage practice using his hands on healing. I
am spending time with my children in a childfrendly environment and I
run workshops on NVC,TCS,.

Everyone in the village works either from home or in the centre
unless they have chosen to work outside. No Mother works until she
is ready too and the community supports one parent families.




This is what we are working towards

Jay
