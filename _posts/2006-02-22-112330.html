Hi All,

I am Laura. I am an unschooler. I have been married 11 years as of
this last saturday! We live in Oklahoma City. I am originally from
Oklahoma, but just got home in December from a two year move in the
Houston, TX area. I have been unschooling since the beginning,
although I didn't realize it until about 4 years ago. I have 3 kids.
I have a boy 8, girl 5, and a baby girl 1. I am so glad to be apart
of this group. I have been posting my intro to several groups as I
have been on *no mail* for quite some time. I have loved reading the
posts recently. I especially liked the ones about what unschooling
looks like this minute.

A friend of mine and I just started a new yahoo group tonight for
christian unschoolers in the OKC area. If any of you are here in
Oklahoma, please come and join us too on a local level! We are
wanting to get together with people that are in our area as well as
online!

I look forward to being a part of this group!
