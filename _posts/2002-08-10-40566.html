I bought a set of phonics flash-cards at Wal-Mart when my son was at that
point. It included 2 cards that reviewed the phonics rules for Mom (who
needed the refresher course), had pretty pictures on them, and were niftily
color-coded by vowels, blends, etc. The song and dance routine you have to
provide yourself, of course, but Dr. Seuss books and such can help. Total
cost: $3.50, which is good, because I didn't feel bad when I gave them away
6 months later.

You daughter might have just as much fun, if not more, with something you
dreamed up out of your own imagination!
