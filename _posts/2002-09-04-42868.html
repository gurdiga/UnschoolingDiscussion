We LOVE 4-H. This year my kids are showing goats, raising rabbits, training
dogs, gardening, doing karate, and sewing all as 4-H projects. They love
it. I love it because it is fairly non-coercive. You have to do the
paperwork to get a pin at the end of the year...but if you don't want a pin,
you most certainly don't HAVE to do much of anything. Most of our projects
have kids of all ages involved.
