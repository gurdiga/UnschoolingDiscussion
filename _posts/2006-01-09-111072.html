I had posted on AU about game ideas with little ones. The replies were very helpful, including the suggestions of games.

We have had a set of Yahtzee dice floating around, having lost the game pad and shaker cup. I found them recently and had them put up on the mantlepiece for "later."

Logan found them and we just sat down to play with them for awhile, each of us with two dice, rolling and counting the tops. Of course, it wasn't long before the girls came to see what we were doing, and grabbing the dice away from us trying to play.

I started to get annoyed and irritated, but I thought, no..let them play too. So I let them roll and I counted for them. Then it hit me..I should buy alot of dice of varying types so that it's not even close to being a problem. I know there are some very neat dice out there for gaming, and I think we could have alot of fun all six of us throwing dice around. So I've put it on my list to stop by the local game store next payday when we do our errands.

Just thought I'd share...

Beth, NC
