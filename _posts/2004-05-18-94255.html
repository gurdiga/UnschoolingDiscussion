Special Announcement:


LIVE Interview with Eragon Author

This Wednesday, May 19th!



For the first time ever, Homeschool.com will be doing a LIVE interview. This
Wednesday, May 19th, Homeschool.com will be interviewing Christopher Paolini,
author of Eragon. Although he is only in his early twenties, his book is #1
on the New York Times bestseller list, he has made guest appearances on both
the David Letterman and Jay Leno shows, and he has sold the movie rights to his
book. Christopher attributes his success to the fact that he was
homeschooled. You can read more about Christopher in Homeschool.com's April newsletter at
Homeschool.com.


Christopher is an inspiration to homeschooled teens everywhere and we invite
both you and your older children to listen in as we interview Christopher and
find out:

a.. What type of homeschooling did Christopher do? Unschooling?
School-at-Home?

b.. Did he follow a particular curriculum?

c.. Is his household TV-free?

d.. How did his parents encourage him? What can we learn from that?

e.. What advice does he have for other aspiring writers?

f.. Can he give us a sneak preview on what will happen in Part Two?


Want to listen in on the interview? Maybe even ask your own question?

Here's how it works:


1) This Wednesday, May 19th

12:00 (Pacific time)

1:00 (Mountain Time)

2:00 (Central Time)

3:00 (Eastern Time)

2) Dial (507) 726-3270 and then enter the passcode: 55095#

3) You will automatically be patched into a giant conference call (Lots of
energy and very exciting).


If you happen to miss the live interview, you can still listen to the
recorded interview at: http://www.homeschool.com/articles/Camp2004


P.S. If you have not yet read Eragon, it is available from Amazon.com for
$13.26.


Thank you so much for letting your members know about this exciting event. We
sure appreciate working with you and I hope that you will let me know if we
can ever return the favor.


Thanks!

Katie Whiting
