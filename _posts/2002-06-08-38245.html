Our favorite Math games:
Quiddler (this is a word building card game but each letter is worth certain points and you have to add and subtract depending on what you use and don't use)

S'Math (it's a math equation game set up like Scrabble)

Dominoes (I had NO idea how many math games this takes care of!)

Mille Bournes (It's a card game where you accumulate miles to win a "race" and has "bad" cards like run out of gas, pop a tire..etc)

Yahtzee!!

Check out these books: Family Math and Math for Fun Projects
