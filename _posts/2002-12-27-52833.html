I got a digital camera for Christmas; dh found one that was
refurbished. It was about $100 and has all the features I wanted
(compact, picture display, zoom lens, crisp pictures, no delay
between pushing the button and the picture being taken, etc.).
It's a Kodak DX3215; he got it from tigerdirect.com. His stragegy
was to research cameras with desired features, then did a google
search to see who carried them refurbished, thus cutting the price to
1/2 to 1/3 of new.

HTH,
Anne.
