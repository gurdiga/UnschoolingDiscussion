I just love Port Townsend. We do our grocery shopping
there on the weekends at the Co-Op, but I love to shop
at the little stores downtown as well. Rising
Phoenix, Sand Castle, and Wild Sage are some of my
favorites. We love all the wonderful restaurants too.
You won't find a Denny's there, but you will find
quirky, original little restaurants with lots of
personality and local color, and a ton of fresh
seafood. We often take the kids to Chetzamoka park
after we do our shopping, or to play at one of the
many little beaches off Water Street.

Nanci K.
