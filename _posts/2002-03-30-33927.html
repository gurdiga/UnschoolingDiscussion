There are Six different combat style's within the AoN.

1.) Spar's...A spar is to 10. Two individuals fight. All forms of
fighting apply in a spar, all special weapons can be used. No one dies, no
rezz is lost in a Spar to 10.
New players and old players spar to see who is superior, and also to advance
to higher ranks.

DM. All forms of special weapons can be used. Individuals die, and rezz is
lost. DM are done to see
which player is superior over others. They can be fought for many reasons. If
one player in a room wishes to begin a DM on another. He calls forth {{ AoN
DMstrike then the persons name}} then makes his/her first hit. If one is
using a +weapon enhancer the first strike will not be with the +weapon
enhancer. After the first round, then the two individuals can activate there
+weapon enhancers if they use one. By simply stating ( activating +1 through
+5 ) of there own official +weapon enhancer which is listed in there profile.
Then and only then after stating the enhancer can it be used for the rest of
the fight. If a player is losing badly he/she may call for assisstance from
others, but only is help is asked for. Then another player can jump into the
DM to help out a friend with the fight.
Then there will be 2 individuals attacking 1 individual. The individual
lending aid to a comrade can use the +weapon enhancer on there enterence into
the DM. But must state within there description (Activating +weapon) , if not
then they can only use there Base dice, until the next round. Even though the

fight will still only go to 13, as the original fight began as a DM not an
MPC. If further aid is requested by another player in the DM, now MPC.

Special Attacks:

1.) (KO's) or Knock-outs...during any of the six combat forms of
fighting within AoN, one can use (KO's). This is simply done when describing
your defense and attack at the
end of said description you place a (KO1). Then you roll your dice. If
doubled come up, any type of doubles even a 1 1, you have knocked out your
opponent. And they will be out-cold for 1 round. Meaning you roll again, and
your oppenent takes more damage because they have been knocked-out. If
doubles are not rolled the fight continues as normal. One can use 3 (KO's) in
a match. make sure you keep track of the number you have used.(you must put a
(KO1, KO2, or KO3) within your description or the doubles will not be counted
as a knockout. no exceptions.

2.) *TA* or Total Attack...*TA* can be used once in any type of match.
Since it is a total attack there is no defense in your roll. Example: 34 23.
normally the 34 would be
your DR, and the 23 would be your AR. Except by using a *TA* you simply add
the first #'s of each roll togeather or (3+2=5 or a 5 point hit.) If your
roll is higher the same thing applies. It can be a deadly special attack..if
used at the right time.And it must be mentioned in your discription before
your roll as *TA* for it to work.

3.) *TD* or Total defense...can be used once within any match. Ruffly
the same as a *TA*, but the reverse of it. As well it must be stated within
your description *TD* for
it to work before you roll. Example: You roll a 45 18.
Now usually your DR would be 45 and your AR would be 18. Except you
already stated it to be a *TD*. so its a total defensive move. You simply add
the first #'s of each roll togeather or (4+1=5 or a 5 point defense roll.) If
you roll higher your defense will be greater. *TD* can be used to block a
*US* if it has not be used yet in a match. And only if the *TD* is higher
than the *US* used. Its also used against a *TA* or total attack.

4.) *US* or Ultimate Sacrifice...something to use if your about to
die, and you have used all of your other special attacks and defenses.... So,
you discribe your last bit
of energy list *US*, and roll. Except since its a total last attack there is
no defense to your *US*.
