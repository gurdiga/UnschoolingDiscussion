Well, after one whole week of no phone service (an old bill did not get
paid, but it was not indicated on my statement that service would be
disconnected), I received a lot of unschooling emails. What interesting
subjects. I took some notes because I really do enjoy reviewing other
people's perspectives. This club has helped me to grow as a person and
as a mother, since it really has caused me to stop and think about my
viewpoints, and to question my choices more.
Karin, your comments on how unschooling has changed the way you parent
and relate to your children really impressed me. I have found my own
life changing in a similar way since unschooling. I am learning to be
respectful of myself and others without being either controlling or
passive.
I noticed your opinions caused a lot of sparks, but very interesting
ones.
Bonni
