Hey Barb,

When we moved last year, I called a local box making company and found that
they have tons of "defective boxes" that they don't ship to their vendors. I
was willing to pay for them, but was told I could just come pick them up. So I
did. There was zero wrong with the boxes themselves. The defects were generally
labeling errors, like the company name or printing being sideways as opposed
to up or down. We also called our local grocery stores, asked for the store
manager and he notified the night crew responsible for stocking to not cut up
new boxes for a couple nights boxes. I went in and picked them up, they had
collapsed them so I could put more in the car, but they didn't cut any up. Maybe
these could be options for you????

Good Luck,
