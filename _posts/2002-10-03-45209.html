if we didn't have Avery. He stoped me and told me that I forgot about
Avery and I asked him if he wanted me to include Avery and he said
yes, because Avery is his favorite brother and he loves him soooooo
much and he would be sad if we didn't have Avery anymore. Then I
asked him why he likes to hurt Avery so much and he said that he
dosen't like to hurt him he's just mad at him, because now that we
have Avery he has to wait for everything, he never gets to be first
anymore and that makes him mad and sad nad he just ends up hurting
Avery because he's there. So we talked about the things that he
misses by not being the only one. We also talked about him letting
me sleep in the mornings, and he told me that the reason he wakes me

vcr by himself. (we don't have cable and we can't get reception so

and a movie all ready to go and I got to sleep until Avery woke up
