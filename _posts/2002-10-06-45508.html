the staples weren't going all the was through the paper, so tape was a
must.

maybe next time - one is not going to be strong enough.
Ours is still up, we are walking around it and crawling through it. It
has already had many visitors and generated a lot of discussion (esp. by
my dad who was a friend of Bucky Fuller's).
