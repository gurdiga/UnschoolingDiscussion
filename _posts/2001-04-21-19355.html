First of all - if I say something wrong, please don't get offended at
my ignorance. Kindly correct me, please...

That said, I'm curious about different religions, what they believe,
etc. Not to be converted or anything, I'm happy with my current
choice of religion, I'm just curious. So if anyone would please e-
mail me privately (I really don't want to start a religious argument
on this list) about their religion (no matter what) - like basic
beliefs, what you believe about deity, holy books, what you believe
about purpose for earth life, the history of your religion, etc.
Please, only if you really know, and not just what someone
in "authority" told you about another religion (I can get all that by
going through a search engine, which I'd rather avoid). Then, I'd
like to email you with individual questions.

Thank you.
