Holly says:

they all need to be the same but different, like when you're playing with
all of different colors [the card game] they would need to be the same color
but like still all different, like 1 2 3 or if you're playing with all of the
same color [like on the computer] they could be like 1 diamond, 2 squiggles,
and 3 ovals/oblongs

Sandra says:

If two are the same in ANY way they all three need to have that
characteristic. If one is striped, they all have to be. If one is single
they all have to be.

If you have lots of diamonds, for instance, they don't ALL have to be solid,
or hollow, or striped, but either they all have to be the same or they all
have to be different.

No odd man out (no two of one kind and the third different).
