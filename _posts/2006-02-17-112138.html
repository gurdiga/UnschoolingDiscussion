17 Feb 2006, at 10:19am

Maybe not all 100% unschooly, but here's some movies my kids have been
into lately. Seems we are on a fantasy binge.

*Legend....starring a very young Tom Cruise before he went
crazy...very pretty to watch, but dark (a unicorn is slain,
some "hell" type imagery) so not for young children necessarily.
*Secret of Roan Inish Our family favorite-great for St. Pattys' Day.
*Indian in the Cupboard (a few school scenes)
*Neverending Story (all 3, best 1) (again, some in school scenes and
bullies)
*Milo in the Land of Faraway
*Labryinth
*Willow

A couple of these movies have some in school scenes:
NeverEnding story has a lot of bullying so the kid "turns to fantasy
to cope with reality" crap, that part stinks and it's sad nobody helps
this kid. But my son LOVES the warrior boy, Atreyu. Actually, because
of the bullying scenes I wouldn't recommend it at all but the first
movie was just so cool whenever Bastian was in the fantasy scenes.

Labryinth has some "life lessons" about growing up and leaving behind
childhood fantasies. I was not so thrilled about that, but my oldest
daughter picked right up on it and commented how stupid an idea that
was. Overall, for us, the fantasy made up for the schooly aspects,
especially since my kids are all a little older now and acutely aware
when someone is trying to slip in a lesson on how to make their life
less interesting.

Cindi
