5 Dec 2000, at 3:21am
okay y'all. so i had this idea.
tonight i was thinking about how sad it is that the awesome zine Dropout is
defunct (i just found that out last night), and i was thinking about how it
would be cool to pick it up again, but then i realized how that isn't very
feasable since i don't know how to contact anyone about it. SO i was
thinking that instead, it would be really awesome to create an alternative.
a punk-esque zine about being unschooled youth, being in school, dropping
out, activities and resources, et cetera. not -only- for unschoolers, but
for unschoolers, those wanting to drop out, those who /cant/ drop out, but
have experiences they want to get out of their system, etc. So. i need
help. i need submissions out the ying yang (well-thought-out articles and
poems and photographs and essays are preferable but send whatever inspires
you!), quotes and clips from literature, etc., by teens and young adults.
ALSO, if anyone's interested in helping emoi with this project, /do/ let me
know, eh?
email me personally please.
