Elissa, sure go ahead and print it. The fact is I never heard anything
in Christian circles about not spanking at all, until my present church.
So, I don't know. I did read a lot that described specifically when and
how to spank in a controlled, careful way, but none that says no spanking
and not even close for no negative discipline. The fact is, God speaks
to us, so they say, through the Word (the Bible) and through our
conscience (God's Spirit to our spirit) and through our circumstances
(other people, events and experiences). I was in a deficit of
circumstances, in ignorance and blindness of the Word, and in denial of
my conscience. It wasn't until I had the courage to face my fears that
something I believed could be false that I could seek out and accept this
truth, only to find that it perfectly coincides with scripture.
Bonni
