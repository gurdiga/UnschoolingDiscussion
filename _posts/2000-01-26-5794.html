Our support group here in NC, is having an international fair later this
year. In keeping with this theme, we are looking for Pen Pals in other
countries. Right now we are still in the planning stages (or should I
say, I am) Is there anyone in another country that might be interested
in this idea? Maybe even more than just a pen pal. Last year my kids
and I participated in the "flat farm" project. A girl in California
send a paper doll of herself and sent it to us. We created a journal of
her time here, and we sent it back several months later. We took
pictures and wrote things et.... We also made paper dolls of our kids,
and sent them to her. It was quite interesting. We really thought it
was fun to get our journal back. During the time we were working on our
journals, we send postcard to her ect.. I'm thinking this would be a
nice way to correspond during a several month period, and then we will
share these at our international fair.
So........ anyone out there interested?
Ann
