Niagara Mohawk, Massachusetts Electric, Narragansett Electric, Granite State
Electric, and Nantucket Electric.

If those sound like your electric company, you might be able to get a free
VSH tape of a long-out-of-print late-'50s Disney cartoon called Donald in
Mathemagicland. I saw it (a short version, probably) in a theatre when I was a kid.
We've rented it from Hollywood Video, but it's not available for sale or
I'd've bought one. But while I was looking, I found this.

It has a VERY fun section on pool tables and angles and fancy pool shots.



http://www.culverco.com/edservices/catalog_ny/pages_detail/v130_detail.html


Only schools and customers in the following communities are eligible for
these free resources.

Teachers can order classroom quantities. Parents can order single copies of
the various literature titles. These materials are available ONLY to those
customers served by Niagara Mohawk, Massachusetts Electric, Narragansett Electric,
Granite State Electric, and Nantucket Electric.
