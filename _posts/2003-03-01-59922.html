<<My son wants to go to collage and now says he can not go because he will
not get a GED or a diploma.>>


Depends on the college he wants to go to. Many will take homeschoolers
without a diploma. Some want a diploma or GED, some want SAT's and some a
combination of all of them. Some will just look at a portfolio or essay of
accomplishments. If he wants a GED, he can go and get one. You can also make
a diploma for him if that's important.
