Jon and Rue Kream

16 Dec 2000, at 1:10pm

Valerie - Each town in Massachusetts can make their own rules, within certain guidelines.  It can be difficult depending on what town you live in, and what the superintendent in your town thinks of homeschooling.  Last year we lived in a different town and kept a daily journal.  When we moved this year we joined Clonlara. Clonlara encourages unschooling and handles all communication with your local superintendent. (I don't think they'll handle your son's father though :)).    We have to submit a "plan" to the school committee for approval, and then send Clonlara two reports per year.  So far it is working well for us, although we wish we didn't have to report to anyone.  - Rue
