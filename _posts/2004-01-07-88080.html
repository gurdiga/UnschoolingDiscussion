Well, one point, which is pretty easy to grasp, is that learning can
take place in your home or community *even if* there are no worksheets
produced to turn in to the school.

I'm not sure how much the school funding system is different in Canada
from what I'm used to in the U.S., but here the independent study system
is (foolishly?) built around the idea that it's important to have flat
samples to submit to the school for their files.
