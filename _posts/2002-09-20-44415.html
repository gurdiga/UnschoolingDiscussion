David Gutterson's Family Matters: Why Homeschooling Makes Sense
As I was reading this, I would have ahaa moments that I would share with my husband. He seemed to really relate to him, and the author is a teacher. So that's a great perspective. Very realistic, in terms of the masses. I was able to check it out at my library. I don't know if it really is on deschooling but it really was great for my husband.
Kelli T
