hi,

relaxing is really the key. your son's and your bond will do nothing but
deep if you take care of both your needs and his needs and try to not allow
your 'mother' and her deal to distract you from the truly loving
relationship you have with your son. the only needs that are important now
are yours and your sons. stay strong, enjoy your family (even if it's just
the 2 of you) and have as much fun as possible. one of our mottos is
'dessert first'. i've spent way too much of my life 'fixing' all the
things wrong with me now it's time to play:) i would recommend the same to
you:) just enjoy his wild antics as much as possible.

best wish
susan
austin,tx
