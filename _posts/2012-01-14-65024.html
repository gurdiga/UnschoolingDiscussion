If you move from any sort of teaching (and I think 'eclectic' is likely to qualify), you'll need to start from scratch with deschooling.

If you can go a year without using the word learning at all, that would help. Get your husband in on it too. Maybe put a dollar in a jar if either of you says "learn" or "learning," especially about the kids. If you THINK "learning," chuck a quarter in the jar. :-)

Probably by summer you won't be saying "learning" anymore, and you might have enough in the jar to get to the ALL Unschooling Symposium in Albuquerque in late December. :-)
