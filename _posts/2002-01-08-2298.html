No, unfortunatley my son and I learned that awhile ago. A couple of
years back on Easters, my son Christopher asked me to warm up one of
his Easter eggs, just a bit please...Sure hun, here you go one
slightly warmed hard boiled egg. ....I only warmed it a few seconds
in the microwave and that is all it took to exploded when he bit into
it. OUCH! It still makes me sad to think of it. It even gave him a
small blister on his lip. All I could think was, Duh! How could I
have not known? I seen what a microwave does to a bean or a potato
that has not been piecred...Sometimes these little facts just slip
your mind.

Fortunatley he now laughs about the time mom served him that
exploding egg. AAH! thankfully they are forgiving. :-)
