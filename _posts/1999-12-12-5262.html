Thad Martin

19 Dec 1999, at 3:29am
hi,

we moved to austin,tx (davis, ca) when i was around 5 months pregnant. we are
about equi-distance (1800 miles) from both families (n. ca & e. pa) which has
its obvious down sides especially in terms of our son getting to know all his
grand parents, aunts & uncles .... but the up side is exactly what you're
talking about
ALL that advice and you really don't have much of a clue when everyone around
you is interfering with ones instincts. i'm really happy we had it very quiet
only the occasional visit. it's allowed us some time to look into things, and
rather some raw experience so we could do things the way we felt was best. i
guess i find that i want the same for r'. the idea that without a lot of
judgment and pressure to conform, he could 'show' me who he is and not get
stuck in that cycle of pleasing/performing. i want him (and me as well) to
discover that when he is truly happy, when he follows what he believes, within
himself, he will feel contentment and excitement, and those around him will be
happier than if he had done 'what he was told'. that is not to say everything
he does pleases me:) - it certainly is not the case but without a lot of people
telling him or me what is the right way we can work our way through any dilemma
we happen upon.

-susan
