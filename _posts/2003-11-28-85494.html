28 Nov 2003, at 7:51am

I'm not sure every homeschooler in Baltimore City has been made aware
that the homeschool liaison has received notice of layoff (he's
retiring) and his last day will be December 5.

A new focus group, charmcityhomeschoolers@... has been formed
to begin to discuss the issues that will inevitably affect Baltimore
City homeschoolers and strategize on a collective response to same.

We (the groups moderators) would like to invite Baltimore City
homeschooling parents an opportunity to work on a plan of action to
address the school system's next choice of a liaison and the office
out of which reviews and administration takes place.

Everyone is welcome to join the group and all your ideas and insights
would be appreciated.

Theresa Towers

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Charmcityhomeschoolers
