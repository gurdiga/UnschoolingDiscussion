1 Oct 2002, at 1:35pm

I have teens and little ones.
Each stage is such a treasure, and it is often hard to appreciate the treasure when it comes, like all gems, surrouded by all that dirt..and all the work to get it free.

My 8 spent most of the day away...left last night to a friends and spent the night. MADE IT THROUGH. No phone calls, no homesickness. I woke up and realized she is in the next stage of attachment, and I feel a bit sad and proud at the same time.

It is so odd watching them grow, and take their place among the world, and wondering if I did it right, what I did wrong, and how I may never really know...

be well,
Rachel Ann
