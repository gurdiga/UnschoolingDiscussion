HI Libby,

I can relate to your Christian group problem. I have had the same problems
here in Pennsylvania. Even though we are Christian, we are not Christian enough
for most of the closed minded hypocrites we have met.
How most of these people can call themselves Christians is beyond me, when
all they do is judge you and then ignore you because they don't like the way you
look or dress.
I let my kids dress the way they want and wear their hair the way they want
and that just doesn't go over real big out here. Moving here was the biggest
mistake I ever made. I thought that it would be a better environment for my
kids...what a joke! All that they have had to deal with is being judged on their
looks and not by the kind of people they are.

I have never tried to shove the Bible down anyone's throat nor look down on
anyone that doesn't go to church. The only one that has the right to judge
others is Jesus, but I guess most people out here skipped that part of the Bible!

Hang in there, maybe you should try to start a group on your own. That is my
new plan, I have started an unschoolers group, we will see what happens.

Lorrie
