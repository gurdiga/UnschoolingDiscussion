> Dear Friends,
>
> The Butler County Superintendent (Butler County, OHIO) has sent all home
> schoolers a request to update their intent to home school next year.
> PLEASE DO
> NOT USE THEIR FORM! If you belong to a Butler County home school group
> please
> be sure to notify your friends as well. At the end of this message I have
> provided a URL to a Notification Form which meets the state minimum
> requirements for annual notification. As well, I have attached the text
> from
> this form for those on JUNO.
>
> Butler County has requested the following not required by law.
> 1. Name of school system you reside in.
> 2. Home phone number (labeled optional)
> 3. Have asked you to specify the home school parents level of education.
> This is not required by law. By law you only
> have to agree that you meet one of the criteria,
> you do not need to tell them which one you meet!
> 4. Stated in their cover letter that they "need to notify everyone" about
> your
> home school activities.
> I have not called to determine what this means,
> however, State Law does not require or stipulate
> that any other state agencies or educational
> institutions should be notified about your intent
> to home school your child. I plan to call the
> Superintendent tomorrow to determine the
> extent of this comment.
>
> Andrew Bare - At-HomeDad, Home Schooler
