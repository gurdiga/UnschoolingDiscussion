Hi--

I remember hearing about this study but I'd never seen it.

Interesting results (not surprising!:))--yet I remember people on a list I was on saying that the religious-affiliation demographics, among others, were skewed by the fact that Bob Jones University was involved. Lots and lots of home/unschooling people I know said they would have opted out of such a study because they didn't want to be affiliated with that particular institution...

Now if they had a non-religious organization collecting the data, the demographics might be quite different, and a picture of homeschoolers as more varied in their makeup might have emerged...

Thanks for passing it along--
Carolyn
