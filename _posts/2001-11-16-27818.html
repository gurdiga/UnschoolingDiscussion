The HEM-Letters email list was created specifically for discussion of
issues of interest to homeschoolers, and for those discussions to be
considered for publication in Home Education Magazine's Letters &
Discussions section. Posts will be edited for length, spelling,
typographical errors, etc. - but meaning and intent will not be
changed, and all selected posts will be available for review before
actual publication in Home Education Magazine.

Everyone is welcome to join the list. However, if you do not want
what you write to be considered for publication in Home Education
Magazine please do not post to this list. Thanks for understanding!
