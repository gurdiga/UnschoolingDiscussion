Money-At-Home.Com: New Web Site Helps Moms, Home Business
Entrepreneurs, & Others Find Work-at-Home Opportunities & Jobs

August 24, 1999 (INB) -- Money-At-Home.Com <http://www.money-at-
home.com/> was an idea conceived by Andrea Taylor, a mother who
was looking to stay at home and not have to put her child in day
care. She thought it would be great if she could help others who
would also like to work at home, so she started Money-At-Home.Com
on June 24th, 1998. After just one year, her web site has logged
nearly one million total hits and has recorded over 470,000 total
hits in the past 87 days alone. Most of the traffic came with
very little advertising. Money-At-Home.Com is fast becoming one
of the most visited sites for people who are interested in
working at home and making money from home.

"Money-At-Home.Com offers a personal touch and provides some of
the best resources on the Internet for anyone wishing to work
from home," states owner and founder Andrea Taylor. "Money-At-
Home.Com is set on becoming the site to visit for the best and
highest quality work-at-home resources, with guarantees on all
our products, the lowest prices, and a wide array of
opportunities to fit most lifestyles."

Money-At-Home.Com is about more than selling, says Taylor. "We
are also about giving. At Money-At-Home.Com, our visitors can
enjoy free advertising, free contests, and free expert advice for
their home businesses. They can access upcoming trade shows and
events, send free electronic postcards, and utilize our free
reminder service."

Money-At-Home.Com is in tune with the growing trend toward
telecommuting and the increased interest that Americans have in
working from home.

Taylor invites companies that hires home workers to visit Money-
At-Home.Com to post their job openings free of charge or to send
an e-mail to <mailto:AndreaTaylor@...>.
