I agree. I have only been here a short time and I have found it to be
a very good tool while I am just getting started on my unschooling
path. It is a very important goal for me and I have a lot to let go of
to get there. The honesty and simplicity with which my requests have
been answered is causing me to look at my self closely. That's not
easy and I suppose one needs to be ready to do that in order for it to
be helpful. I for one am grateful to be able to bend the ear of other
folks who are living in a style I want for myself.

as for the slug discussion, I have a friend who greeted both my new
babies with 'Well, hello there lump!' I thought it was such a cool
welcome the first time, and very welcome and familiar the second. Made
me think of homemade gravy or something. They are lumps! soft, round,
often wet and milky but warm.
