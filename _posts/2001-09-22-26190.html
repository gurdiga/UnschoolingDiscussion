I don't like the shoes, either, especially since I have what my dh calls

There are many aggravating things about the Flylady....but I've tried it for
about six weeks now. I can only say I'm tremendously grateful to her/this
website for enabling me to find a way to begin managing my home. My home
is more manageable...my children get more of my attention, and more freedom
to be creative; and for the first tie in any of our lives, we can get out
the door for activities wothout the usual grousing, grumbling, and
yelling. Like most things out there...you take what you need and ditch the
rest - I find this program quite adaptable to our eclectic lifestyle!

of six!)
