Hello fellow unschoolers!

Just in case any of you are not aware, iwon.com gives away 10k a day, 1
mil a month and monthly bonus prizes when you use their web page as a
portal to the web.

Its worth it to make www.iwon.com your homepage when signing on. I get my
news from there and use their search engine when I need to add points.

Normally, I've been using Google from the beginning of them because I
think its the best thing since sliced bread *smile*. But for the year
iwon is doing this promo, I use them. March 2001 - 2002

Additionally, you can open up your own iwonstore and get a percentage of
all the purchases.

I opened a superstore and hopefully all my online ordering friends and
relatives will shop there. Stop by to take a look at what you can do for
yourselves while trying to make ends meet. This is NOT an ad to ask you
to make a purchse.. I want you to make your own store!

http://ourchildrensfuture.iwonstores.com

I want to take any money this store makes and put it towards opening a
democratic school (which is in the works anyway).

There is nothing I gain from this announcement, except the inate pleasure
I would get if a fellow unschooler won something!

So good luck to all..
kolleen

Here's the two issues I bring up:

1. Go to www.iwon.com and register yourself for the winnings. Visit there
everyday and lets get an unschooler the booty instead of just anyone!!!
*smile*

2.Go to http://www.iwon.com/home/shopping/store_builder/ and build your
own store in seconds. Its foolproof!

_______

The school my group is putting together is benefactor of the store I put
up. I don't want people here to buy from it. I'll market it my own way.
(Unless you REALLY want to!)

I want people here to open their own store and benefit from it.
Even your own purchases.

www.iwon.com offers this EASY step method of putting one together. They
host it, they deal with ALL the transactions, including the returns. YOU
get a percentage of the sales. YOU get your friends and family to buy
from it.

Also using iwon.com enters you into a sweepstakes that they are currently
giving away 10k a day. (Make sure you use an email address that isn't
your favourite, as you will get spammed from them when you enter).
_______

I do a lot of graphic design from my house to make money. Not everyone
has the clientelle or the time (more than 1 kid) to work from home.

I'm hoping this will help some of us that need to keep bringing income
into the house without giving up unschooling.

Of course, winning the 10k would be great!!!

Good luck everyone,
kolleen
