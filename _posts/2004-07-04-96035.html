I posted this at unschoolingbasics, so forgive me if you're a member
of both lists!:) Just wanted to share a list I've formed, for those
that are interested:

This has to do with unschooling in a non-direct way, but is
definitely not an unschooling list.
I started an email list called "imagination tribe" for the support
of creativity. Initially, I wanted it there for folks that had taken
my creativity workshop here in Pensacola, but realized it would just
be nice to have a safe place on the web in which we can explore our
creative genius selves!:)
Since unschoolers are such creative individuals anyway, I thought
I'd share it with you all, maybe a few of you will feel a need for
that kind of support.
If you're a repressed creative, yearning to break out of your shell,
or a fully creative genius exploring freely, I hope you'll join me
and create a wonderful list.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/imaginationtribe/

And here is the group description:

"Imagination tribe is a group of people, interested in supporting
one another in our creative endeavors. Whether you're a repressed
creative, yearning to express yourself, or a successful
artist/writer/dreamer/entrepreneur, join us as we discuss issues
that pertain to thinking, living and seeing more creatively.
This group has been started as a base of support for those that have
taken the "Awakening the Dragon" creativity workshop in Pensacola,
but is open to all creative souls, seeking other imaginative spirits
for support and idea sharing.
This is a safe place to share your dreams and goals. We ask that all
information and advice will be shared in a spirit of friendship and
true desire to enable one another. New members will be moderated to
ensure a supportive atmosphere. "

I'm the only one there so far...because I haven't done my workshop
here yet. So it looks rather lonely right now.:) After I get my
business up and running and do the workshop a few times, I expect it
to get more lively!
