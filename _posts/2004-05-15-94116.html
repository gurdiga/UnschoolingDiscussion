Why are we talking about this here? Honestly.

There are other places online to discuss schools.
Especially "pre-schools."

People leave this list because of the high volume of e-mail, and I'm
uncomfortable when the volume isn't at least about unschooling.

People come on for a day or two and leave, too, and I would REALLY hate for
their day or two to have been a discussion of which pre-school is better.

For unschoolers, there isn't a good pre-school. If your circumstances demand
that you put your children in ANY kind of institution, then it might be
better to go to the pre-school-comparison discussions that I'm sure are happening
all over yahoogroups and the web.
