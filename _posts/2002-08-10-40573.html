NOT easy reading (Asterix). I've really enjoyed them as an adult, but
beginning readers won't be able to use them. Most of the jokes require a
fair amount of prior knowledge to be funny, the print is teensy, and the
words are difficult.

They're originally French, and translated into British English. For more
advanced readers they're wonderful. For medieval history buffs they're GREAT!

For years they were hard to come by in the U.S. but still easy in England.
They're easy again here, for a while probably. It seems to go in waves.
There are some newer ones, too. (Meaning in the past ten years <g>.)
