24 Feb 2004, at 9:26am

Sarah had just sat down to watch Spirited Away again. I just realized that there is a "luck dragon" in the movie...Sarah noticed it too, and said "Hey Mom, what's that other movie, with the boy in it, that's got one of those?" And I said "The Never Ending Story" and remembered that the luck dragon's name was Falcor.

So now she's wanting to look up luck dragons to find out more about the symbology (is that the right word?!) involved, and anything else she can find about it...after she's done watching Spirited Away. She wants us to rent Never Ending Story tonight when we return Spirited Away.

But we talked for a little while about the two movies, what we liked about them, what we hadn't...and she says "I think it's neat that we both like the Luck Dragons!"

Gosh..that was just a nice moment for us!!!
