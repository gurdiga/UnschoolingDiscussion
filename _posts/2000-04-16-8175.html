Hi Lisa,
It could be that he feels that because his brother asks so many questions
that he doesn,t have the opportunity to ask his or that he doesn't need to
ask his. You hear about this kind of thing with babies, when they talk late
because the older siblings do all the talking for them. Perhaps you could
set aside a time daily or weekly and ask him if he has any questions. You
could also have him keep a journal and questions might pop up in his daily
reflections. Of course if this isn't the reason, then these things may or
may not make a differnce. Just a suggestion.
Amy
