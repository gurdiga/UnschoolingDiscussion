I went through the reasoning with Emma on why a parent may not want their child to have a nose piercing. Infection - unlikely under sanitary conditions with proper after care. Appearance - but that's why they want it done! If they decide that it's really not their thing it's a simple matter of removing the stud or sleeper and letting it grow over. Cost - wasn't really an issue, if we couldn't afford it then she could have saved her pocket money. I can't think of any other reasons and Emma couldn't think of any others either. Peer Group reaction wasn't an issue.

In the 'old days' the only reason for not getting it done would have been because I, the parent, said NO. I like these 'new days' much better <g>.
