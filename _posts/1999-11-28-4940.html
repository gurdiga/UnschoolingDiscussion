In the text of the letter by Peggy Griffin I noticed this:

<<My sons both participate in theatre class with 2 productions each year (all
of the cast members are also home educated).>>

Very interesting to me, since we began a homeschool community theater group
about five years ago. We also do two productions a year and all our cast
membrs are homeschooled. In fact, we're hard at work rehearsing/preparing
for our next show which opens (gulp) THIS week.

Are there other hs theater groups out there that anyone knows of (I
correspond with a great woman in Wash. D.C. who has had a group for a long
time)? It would be fun to start a loop or at least exchange some
information....

Susan--Theatrix, Homeschool Community Theater
