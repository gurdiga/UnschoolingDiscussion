I may be mistaken, but I don't think it is a bill--I think it is officially
law. I know it was passed by both House and Senate and that Bush was
definitely going to sign it. I imagine he has by now.

Whether homeschoolers need to be tested is dependent on individual state
laws. I don't know the law for Florida; it would be best to ask someone
with one of the larger homeschooling organizations there--they will know.

It does not affect Oklahoma homeschoolers at all, thankfully.
