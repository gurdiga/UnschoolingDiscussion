Hello,

My name is Laura and our family lives in Maine. Our children are 11b, 3 g
and 11 mob.
Our oldest likes to surf, archery, snowmobile, xBox etc.
We have been schooling/Unschooling for almost 3 years. The first two years
had breaks in them to explore a different Private school and public for 3
weeks.
I have always known the Unschooling was the way he learned best. I have piles
of text books but we rarely use them.

I was wondering if maybe some could assist us with suggestions for our
portfolio? This is my first portfolio year as the past 2 we have tests
already. Our group had a teacher that was pretty relaxed as far as papers and
proof but she is unable to do them this year so there is another teacher
doing them. I have been told she just wants to see "progress." If I wasn't
so panicked over this I could probably have a huge list but I am totally
drawing a blank. I have some items to put down but I am certain there are
life experiences I am forgetting. I have searched the net off and on since
we started this but most of what I see is like Drama, Piano, Mad Science etc.
We don't do too much outside our home as far as classes.

Thank you in advance,

Laura
Maine
