I think it sounds great!

Another one that I get asked is, "Doesn't homeschooling take a lot of your
time?"

My answer is that it takes none of my time and all of my time. I explain
that I don't do school, so I don't spend any time *teaching*, but that I'm
always on duty to explain things and seek out things my son might be
interested in.
