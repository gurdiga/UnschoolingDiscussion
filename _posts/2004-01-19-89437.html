I don't believe in Satan, I don't believe in hell. I've seen the kind of things your "first hand experience" get people all excited about, and all I see is natural (and sometimes unhealthy) human behavior causing all the problems.
People need to blame evil entities in order to explain things, but that doesn't have anything to do with my point...which was about real witches.
I know real witches, I know practicing wicca members.
Not ONE of them seems in any way worshipful of anything remotely evil. Actually, they are very peaceable, earthy folks, interested in environmentalism and connections to the earth.
Let's just assume Satan is real for a moment.
The Bible says he will be seen "as an angel of light", not exactly the demonic version most Christians ascribe to.
And if a person is using their intentions to bring about GOOD in this world, if their religious path is all about being positive and being a better person and helping others, how on earth could THAT be a vehicle for Satan?

I see witchcraft the same as I do all religious ju-ju. The true power comes from within each of us. We all have great power to do wonderful things with our lives. Our positive thoughts and intentions can bring those very things into our lives (energy attracting energy). So whether one uses spells, or prayers or meditations or some other ju-ju, it's all coming from within us.
We need not fear our own greatness and power, we can embrace it and create lives that create happiness and joy that spills over to everyone around us.
Fear of witches or witchcraft will not help your children love other human beings. It will help them separate things into neat little boxes (Christian/not-Christian) and that inhibits true learning.

Ren, who dabbled with witchcraft as a teen and certainly did not end up being a witch!
