I believe from your previous posts that you are a high school age girl who has been with us on the list for a short while and have also posted on the message board...Is this correct?
If so Hi-and yes a lot of us have concerns before we "make the move".
Well I guess if I was sitting having a coffee with you in person I might ask what your real joys and interests are. Are they such that you can get just so involved in following your interests that one day soon you just don't have time to bother going to public school? LOL
Actually if we knew more about your situation , plans, interests, reasons, parents viewpoint many of us would be more than happy to make more useful comments or offer some ideas.
But yes, thinking of leaving public school could be too scary if you are thinking of it as quitting. If you are looking ahead to having more time and energy and enthusiasm to follow some dreams its more like coming in from the cold to a warm and exciting place.
