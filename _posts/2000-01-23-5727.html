Dear Homeschooling Friends,

Guess what I did in some of my spare time this Christmas break!... I created a Web site for home schoolers! I named it apricot--don't ask me why. I have been racking my brain (and anyone else's brain who got too close to me) this past week, trying to think of fun things home schoolers could do together on the Internet. Finally, I breathed life into some of these ideas and put them on apricot. And now, it's ready for you to see!
Go see apricot at:
http://www.apricot.itgo.com

I brought this Web site into the world for all home schoolers to enjoy. Now, I'm counting on seeing your thoughts, your ideas, your stories, your votes, and your conversations on apricot. Spread the word to other home schoolers! I'll meet you there.

Let's have fun (at apricot, that is),
