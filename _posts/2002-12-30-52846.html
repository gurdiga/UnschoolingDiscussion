Sheila,
I learned this poem for tying a square knot. Start by holding the ropes ends
one in each hand. "Left over right, (pull new right end of rope through the
loop) then right over left,(pull new left end of rope through loop) makes a
knot that's neat and tidy and tight."
HTH
Mary-NY
