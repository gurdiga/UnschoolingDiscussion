"The Peabody Hotel in Memphis is where the ducks live."

Yes, but I think other Peabody hotels do it now too....

It all started in Memphis. It is SO funny to see a fancy lobby full
of people, craning their necks towards the elevator. At exactly 11am,
the door opens and out waddle these ducks, who walk down a red carpet
and jump in the lobby fountain (solid marble) to swim all day.

We've stopped there twice, it's really fun. Once, they gave the kids
little duck pins to wear. My children think it's quite funny that
ducks have their own penthouse upstairs somewhere...
