Registration is now open for the 2nd Annual Unschoolers Winter Waterpark Gathering (UWWG) at Sandusky, Ohio's Kalahari Resort, Feb. 2 - 6, 2009!

Go to our new UWWG website: ugoevent.com

Join unschooolers from around the country for a fun week among other unschoolers at the largest indoor waterpark in America! Their waterpark includes everything from lazy river rides, wave pool for surfing and boogie boarding, to giant water slides and thrilling water coasters.

Thats not all. There will be plenty of opportunities for families to connect with other unschoolers during the week. At no additional cost, our UWWG families will have exclusive use of the Executive Conference rooms where funshops, discussions and social gatherings will be held. Add in talent shows, movie nights, plus an unforgettable night of dancing in the Grand Ballroom and you have a week that cant be missed!!

Our super discounted rooms rates include several types of hotel style rooms along with multiroom full kitchen suites, which were by far our most requested rooms last year (they average out to be only $21.50 per person per night and includes your waterpark passes and participation in all UWWG events!).


The Kalahari Resort has extended our discounted room rates to also include Sunday, Feb. 1, for those wanting to arrive a day early!



Plus, NO registration or conference fees!!!


Last year's gathering was our first and was a big succuss. It ballooned into a gathering of over 1000 unschoolers from 19 states and Canada!
