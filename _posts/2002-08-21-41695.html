It's a book, not a movie, and it's definitely Christian, but I am
reading a book called The Wounded Spirit by Frank Peretti. I'm only
half way through it, but in the first few chapters he describes
incredible cruelty that he endured at school. The first chapter
starts out by talking about these terrible things and it makes you
think he's describing some type of juvenile prison and then you find
out he's talking about his school. I'm anxious to see where he goes
with it...I'm tempted to write him a letter and explain the freedom
that unschoolers have from that kind of school cruelty!
