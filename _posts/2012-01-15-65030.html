unschooling_permission2enjoylife at 2012-01-16 06:21:08 +0200 said:

Hi Stephanie

These are a few of the books I have read, other than Sandra's =) which I personally found to be inspirational also...


Rue Kream's 'Parenting a Free Child - An Unschooled Life' (Easy to read and enjoy another families journey).
http://www.amazon.com/Parenting-Free-Child-Unschooled-Life/dp/1411641558
or directly
http://www.freechild.info/


Jan Fortune-Wood 'Winning Parent, Winning Child - Parenting so that everybody wins' (Very good at helping you think!! Well, it helped me think about my own automated responses to children and helped unblock some of my muddiness).
http://www.amazon.com/Winning-Parent-Child-Jan-Fotune-Wood/dp/0954943309/ref=ntt_at_ep_dpi_4
She has other books too (I havne't read any others though) if you click on her name they should show up in amazon.


The Unschooling Unmanual (A compilation of unschoolers experiences - easy to read and joyful).
http://www.amazon.com/Unschooling-Unmanual-Nanda-Van-Gestel/dp/0968575455


All the best
Natarsha
