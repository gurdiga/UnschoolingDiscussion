This is so funny! After Jack and I spent some time with the frog yesterday,
we went to Barnes and Noble. Jack came up to me lugging this huge book and
said "Look! Its a dissecting book!" What it really is is a DK book called '
Stephen Biesty's Incredible Explosions Exploded Views of Astonishing Things'
written by Richard Platt. The artist, Biesty, draws things that look like
they have exploded. There is a steam tractor, the space station, a windmill,
the tower bridge, the human body and the Grand Canyon to name a few of the
things in the book. Everything is drawn in layers, and cross section views.
It is such a great book and we picked it up on clearance for $4.98 Other
books written by Richard Platt and illustrated by Stephen Biesty are 'Stephen
Biesty's Incredible Cross-Sections', 'Stephen Biesty's Cross-Sections
Man-of-War', and 'Stephen Biesty's Cross-Sections Castle.' As Jack says,
these are all great dissecting books!
~Nancy
