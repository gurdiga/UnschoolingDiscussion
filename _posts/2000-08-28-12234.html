I too have a daughter who does not really have a bed time. Even as a
baby she never needed as much sleep as other children. So we do not
enforce *bedtime* - she goes when she is ready. She is usually
asleep about 10-10.30pm. Rising about 8 - 8.30am. She is not a
morning person and lounges around untill about 10am. It took me a
while to realize that she is different to me - I am a morning person
I do my best work BEFORE 10am... Our body clocks obviously work
differently.

working from work books and loved to learn. My daughter is the
opposite. So when we started H/Sing 5 months ago i expected her to
be like me - my daughter is 11yrs and had attended school for 6yrs -
I really did EXPECT her to be so happy not to have to go to school
(she was very depressed because she was getting bullied) wrong. So
after 5months I decided to go along with what people had suggested on
these message boards and unschool - well at the moment we are
deschooling - I am begining to wonder if she is ever going to
progress to unschooling...............
