VERY very very cold in the winter, and stifling hot and swampy in the summer,
with short but pleasant spring and fall.

My husband went there for a six month project. Nearly four years later I
asked him to please PLEASE just come back home. At first, we visited him
quarterly. After a while the visits were too expensive and the kids weren't
liking the long plane rides. So some of our free quarterly family visits we
bypassed. He got to come home monthly, and so used to work ten hour days so
he could have a full week and two weekends home. So my view of Minnesota,
althought sampled throughout three years, was still as a New Mexican visiting
for a few days.
