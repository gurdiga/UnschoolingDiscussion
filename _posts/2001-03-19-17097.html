Hi, Jennifer --

The laws governing education are different in every state. If you go to
the unschooling.com website, there is a section on the message boards where
you can ask questions about complying with your state laws.

You can find the complete laws at the NHEN.org website, also.

If you want to post here what state you are in, there may be someone on
this list who can give you a thorough answer.

Betsy
