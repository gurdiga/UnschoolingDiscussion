asking for legal advice, I'm just wondering what the NV homeschoolers
are required to do, from people who actually live there and are doing
it.

Thank you,
Melissa

Pamela Sorooshian

5 Jun 2003, at 3:17pm

There is a conference happening in Las Vegas in a couple of weeks -
maybe you could go. It'll be a good one and a GREAT way to meet
homeschoolers and get up-to-date info. They had a legislative victory
there in the past year - so you'd need to be sure your info is current.

To find the info - go to the NHEN website (www.nhen.org) and click on
networking on the top menu on events on the dropdown. You'll see all
the June conferences listed there.

-pam
