Registration for the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference is now Open!

http://www.LiveandLearnConference.org

The conference this year is in beautiful Black Mountain, NC---just
outside of Asheville, September 5-9, 2007. FIVE days filled with
unschoolers having fun!

We are still adding funshops and chats, so check back often. If there
is something *you* would like to see offered that is not there,
consider hosting a funshop or chat yourself!


The prices and deadlines for registration are:

(Early Bird---April 30) Final Deadline---July 31

Family ($290) $350
Adults ($90) $110
Children ($65) $75
Children under 3 free!
Grandparents free!
Family Sitter ($35) $35


Lodging:

We have several lodging options---from hotels to cottages to family
lodges. Co-sleeping children under 5 are not charged for a bed. All
others must buy a bed. $20/person/night.


Meal Plan:

You must choose from one of two meal plans:

The full meal plan - RELAX, VISIT, and EAT - 13 meals served buffet
style at the Blue Ridge Center Dining Hall. This includes breakfast,
lunch, and dinner during your stay.

Children 5-12 $74.25 and adults 13 & up $141

The commuter meal plan -- designed for those staying off-site. Lunch
and Supper each day of your stay.

Children 5-12 $66.75 and adults 13 & up $118.50

Please consider a donation to the Sam Wilkinson Scholarship Fund when
you register to help those who need assistance!

****Registration is NOT refundable. Please be sure you can come before
registering. You may "sell" your registration to another family if
you'd like.

****Payments for lodging and meals are only refundable UNTIL August 5.
The conference must pay the BRA for all lodging and meal plans by the
fifth, so *I* am responsible. There will be no refunds for meals and
lodging after August 5, 2007.
