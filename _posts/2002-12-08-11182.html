This whole thread reminds me when a friend of ours asked for our help to
plan her party, a halloween party. Her theme was Mother Goose stories,
with everyone dressing up as characters from mother goose. Lexie helped
me find books, etc. to help her but was mad that Rita would ask us to
help and not invite us. I explained the party was late evening hours,
and Lexie reminded me she stays up late constantly. I reminded Lexie
that it was an adult only party and lexie said that was not fair and
that she was good with adults and could just come for a bit. I finally
said "Lexie, it's for lesbians only. She does this every year." Lexie
said something about how she might be a lesbian when she grew up and she
needed to have this experience! LOL. She didn't get to go, but let me
tell you, Rita and I were sure thinking that if she could argue her
point that well, she should be allowed to go!

It's hard on kids but you don't get to go to every party, even to every
party you are invited to.

joylyn
