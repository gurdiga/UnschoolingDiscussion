There is a mothers group called FEMALE that was started in order to help moms with this. In fact I think the woman who wrote the sequencing book is a member of FEMALE. Female is a national group and it stands for Formerly Employed Mothers At Loose Ends (it used to be Leading Edge). They are going to be changing their name this spring. I think it's going to be sequencing mothers or something like that. BTW sequencing just means going from full time work to maybe full time mom back to parttime when the kids get a little older to maybe full time again...just for example. It really is an excellent book for those moms who are having a hard time adjusting to full time mother. I know I did. Which is part of the reason I started my very part time home based business. :)
