28 Jun 2000, at 7:57am

Hello everyone,

I just had to write and tell you all about our afternoon art project today!
It is pouring out raining here so I decided for todays art project we would
try some "rain painting". To rain paint you put drops of food coloring on a
paper plate and go out into the rain with it and it supposedly makes a
beautiful picture. WELL......we did end up with beautiful pictures and we
had fun too. But leave it to me to pick a day when it is REALLY raining to
try this project. Lili and I did our food coloring drops and headed out
into the downpour only to have the rain SPLASH food coloring all over
us!!!!! Oh well, C'est la vie. <grin> We had a lot of fun doing it but I
just thought you might be warned that if you should ever try this project to
wear clothes that you don't really care that much about and to be prepared
to do some scrubbing on your skin when you are done! Lili and I both look
like we caught some weird polkadot skin virus! LOL

Holly

ps....Just thought I'd add that even though I ruined my favorite pair of
shorts....the ruined clothes were worth it! Lili had so much fun!
