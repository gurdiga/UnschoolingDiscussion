I just wanted to comment that I found and excellent re-usable resource....the
plastic zip bags that sheets come in!! Seems that everyone in my family got
a set of sheets, matress pad or something bed related that had these
wonderful, heavy duty, zippered bags -- they have come in handy for games,
"little people" and their accesories (my kidlets are under 4), legos, and
other building blocks. If you can find the kind that have snaps instead of
zippers you can snap them over a hanger and hand things out of the way...many
art supplies are in this type of bag and hand in my coat closet (when it's
not BURSTING with coats and winter gear!!).
