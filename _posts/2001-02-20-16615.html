What we did was take some styrofoam balls of different sizes and paint them
up, then hung them from the ceiling in his room. As we painted each one, I
described what it looked like and where in reference to the earth it was
along with what it was made from. It made a great day of both crafting and
learning, and since he helped he was more attentive during the project.
