National Home Education Network

A name has been selected for the new national homeschooling organization:
the National Home Education Network! A mission statement has been defined,
a website is being planned, and nine committees are already hard at work!

The mission statement: "We exist to encourage and facilitate the vital
grassroots work of state and local homeschooling groups and individuals by
providing information, fostering networking and promoting public relations
on a national level. Because we believe there is strength in a diverse
network of homeschoolers, we support the freedoms of all individual
families to choose home education and to direct such education."

To participate in the ongoing discussion about organizational structure,
bylaws, services, philosophy, public relations, etc., please join the
National Organization Steering Committee email list at:
<http://www.onelist.com/subscribe/NHEN>
