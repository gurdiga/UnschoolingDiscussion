Castle Crawford

19 Aug 2001, at 8:13pm
wow. I just read the whole thing and I can say I feel less than inspired! It seemed like each time a good point about homeschooling was brought up, the writer had 2 more points that made it seem like maybe not such a good idea. Even on the "How to get started" page, the last paragraph was nothing less than depressing.

The only valid point I saw FOR putting my kids in public education was the line that read "Home schooling is a social threat to public education," says Chris Lubienski, who teaches at Iowa State University's college of education. "It is taking some of the most affluent and articulate parents out of the system. These are the parents who know how to get things done with administrators." That still did not have me setting my alarm to get Jake enrolled first thing in the morning with the rest of middle Tennessee...

All in all, an interesting article. Thanks for sharing the link, I never would have read it otherwise!

Esther
