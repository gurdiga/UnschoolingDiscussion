Hey Cheryl,

FYI, hubby the computer jock says that Morrowind requires 32bit color
hardware that most laptop graphic cards don't have particularly if they are
still operating on Windows 98. And as the laptop graphic card is built-in,
it can't be upgraded unlike a desktop. Too bad you don't have a desktop to
play it on. My kids were absolutely obsessed with it for at least a
year. The newer laptops, however might be able to accommodate this program.

Anyone in your neighborhood have a desktop that your son could have
visiting privileges to? I know that we accommodate at least three other
boys on a regular basis here!!

Christina in Seattle
