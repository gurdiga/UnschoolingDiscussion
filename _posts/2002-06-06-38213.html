I am on MSM right now for my back injury.
The full name is Methyl-sulfonyl-methane, it's naturally occurring organic
sulfur....very safe.
It has multiple health benefits. The reason I'm on it is to assist my
inflamed disc in calming down, it has anti-inflammatory effects.
Miraculously, all of my allergy symptoms disappeared when I started taking
it. Here's what it says on the bottle:

"MSM provides the body with a bio-available form of organic sulfur to form
connective tissue for healthy joints and muscles. It is necessary for
collagen synthesis for healthy skin, hair and nails. MSM is an antioxidant
and also cleanses the body of pathogens, benefiting those who need seasonal
respiratory support. This miraculous nutrient improves digestion and
metabolism, increases blood circulation, and enjances memory and
concentration. MSM found in natural foods is easily lost during food
processing, making MSM suppplementation a safe and natural way to optimize
your health."

Not sure why it would be useful as a poultice, unless it was for the
anti-inflammatory effect?
All I know is it helped my back heal much quicker, and I'm not sneezing all
the time from pollen!! My son is on it now with his sprained knee, to help
heal the tendons.
