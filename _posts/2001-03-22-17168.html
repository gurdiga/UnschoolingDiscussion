Hi Mel,
I'm from NY. I think the curriculum strictness depends on where you live.
i just copy the appropriate grade information from the world book of
knowledge for my ihips and only have to say we covered "x" number of hours
of a subject, they reached 100% of expectations (i expect them to unschool).
We can test them at home (IOWA test provided free by school) starting in
5th grade. I'm even ask to mark days present and absent. Where else would
they be? As long as they're on this planet I'll mark them present.
Where is Newfield? I don't feel like hunting down the map.
