I don't really know what to say. Thanks.

People helped me unschool and so I'm glad to pass it along. I think it is SO
wonderful to give kids this incredible (and relatively inexpensive) gift. I
think it will change the world, a little bit, and that's enough. Lots of
people don't get to change the world, but every one of these unschooled kids
will affect the people around him or her, and even if it's just a little bit,
it will be a lot. Some of them will unschool their kids. Some of them will
be researchers, writers, politicians, doctors, bus drivers, and will carry on
REAL conversations with kids they meet.

I could go on. Gradually over the days and months, I DO!

This list is great (when it's not dangerously crazy, and even then there's
sometimes an interactive soap-opera greatness), and I'm glad there are so
many others here whose stories we all get to read as they unfold!

Thanks for the kind words, Kelli (and those others, about the "perspective"
post, I saved those; thanks).

Sandra
