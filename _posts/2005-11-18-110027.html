I have to wonder whether they were actually shot in Hawaii or just around
Los Angeles and Malibu. Probably a mixture of both. "CSI Miami" is shot here
in LA; the sound stages for interiors are just down the street in Manhattan
Beach and they shoot most of their exteriors in Venice and environs, with
the occasional "money shot" from Miami. "ER", set in Chicago, is shot in LA.
They take the relevant characters to Chicago for a few days/nights every
couple of months and shoot a bunch of exteriors for multiple episodes.

A movie I designed called "True Friends" is set in Brooklyn and was shot
here too except for some key establishing exteriors. I spent most of my time
trying to find ways to hide distant palm trees, since we weren't of the
budget to use CGI to remove and problem visual elements.

James was working second unit on "Desperate Housewives". They have their own
water bottles for the cast and crew's use with their logo/cast picture
printed on the label.

Trivia, trivia, trivia....
