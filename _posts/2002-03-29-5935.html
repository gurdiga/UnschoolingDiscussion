Yahoo has recently reset all yahoo profiles so that you may be receiving a
lot more spam -- not just email, but phone and snail-mail, too.

You'll have to go in and reset your "marketing preferences" to stop the flood
of spam.

Here is how to do it.

Go to: http://edit.my.yahoo.com/config/eval_profile
and put in your yahoo id and password.

Go down into the middle of the page and click on "Edit my marketing
preferences."

Go down that page and turn everything from "yes" to "no" -- be sure to go all
the way to the bottom - there are more questions about phone contacts and
junk mail down there.

Be sure to click on "save changes" - don't just close the page - when you're
done.
