I have made contact with a gentleman that has started a website that I think
you should all know about and definitely save to favorites!
He is the first person to start a web of information specifically for
unschoolers. It's amazing. He has created a map you can click on and get
information on support groups that are inclusive, non-political AND natural
learning oriented all over the WORLD.

It's a work in progress, so if you go there and don't see a group listed that
you know about, please forward it to him. It must be an inclusive/natural
learning support group.
He has ours listed and I did tell him about Kelly and SOS.

I am formally the U.S. contact person (yikes!) which is very exciting. I hope
he gets a lot of support and it becomes a wonderful resource for everyone.

http://www.alternative-learning.org/

Please check it out!

Also, I am looking for some unschooling support in PA as he has no contact
there yet.
He needs to refer a family that is searching for answers right now.
Anyone in PA?
