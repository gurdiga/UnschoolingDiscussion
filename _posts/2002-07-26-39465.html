Hi folks,

I wanted to (re)introduce myself because I keep wanting to talk here but not
without introducing myself. I'm Pam from Albany, CA (next to Berkeley) and I
used to participate in the AOL unschooling boards back in about 1995 or 96,
so I remember many of you. I'm glad to see so many of you have stuck with
this.

I never stopped unschooling but I did pretty much stop using my computer
(while the rest of the world started doing nothing but...) I'm not sure why
- my mom died, we moved, I had another kid, my husband monopolized the
computer - but anyway, I'm back because I needed the support and found the
time. It doesn't seem like there are many unschoolers in Berkeley, which it
surprising, huh?

My kids are Sam, 8, and Nico, 3. My husband Eric lost his job this month and
we are contemplating a move to Pennsylvania which is hecka scary to me,
native Californian. Especially when I looked up the PA homeschooling laws.
Are any of you unschoolers in PA? I'm wondering how you deal with those
laws. Thanks.

-Pam

Hi Pam!

I'm an unschooler in PA...and, well, it can be a
