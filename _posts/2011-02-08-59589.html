We went to the tour in Seattle and had a great time. My husband went
on the Saturday with Senna and I went to the movie on Sunday with her.
There's a huge display of the new legendary Pokemon (good photo op), a
Pokemon store (for plushies and t-shirts etc.), a stamp rally, an
activity zone, a demo version of the game, and a downloadable Celebi
at Gamestop.

The movie tickets are free and they have around 6 showings a day, but
be prepared to hang around and stand in line and have the distribution
changed at the last minute <sigh>. Also, all the parties you want
tickets for must be present when you request tickets.

The coolest thing is seeing just how many Pokemon afficionados there
are, young and older. Some cosplay, so it's fun figuring out what
characters they are.
