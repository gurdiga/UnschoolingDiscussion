There is more than a calendar to natural family planning now -
and probably always has been among folks in tune with their
bodies. After a few months of using NFP, you will find which of
the multiple indicators of fertility are easiest for you to keep
up with. My daughter-in-law swears by basal temperature; I am
more of a cervix and mucus kind of girl (especially with peri
menopause beginning to rear its head); my mother was blessed with
reliable mittelschmertz, which gave her a fool proof half of the
month.

...and ya know, we each are pretty happy with the number of kids
we have - though my mother lost a number to miscarriage, and my
D-i-L isn't done by any stretch of the imagination.
