whole list, because that would defeat the purpose of this post. I am

offended, and attacking people. If that sentence makes you mad, then
maybe you are a committing the offenses listed and need to stop.

Remember we are all on the same side. We all want our freedom, we all
love to learn. We all have things we feel very strongly about. We need
to remember that beating someone over the head with our viewpoint will
not change their mind, but polite discourse can do wonders to open
minds.
