Maria,
I couldn't find my directory issue of GWS just yet, but here are some
websites to get ya started.
This one is a Georgia coast unschooling group.
Now I know nothing about these groups, if they truly unschool or not. But at
least you could start here and see where it leads!

http://www.angelfire.com/ga2/unschoolers/

This next link is to a group that looks very inclusive...all religious
beliefs and so on. Not unschool support, but at least they'll know the laws
well and probably be able to help you.

http://llg.freeyellow.com/

sunschoolers@... This is an email addy for the S.U.N. group. They
are the main group that the first link I gave you is under. Stands for
Southern Unschooler Network.
This one is a group that has a lot of information at their website. Sounds
fairly inclusive and eclectic, this was listed at unschooling.com.

http://www.mindspring.com/~lei/aaen/

And don't forget to visit unschooling.com!!

Happy trails,
Ren
