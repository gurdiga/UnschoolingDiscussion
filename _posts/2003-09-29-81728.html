**I have posted a bit before and thought some of you might have
suggestions as you are good at thinking out of the box. My name is
Christine. I live in Costa Rica. I am trying to return to the U.S.A. I
would be returning without my husband. **

Unless you have family that you want or need to live near, I would
encourage you to compare the cost of living in different parts of the US
before picking a place to live. (When a friend of mine moved from
Tucson to Silicon Valley, the family income doubled but they had a
*harder* time making ends meet and felt poorer.)

I've seen a calculator that compares cost of living in different U.S.
cities on the Money magazine website, somewhere.
