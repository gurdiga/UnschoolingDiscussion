I did my psychology Master's thesis on whether the University of Houston's
selections criteria for their undergraduate psychology students predicted
success in the program (graduation). I found zero correlation between SAT
scores, GPA, whatever and graduation. I was thrilled to debunk the system.
I got called for an interview with the Dean for my troubles. He was less
than thrilled.
