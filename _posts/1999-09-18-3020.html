The GWS conference is next week! Thought some of you might be interested in
this info. Please pass this info on to anyone you know who might be
interested in a conference geared toward unschoolers. Nancy and I will be
attending and hope to see you there!

***********************
**** MASSACHUSETTS - Growing Without Schooling Conference at Eisner Camp,
Sept. 24, 25, 26, 1999, Great Barrington, MA - The Berkshires, sponsored by
Holt Associates, Inc. GWS Annual Conference, 2380 Mass. Ave Suite 104,
Cambridge, MA 02140-1226; (617) 864-3100; or holtconf@... -
http://www.holtgws.com/conference.htm

This is a conference that the whole family can enjoy! Nestled in the hills
of the Berkshire mountains, the camp lets you experience early fall foliage
season in New England in the company of homeschooling families, speakers
from across the United States, and the friendly and knowledgeable staff of
Eisner Camp. Children participate in well-run activities while you attend
the sessions you wish; teens can attend activities or sessions. There are
also a private teen lounge and special teen activities, vendors, plenty of
indoor meeting spaces, playground, boating, biking, basketball and tennis
courts and a heated swimming pool. Accommodations on site include two nights
lodging, six full meals, coffee, fruit and snacks throughout the weekend.

FUN Books and other vendors will be there so come prepared to shop! If you
have any special order items you would like to get, or if you would just
like to save on shipping, send us a list of the items you want and you can
pick them up at the conference. Be sure to stop by our table and say hi!
**************************
