My daughter and I got a Pooh book from the library about school. >rolling my
eyes> She loves anything with Pooh. As I was reading it, I was think of
this list. I wanted to share some of the comments in it.

"This schooling business-pencils and whatnot-it's overrated if you ask me."
(says Eeyore)

Later .. "If you have to go that far from home, I'm sure school is not a good
thing for Piglets, " said Piglet.

A page or two later, Tigger is discussing bouncing in school. Here is the
conversation: "there's no bouncing in school,"said Eeyore decisively.
"None?" asked Tigger. "School is work. No time for fun, " said Eeyore.

hummmmmmm, need I say more. ........Of course the book goes on to say what
neat things you learn in school like ABC and counting, but we all know these
things are easier to learn when we are having fun.
