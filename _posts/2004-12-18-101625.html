And attitude isn't everything, really, when it comes to cleaning. Some of it
is actually physically moving stuff around. But that's not my problem; my
problem is when I touch something I haven't touched for "the past year" (or
years) I remember what I was supposed to have done with it, what I wish I could do
with it, who I meant to give it to, and then I put it in a different pile.
So that attitude I've come to like is that I'm glad the roof isn't leaking on
my important piles of stuff. I'm glad we have enough clothes that some can sit
clean on the couch for days before I get around to folding them. I'm glad we
have enough dishes that even if the sink is overflowing with dirties, people
can find a clean plate and glass and spoon.

I guess attitude IS everything!
