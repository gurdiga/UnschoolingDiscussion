Finger Paint Recipe
(yield 2 cups)

1/2 cup white flour
2 cups of water
1 TBSP of glycerin
1 tsp borax
several small jars and lids for storing
food coloring

mix the flour with 1/2 cup of water to form a paste.
add the rest of the water and cook over low heat until the mixture is thick
and clear, stirring constantly.
cool the mixture and then add the glycerin and the borax. if mixture is too
thick, you may add a little more water.
Divide the mixture into the jas and add the desired color.
This recipe dries to a matte (dull) finish.
