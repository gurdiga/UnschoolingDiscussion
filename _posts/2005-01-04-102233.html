My girls' friend discovered a text to speech program on the computer that
the girls have been having fun with. We have windows. If you click on *My
Computer*, then select *Control Panel*, then select *speech*, you can type
in anything and it is vocalized as speech in one of three voices you can
choose from. You can speed up or slow down the speech. I hesitate to tell
you what they are typing in, but it's been really fun. (The first thing
they typed in was the phrase "I am a moron." With the voice slowed down, it
was really funny.)
