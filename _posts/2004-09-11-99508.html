Many of you already know Diane Flynn Keith. She's the Editor of Homefires,
author of Carschooling, and owner of several yahoo groups, including
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/ClickSchooling/ and
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Carschooling/. Did I leave anything
out? Probably! She's also a long time homeschooler and CHN Local
Contact, not to mention a person who is often interviewed by the media
regarding homeschooling. She'll be speaking at next year's CHN Family Expo
too, which I'm looking forward to.

In other words, Diane is one busy person! Even so, her passion regarding
the intense government push to make preschool and kindergarten compulsory
not just in California but nationwide is leading her into a new activity.

Please read what she has to say and forward it to all the people you know
who are also interested in young children (homeschoolers or not). Join her
new list and please contribute ideas that will help support parents of
young children so that they don't feel that they MUST send their children
to preschool in order for them to have a successful start in life!
