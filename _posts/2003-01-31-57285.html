I know many of you already posted about your MB type, but I am a very
visual, big-picture type of person, and I would love to see how the
graph would look of this group. I know the tests are sometimes
ambiguous in their wording, but I also find that a good way to
determine type is to look at the description for the types you feel
closely resemble you, and the right one should be pretty obvious.
Maybe not the most scientific of methods, but if nothing more, it
would be a fun way to see how "uniform" our personalities are or
aren't.

Here is a fun description page that I liked because it isn't just a
generic, "good qualities" one. It has a little humor in pointing out
the less-desirable aspects of each personality type also. You have
to scroll way down to find "THE 16 TYPES IN FULL DETAIL" with the 16
types in blue, and click on your type.

http://members.tripod.com/~PersonalityInstitute/Personality1.htm


Have fun!
