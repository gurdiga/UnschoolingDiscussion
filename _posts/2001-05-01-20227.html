Nanci, Here are some quick thoughts:

1. Hydrogen peroxide
2. Tea tree oil products (ointment/first aid cream)
3. Calendula cream/ointment - a derivative of marigold and sold at most health food stores.
4. Colloidal silver products. These can be bought in several forms including ointment. This is a natural antibiotic and can be bought in a form suitable for oral consumption and safe for children.

Hope your son heals soon! Annette
