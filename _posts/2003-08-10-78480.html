Julie Solich

10 Aug 2003, at 1:11am

We have had a great day today. We headed out to the bush to get some more
firewood. jWe found millipedes, geckos of all sizes, all sorts of fungus and
a lot of the wildflowers are starting to bloom so it was really pretty out
three. The boys chose to help us too which was great. So now it's raining
and we have lots of wood, just going to get a fire started now.

It's such a good feeling to have the whole family working together
peacefully. Jacob kept bringing us drinks when we looked thirsty which was
really sweet. So much better when they do stuff without any prompting just
because they love you and they see you doing it for them.

Julie
