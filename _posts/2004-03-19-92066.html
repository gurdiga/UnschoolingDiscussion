There are several support group lists I am on. I live in Columbus, OH.
This one is statewide - oh-homeschool at yahoo groups. There is also
Unschoolers Network Ohio, UNO at yahoo groups - this one hasn't been too
busy lately though. Also OHS-ANN is an ohio homeschool announcement list
for activities, classes, etc., throughout Ohio, another yahoo group.
Several people from northeast Ohio post to these lists regularly -
Akron/Canton area, some in Youngstown. You might check these out for
current info.
Reatha McCafferty in OH
