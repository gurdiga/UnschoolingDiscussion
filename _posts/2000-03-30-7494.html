I wanted to share this....
I'm sitting here at the computer going through email, and then I was
reading the post from Sandra Dodd on Unshcooling.com about reading.
As I'm doing this my boys come to me with paper they have been doing
next to me at the dining room table. They want to know what they wrote
(they are 7 and 5) I have been bothered by the not reading thing, and
the no interest. I also have a bad habit of comparing ect... So here
they come with these papers, and proceed to tell me all the letters they
wrote. I didn't even know they knew a fraction of these letters. I'm
thrilled!! I wish you could see their faces. So proud they are.
