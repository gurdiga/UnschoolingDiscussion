I've played all the racing games we have had or rented. I've played the ones
with real steering wheels in the arcades. They are really hard to play.
They're nothing like actual driving. Whole different sensation. Being good at
the games could lead to a cockiness that should quickly dissipate in a new
driver. I've seen that cockiness on the go-kart track among 14 yo's who have
never driven. My own 14 yo's quickly realized the difference when they were
behind the real wheel.
