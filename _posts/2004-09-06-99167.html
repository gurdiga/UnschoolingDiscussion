OK, so for anyone who was at the conference, I was the wide-eyed,
neophyte woman from Canada who kept saying, "But I'm really
scared..." I am somewhat less terrified these days (thanks to this
great site, etc) but my husband and youngest son go b-2-s tomorrow,
so the old panic is starting to rear its ugly schoolhead again. I
met so many wonderful people at the conference, and now I'm really
lonely. I was wondering if any of you unschoolers would mind moving
to Canada? It's a bit chilly in the winter, but we don't have
hurricanes (OR REPUBLICANS)!! C'mon - think about it! We make things
like bacon, and beer! We made Mike Meyers and Celine Dion! I have a
great husband and 3 beautiful boys. We have a non-shedding dog and a
pool. Let me know whatever else your hearts desire, and I'll see
what I can do to sweeten the pot (oh yeah, they grow that, too).
Hurry, though, cuz we are now down to one income so I don't know how
much longer we'll have the pool (the house may have to go, too).
Cheers,
Sandy
