have now been listed for most main speakers.  I'm particularly happy
to announce that Nicky Hardenbergh, former NHEN board member, will

it has impacted education (as well as homeschooling) in this country. Relaxed
and eclectic homeschoolers will learn much from 'homeschool guru' (and
author of many books) Mary Hood, and unschoolers/child-led proponents will
enjoy unschooling spokesperson and author Alison McKee. By popular request,
Shirley Minster will be presenting her full two-session workshop on learning
styles - you'll
know all about yours and your children's by the end of this one!

Everyone loves performer and storyteller Jim Weiss and one of Maine's
favorites, Ron St. Saviour - a long-time friend of homeschoolers who has
tailored his outdoor education programs to fit their needs.

There are workshops on gifted education, parenting, much needed "how-tos" ,
meetings for leaders/organizers and  much more.
