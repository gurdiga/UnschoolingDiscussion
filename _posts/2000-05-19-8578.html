Hi LeeAnn. The Scope and Sequence is a list of "Typical Course
Study" broken down by grade level and subject. There are different
place to obtain this however, they are pretty much all the same. I
think the best place to get it is:

www.worldbook.com/ptrc/hyml/curr1.htm

Please keep in mind that this list is a "Typical Course Study" that
the ps system thinks should be taught. This is where I ran into
troubles. It is however a good place to get started. If your child
is a hands on learner like mine you may find it difficult to follow
this exactly. I have found that asking questions from people who
have been doing this for a long time is the best.

Good luck in your adventure.
