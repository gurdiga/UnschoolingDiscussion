Many of you know about the Campbell's Soups "Labels for Education"
program.
We just discovered that individual homeschool families are eligible
for the
program as well. The letter below was the response we got when asking what
to provide. Very easy! At the bottom of the letter is the contact
information from the coordinator, Anne Pizarro.

FYI - there is also a "Labels for America" program. With this, if your
child does any kind of volunteer work/project you can get up to 2,000
points
a year.

You can go to their website www.labelsforeducat ion.com then "Coordinators
and Schools" (on the left), then "Manage the Program", then "Advice from
Anne".

This is a great program where you can get all kinds of stuff from art
supplies to electronics.

Happy collecting!
Vallery LaBarre

PS: Box Tops for Education (from General Mills) also allows homeschool
groups to participate, but there has to be 15+ children. It's worth
getting
your homeschool group involved. www.boxtopsforeduca tion.com
