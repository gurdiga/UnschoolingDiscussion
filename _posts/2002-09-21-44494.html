It's been a long time since I logged on and read here. I had a wallet stollen
1 month ago from my unlocked car. Today it was hot, baby T 2.5years threw a
fit. I brought her shoes with us. Such a terrible mom I am. Well then son 5.5
was a little wild after homeschool tennis group and wanted to race 4 other
kids in the parking lot. We are at the high school in town. Not a runable
parking lot. So I have a new wallet, two kids going a zillion directions and I
just don't know what I did with the fine black leather which houses my
identity and ice cream money. At 3pm I tear the car apart wondering where it
is. I want to take the kids for ice cream at 4pm. We drive to swim practice,
the two little one's and I cool off in the local fountain. All the while I'm
wondering, did I leave that wallet on top of the car?

I didn't. Turns out baby T secured my wallet with Bitty baby in her back pack.
I sure am glad I didn't leave my wallet on the roof of my car.

We ate sundaes, shopped for birthday gifts, had pizza, and I never never never
am going to lose this wallet, again.

Mary H.
