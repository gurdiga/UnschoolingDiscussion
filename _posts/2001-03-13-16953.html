Become a Member!

As members of the Canadian Museum of Nature, you and your family can explore Canada's natural history as often as you wish. With our extensive displays of specimens covering four billion years of the Earth's history, you always find something new to discover in our signature galleries and frequently changing special exhibitions. Our ongoing and seasonal programmes respond to the interests of Nature's diverse visitors through events and activities geared to the general public as well as unique activities for educators and their students.

We guarantee your relationship with us will stimulate your curiosity, provoke thought and strengthen your knowledge of and respect for the natural world. And as a member, you receive benefits that include free access to over 250 museums world-wide, making the fun and learning even greater.

Spend extra time at Nature -- not extra money! Treat yourself, your family or friends to a full year of fun and discovery with a membership to the Canadian Museum of Nature.

Highlights of Member Benefits

One of the unique benefits of becoming a member of the Canadian Museum of Nature is that you receive a piece of Canada's ancient past in return. With your membership card, you become the owner of an authentic Canadian fossil fragment of a mammal dating from the last Ice Age period. Your fossil may be up to 25,000 years old, placing it in the late Pleistocene, or the most recent period of the epoch that covered from two million to 10,000 years ago.

You also receive:
unlimited free admission to the Museum at the Victoria Memorial Museum Building;
discounts on all of Nature's mail-order publications [Take a look at our online catalogue!];
discounts on selected public programmes;
special mailings of Nature's Calendar of Events brochure and our Nature Scene newsletter;
one complimentary family pass, so you can bring your friends; and
invitations to special events, gallery openings, book launches and workshops.
