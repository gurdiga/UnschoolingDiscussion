**I think that the message boards at www.unschooling.com could be a
little bit more user-friendly. I'll be reading through messages
there and consider responding to something and then realize that I'm
about to respond to something that is a year or two old. **

I've recently reset the boards interface so that new conversations will
appear at the top of the list and I'm gradually reordering old conversations so
oldest are last (removing truly outdated and useless stuff as I go - some stuff
is timeless). I *think* that should help with the problem of folks
inadvertantly responding to very old messages because they didn't notice the date.

I'd appreciate hearing (in the message boards Suggestions folder
http://www.unschooling.com/discus/messages/3583/3583.html ) what other changes users might
like to see. Different folders, fewer folders, different order, whatever... :)
