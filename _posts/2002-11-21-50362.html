> What a helpful response! I would certainly like to volunteer to attend
> the LIFE fest. I will bring over our Education Information and answer
> any questions from the participants. This would be a great chance to
> listen to comments and receive a different perspective directly from the
> market. Thank you for the invitation! What a coincidence in dates!
>
> The charge for attendance is certainly a change from the open policy
> from prior years, and local teachers have had a bit of a harder time
> organizing and executing field trips. However, the increase in price was
> necessary to bring in the new amazing exhibits, activities and
> resources. See, KSCVC is not funded at all by government money, and the
> admission prices have allowed incredible improvements to the educators
> experience! We are now, after these improvements, one of the top 5
> attractions in Central Florida, and less expensive than our competition.
>
>
> I will definitely keep the discounted admission day in mind. The Salute
> to Homeschoolers Day does have a cost, but lunch and specialized
> activities are included to the participants.
