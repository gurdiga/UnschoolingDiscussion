I just had to share my favorite Ocarina site:
http://www.songbirdocarina.com/

I love instruments that you can carry in a pocket or around your
neck. Something really cool about having your tunes handy for any
situation!:)

I bought the soprano ocarina from Songbird and it sounds lovely.
I've really been wanting the little silver pendant one. I bet it's
hard to get your fingers on those teensy holes though.

Has anyone ever heard of "puny tunes"? This guy made up his own
little instrument which is a tiny wood flute, like a mix between a
recorder and an ocarina and called them Puny Tunes. He doesn't sell
them anymore, but a friend of mine gave me one...it's really cool.
