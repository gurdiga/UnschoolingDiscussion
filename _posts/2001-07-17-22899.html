Hi Everyone

I've just joined this List (and three others)! We've "officially" been
homeschooling for nearly two years now (we were doing some intensive
"parenting" before then *grin*). We've been following a delight directed
Unit Study approach for our content subjects using good childrens literature
to tie it all together (FIAR has sort of guided us and given me tons of
confidence to "go-it-on-my-own"). For Math we've been dabbling around in
Miquon (and loving it) and for phonics we use Letterland (a British
curriculum) to introduce the alphabet and the phonic sounds but have moved
onto the British version of Reading Reflex to actually teach reading when
we've felt like some formal stuff. I've just completed the Unschooling
Handbook by Mary Griffith and am very excited to slide into this method of
learning (just find the Math-thing a little daunting!) I'm the South
African distributor of all the above mentioned curricula so this change of
direction is sort of embarassing *smile* ! I was sooo pleased to see Teri
on this List as I've just discovered your beautiful Site and the book you
wrote for Christian Unschoolers on the 'Net!!! Are there any Lists or
Message Boards for *Christian* Unschoolers available?

Adèle in South Africa
HS Mom to a 7yob and 5yo twin boys
