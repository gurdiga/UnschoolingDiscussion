If any of you have a child who is interested in chemistry but hates dealing with the math part of it, you should check out Merlin's Academy of Alchemy online or email Merlin at
Merlin@... .
It makes chemistry possible WITHOUT the emphasis on the math part of it. When your child finishes this (at his/her own pace) they will have equivalent to 1st year college chemistry. It's really good. We got it a couple of years ago and then lost it (it's a hypertextbook) because of computer problems so I emailed Merlin and we downloaded it again. He also sent us Astronomy as a sort of bonus the second time because he no longer offered the test that went along with the Alchemy program which we had originally bought. I think the Alchemy is $40. for the entire program. (The Astronomy is also really good! though I don't know the cost of it.)This is all on the computer so if they like science and using the computer then they will probably love this.
I'm not preaching textbooks and structured learning here. Just making it available to them to use as they would like.
Are any of you familiar with this program?
