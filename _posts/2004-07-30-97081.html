anthing by Robert Kiyosaki?

If you can get a copy of his book, "If You Want to be Rich and Happy, Don't Go to School" - I would highly highly recommend it. It deals with the programming that school (and society/family etc...) gives you. Actually, if you can devour any of his books I'd suggest it! His books are usually financially geared, but they are very interesting and deal with the mindset that we are programmed with from school. His book "Rich Dad, Poor Dad" is excellent as well and shows the difference between his biological father ("Poor Dad") who was a public school teacher, etc... and his best friend's dad and his financial mentor ("Rich Dad") who did not have the education his biological father had.
