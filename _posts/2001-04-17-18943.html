My sister is 33 and she was told she needed an amnio done because she was
past 30. She decided against it but she worried that maybe she should have it
done. The doctors really scared her. Personally, she had enough problems thru
her pregnancy (her uterine wall tore at around 6 mos. & caused vag. bleeding
and she was put on bedrest to let it heal itself which it did), begun
pre-term labor 3 mos. ahead of schedule and had to be given meds to stop it 3
times (they worked the last time and she went to 2 days before her due date),
etc.. The amnio may have caused her to miscarry and believe me, no matter
what she would love Bailey Kaylene no matter what and is so happy that Little
Miss Bailey made her arrival this past Easter Sunday (she is fine with all
her fingers and toes, beautiful curly hair and is perfectly fine in other
areas....the doctors had my sister CONVINCED that Miss Bailey would have
Down's! (not that it would have mattered but my sis sure did not the added
stress of worrying).
