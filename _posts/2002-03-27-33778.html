26 Mar 2002, at 10:18pm

I've been spending a lot of my time since Saturday reflecting on being
one of the new owners of this list. I've been in contact with a number
of friends both on-line and IRL and I've spent a lot of time talking
with my dearest friend of all, my husband. The conclusion that I
reached is that I cannot be an owner of this list now. In fact I
will no longer be a member of this list. The reasons that drew me
here in June, 1999 no longer exist for me. Just so no one feels
like I am singling out this list - I'm leaving a lot of others too.
I've unsubbed from 18 lists in the past 24 hours and I have a few
more to go... (I guess my Flylady 27 Fling Boogie will be email
lists this week.)

I wish all of you well in your unschooling journey. It's very likely
I'll see you around the Internet in the future.

Good-bye and good luck whereever your journey takes you,

Cindy
