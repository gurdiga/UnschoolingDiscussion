In social interaction, many people with Asperger's syndrome demonstrate gaze
avoidance and may actually turn away at the same moment as greeting another.
The children I have known do desire interaction with others but have trouble
knowing how to make it work. They are, however, able to learn social skills much
like you or I would learn to play the piano.
