Hi All--

I've been lurking for a few days, so thought I'd say, "Hi."

I'm mom to three unschoolers, ages 5, 11 and 14 (well, 15 next week--
YIKES!) We started out as reluctant public schoolers, then
homeschooled briefly in a school-at-home manner until I realized that
was just plain stupid, and then took a deep breath and let ourselves
become unschoolers. We're in our 6th year and life simply
feels...RIGHT. :-)

I'm really pleased to find this list, as I used to lurk on the
unschooling.com boards and recognize a few names from there. Those
boards really made me think and re-examine some assumptions about
learning, as I hope (I'm sure) this list will.
