> I'm asking for advice for a friend who's unschooling and is contemplating
> a divorce. Does anyone have any pointers to information about child
> custody for homeschoolers?

Gather as much proof as you can that the husband has supported
homeschooling/unschooling before the separation.
In general, judges are loathe to change the schooling situation when a
family splits up.
If someone is already divorced and wants to homeschool it tends to be harder
to get approval in court.
