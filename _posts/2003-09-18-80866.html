Hi All,

The website is now finished for my un/homeschooling book, Homeschooling
Reflections. I'd love to have you visit the site. My five children have learned
at home since 1981, when the oldest two left public school. They all remained
at home until entering college. At this time Chris, who is married and has a
daughter, is working as an accountant. Shawn will graduate in Dec. with a
degree in Theatre, and is applying for graduate school. Devin is a journey man
in the heating/air conditioning field. Donika majors in music and dance at
Grossmont Community College, and Trevor at 15 is still home.

This book covers some of the stories of our experiences. It is written to
provide you mentoring support, as though I were your older sister. I talk about
some of the challenges we have encountered and some of the questions I have
been asked through the years.

I'm taking advanced orders, which won't be processed until the book is ready
to send out. ****PLEASE NOTE ORDERS PLACED BY OCT 15 WILL NOT BE CHARGED
SHIPPING.

The cover of the book was designed by my good friend's daughter, Ashley
Parsons, who was homeschooled. The website was created by my nephew, Steven
Potter, who left high school out of boredom to attend technical school for
computers, which he also left to run his own business.

It has been an amazing life experience, not only to watch my children grow to
young adults, four of whom are now out in the world, but also to watch the
growth of the un/homeschooling movement.

Please visit the website at: www.Homeschoolingreflections.com

Feel free to share the site with any people or lists that you think might
have an interest in it.

Thanks,
Connie Colten
