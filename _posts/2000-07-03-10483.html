Billy or Nancy

3 Jul 2000, at 3:36pm

Michelle Rafter, a reporter with the Industry Standard
(www.thestandard.com), is writing an article on unschoolers and the
Internet. She's interested in finding out some of the resources used for
unschooling children and if there any special web sites devoted to
unschooling. Do unschoolers just turn their kids loose on the Web and let
them explore, and could they unschool without the Internet? If you would
like to answer these questions and others about unschooling, please contact
Michelle by July 9, 2000 to arrange an interview.

Michelle V. Rafter
Contributing Writer, The Industry Standard
LIVEWIRE columnist, Reuters
Phone: (714) 870-6722
Fax: (707) 248-5013
Email: mvrafter@...

and be sure to note the response deadline of July 9, 2000.
