You are cordially invited to join a new unschooling discussion list.
Why another group? This list is intended to complement existing
groups. Open to both kids and adults, it is a place for us to
discuss the process of unschooling ourselves.

List description:
You don't have to be a kid or have kids in order to be an unschooler.
There are many wonderful groups that already provide a forum for
parents or young people interested in unschooling.

In order to bring everyone together, Unschooling Ourselves is broader
in scope--a home for people of any age who are interested in
exploring how we have been limited by a society that believes in
compulsory schooling, and how we have learned to transcend those
limits in our day-to-day lives.

How has the concept of unschooling changed your life? From the
mundane to the extraordinary, all topics are open for discussion and
debate.

We hope you can join us! If interested, please visit
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/unschoolingourselves/.


Julie Boerst
