Almost time for the Unschoolers Potluck Supper and Games:

Wed. July 13, starting at 5 PM at Redwood Park in Oakland.

We will meet at Redwood Park's Roberts Recreation Area, in the picnic
area above the parking lot (the grassy field by the playground near
Roberts Pool). For more information about the location, please see
www.ebparks.org/parks/robrec.htm.

Please bring:
* a dish to share,
* something to drink,
* a picnic blanket and/or folding chairs,
* your own plates and eating utensils
* flashlight for flashlight tag, if desired
* any other games/rec equipment you want
There may be a charge for parking, so please be prepared with small bills
or coins (or East Bay Parks card).

We're planning a get-to-know-each-other game (optional, of course), hoping
to keep it not too long or lame, but just as a way to get to know each
other more easily.

We'll also bring a big parachute.

Any questions? Please contact either Robyn Talman at ratalman@... or
Pam Tellew at pamtellew@...

We hope to see you there!
Robyn Talman and Pam Tellew
