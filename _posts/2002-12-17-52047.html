Rachel Ann

17 Dec 2002, at 3:32pm

I'm 5'1" and I weigh about 132 lbs...don't know for certain as it has been
sometime since I weighed myself. Fact is, I don't, in general, care. In
any case, I am usually thought of as slim, and when I checked on some diet
site that would give me the perfect diet for me, I was told, there is a
problem, you aren't overweight.

Looks are really a matter of proportion rather than health. I have been
blessed, cursed, what have you with the hour glass figure, and my thighs and
butt takes the weight.

What is more important is how one feels and physically, how one is able to
get on with the day to day tasks. I don't feel good when I weight less than
118, and if I went down to 125 that would be fine too....

As to MIL comments, that lass is short a few. I feel sorry for the gal that
she can't see herself or anyone else in any terms except those of size...

be well,
Rachel Ann
