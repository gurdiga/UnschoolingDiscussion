Here's a neat site. I guess their goal is to make the whole world a
library where you'll never know where you'll find a book. You drop
off books at used book stores, park benches, airports, etc. with a
label in that they're part of this program. People who find the book
are supposed to go to the website and enter into a journal you've
made up for the book where the book ended up, whether or not they
liked the book, etc. http://www.bookcrossing.com
