I told him my symptoms and he picked out the Triaminic that would suit me best. (Adult cold medicine is often a little too much for me). I gave him 15 dollars and we went through the line. The total came up to $15.79 and he said "Mom, I need another dollar". His change was 21 cents that he informed me was "Two dimes and a penny".

When we got home, we all sat down and read "My Many Colored Days" by Dr. Suess. The kids decided I was probably having a "Grey Day".

I'd really like to be better now, but I'm just really so proud of them.
