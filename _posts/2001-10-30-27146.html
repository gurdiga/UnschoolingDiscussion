I find your responses fascinating!

Don't/didn't many of us have kids waking and crying about planes flying
in the sky, asking questions like why is our country bombing another
country, why did this happen to us, why are so many people losing their
jobs now, is our country the best country in the world, etc.?

How do you answer them and what sources do you use for your
information? I can certainly understand that you may have limited (or
no) interest in personal opinions posted but I think we can all learn
from the sources referenced. And as we learn more, we may even be able
to answer our children's questions with better information which must
have something to do with unschooling.

Then again, you could just pick and choose what to read here by subject
line.
