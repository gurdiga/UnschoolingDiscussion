20 Feb 2017, at 3:37am
I have a mystery. I didn’t receive my own Just Add Light and Stir mailing for the past two nights. (I went to bed early and am up at 3:30 a.m. in Albuquerque; unnatural, but just in case anyone noticed the time.)

I went to see what it might be, and Feedburner (a google function I use to let people subscribe to the blog by mail) shows 90 subscribers. I thought there should be 1500.

Looking at the stats there, it says February 18, subscribers: 1,654
February 19, subscribers 90

Maybe this is a glitch feedburner will fix on their own. With it being a Monday-off holiday in the U.S. maybe it won’t be seen or dealt with until Tuesday. But I thought just maybe someone here would know something, or at least I could let some subscribers know of the problem.

My next largest subscription feed is to the unschooling site news
http://sandradodd.com/aboutunschooling
which has 193 subscribers and had 191 the day before, so that one isn’t messed up, seems.

I don’t think re-subscribing is necessary; I think it will be restored, but if anyone wants to see the two that didn’t go out, here:

http://justaddlightandstir.blogspot.com

I hope this is the worst thing that happens this week, to any of us! :-)

Sandra
