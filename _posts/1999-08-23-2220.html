Dear Editor-

I have just received an e-mail on the comic that you chose to print on a
day of homeschooling. I can tell you that your interpretation of
homeschooling is wrong !! I hope that you have a homeschooling support
group in your area that you can visit and families that you can talk to
so that you can see that extremely few homeschoolers are like that. My
daughter at the age of 6yo. knows plenty of the "world" outside of our
home. She has a childhood, more than a child who spends 8 hours away
from their family and stuck in a classroom, behind a desk, with a teacher
that can not give individual attention to each child because she has so
many to teach and she is only one person.

If you don't have a support group in your area, I would love to be your
guinea pig- study our family, see for yourself, and learn more of
homeschoolers before painting such a ugly picture of how you interpret
what homeschoolers are like.

Sincerely,
