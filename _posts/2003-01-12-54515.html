I would definitely call the owner of the house and ask him to look into it.
One of my daughters friends have just recently moved back into their house
after a year because of mold. They were all getting sick and suspected
something as they had roof problems also. They called their insurance
company and they took care of it from there. The whole inside of the house
had to be gutted and redone. It was all covered but they were out of the
house and in an apt. for a year. Definitely something to get taken care of
though.
