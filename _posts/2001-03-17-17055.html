Turn Beauty Inside Out is our ongoing public awareness and action
campaign
to change the unhealthy messages about beauty that harm kids and
adults.
The campaign celebrates Inner Beauty-the beauty of action, caring and
making
the world a better place in ways both large and small.

The special (full-color!) May/June 2001 issue of New Moon with the
theme of
"25 Beautiful Girls" spreads our message and involves all our readers
in the
campaign.

May 16, 2001 is our Action Day (it's the 3rd Wednesday in May every
year).

Get tons more details at our website at http://www.newmoon.org/ -
click on
Turn Beauty Inside Out.

The Latest
We have over 50 organizations who have endorsed the "Turn Beauty
Inside Out"
Campaign and we're receiving more every day. A full list is on our
website
at http://www.newmoon.org/TBIOD/supporting_organizations.htm.
With this kind of united effort we really can make a difference and
Turn
Beauty Inside Out.

It's not too late to be added to this list. Endorse the campaign and
get
your organization to do the same. What does this mean? It means you
agree
to publicize the campaign in newsletters, publications, emails,
websites,
list servs, by distributing flyers, by organizing an event, by talking
to
your local media, and by any other means you can devise. Go to
http://www.newmoon.org/TBIOD/become_an_endorser.htm to find out how
easy it
is!

Create Your Own Ad Contest
The deadline for this contest is less than a month away! We've heard
from
lots of kids who are working in groups or by themselves to enter this
contest. You can help kids age 16 and under enter our "Make Your Own
Ad"
contest. We're seeking ads that successfully and creatively portray
Inner
Beauty. We want ads for TV, radio, print & the web. We're also
encouraging
kids to contact their local media to run the ads on May 16. Contest
rules
and details are at www.newmoon.org/TBIOD/contest.htm

Donate Frequent Flyer Miles
We need 500,000 frequent flyer miles to bring several of the 25
Beautiful
Girls, the Girl Editors, and the winners of our ad contest to New York
for
meetings with the ad industry on May 10-11. Every single donated
ticket
will bring us closer to the goal and make our message louder and
clearer as
it's delivered by girls themselves. Email lindae@n... to make your
donation.

Sponsors Needed
Individuals and corporations are needed to sponsor the campaign's
public
outreach activities. www.newmoon.org/TBIOD/sponsor.htm Email
nancyg@n... for details and to suggest people we can approach.

Celebrate on May 16
See how others are celebrating at
http://www.newmoon.org/TBIOD/what_people_are_doing.htm
Girls Inc. in Sioux City, IA. Are holding classes during the month of
May.
The 8 week session of classes for 10-11 year old is focusing on Inner
vs.
Outer beauty. They will be taking a values survey and completing a
body
image checklist. Then the girls will examine advertisements from
magazines
and analyze the magazine's message about body image. The girls will
write
some letters to the editors, etc. They will use the Internet, several
websites have projects where they put ads online to analyze regarding
body
image and society's portrayal of beauty. The girls will also examine
their
own personal inner beauty and will be involved in several related
activities. They will talk about eating disorders and other ways women
hurt
themselves trying to achieve what they believe is the "perfect body."

We'd love to hear what you are doing in your community. Email
lindae@n... and tell her what you're planning.
