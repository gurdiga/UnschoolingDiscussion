Hi Sandra,

I'm glad you decided not to do the show. I've only watched a time or two, but in the show I saw, the visiting mom really connected with the kids, and when the real mom came back she was devistated.

I was trying to think of how you would be able to show unschooling - or how the mom visiting your family could possibly understand. Through my many years, even with people that know us, and the kids, they still can't believe that the kids make their own decisions. If there were some kind of discussion about your background, or beliefs, or something - but what I saw was alot of torment and drama. And it seems to me it is that kind of behaviour that we have really chosen to stay away from, when the family is flowing, communicating and being respectful of each other.

Wouldn't it be grand if there were a series of shows that would tape your own family, and let you answer and explain what you are thinking and how it all works?
