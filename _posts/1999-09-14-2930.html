Hello all. This week I am walking to work in the mornings. My car needs a
new clutch. The past two mornings, the kids have wanted to come with me, so
I brought them along to the church and put them to work. After work on
Monday I took them to the library and we hung out there for quite a while.
Today, I left for work ahead of them, and told them to follow at their own
pace. I gave them a scavenger hunt list of things to look for along the way.
This way I got about 1/2 hour of work in before they got there, and I felt
it was somewhat educational in the sense that they had to read, observe, and
work together as a team. Okay, okay.... so maybe I'm stretching it just a
bit. At least we're getting our exercise!
