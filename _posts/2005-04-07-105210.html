As copyrights of books lapse or expire, their content becomes part of
the public domain. These books can be published by anyone without
having to pay royalties to the author or an estate.

Over 15,000 works have been digitized, and they add about 400 every
month. I purchased several of the available titles before I found
this resource. Instead of buying these from Barnes & Noble or Amazon,
you can download them for free from this site:

www.gutenberg.net
