Hi!! Thought maybe some of you would like to know that adding essential
oils to the vinegar can change the smell AND kill germs at the same
time. So. you can change the scent to suit you. Lavender is nice as
well as antibacterial, antiviral,etc...also very calming- nice to use on
sheets after colds or for little ones having a time falling asleep.

Or you can put a few drops of the oil of choice on a small cloth and
throw it in the dryer with your clothes.

Have fun--Sharon :)
