My son dived right into history when he began playing the Age of Empires
PC game. He is now an expert in medieval weaponry and the battle
strategies of Alexander -- just to name a couple of things we were
discussing this evening. We have a gazillion games around here and that

but it's a bit tedious without the "cheats."

We also are looking into the Society for Creative Anachronism. We
haven't joined, per se, but we've been to some of their events and
really enjoyed those.
