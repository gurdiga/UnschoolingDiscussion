Lisa H at 2006-01-27 15:42:14 +0200 said:

Arlynn,
If you are in the lower hudson valley vicinity contact me off list. I'll connect you with unschooling families (many with younger siblings) so you and your kids can start right now being a part of the local community. It was important for me when my oldest was around your kids ages to make the homeschooling community our main group. It was then transparent to my dd when other 5yo's were going to Kindergarten...she never felt like she missed out on a thing. The unschooling/homeschooling world of living and learning together was her "mainstream."
