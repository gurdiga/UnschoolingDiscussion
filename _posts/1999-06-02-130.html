STAY IN SCHOOL- OR IT'LL COST YOU
A town in the Philadelphia suburbs begins enforcing a daylight curfew
for children and teenagers. In Bensalem, PA parents of children found on the
streets during school hours can be fined $300 dollars. Those being
homeschooled must have proof with them.
