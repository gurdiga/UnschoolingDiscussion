My name is Elizabeth. We've been home schooling our children for 5 years. This
year we enrolled them in Einstein Academy, and it was a disaster.

Before considering The Grace Academy, you should know that Mimi and Howie are
Einstein's founders. Their company, Tutorbots, mismanaged the school
until about
three weeks ago. In her dealings with Einstein families, Mimi used the name
Mimi Rothschild.

IEP's were not done for the 300 special ed children who were enrolled in the
school. It is now the end of April and only nine IEP's have been written.

"I have never seen a more substantive fundamental violation of
special education
by a local education agency," charged Jane Sullivan, special education adviser
for the education department, who testified against Einstein Academy. Courier
Times 3/20/02

The curriculum was not what Einstein promised. One class didn't have a teacher
until early Dec - then he left the school in February, and the class
is teacherless
again.

Einstein has been sued by many PA school districts and the PA Department of
Education. It is only open now through a court-brokered agreement
which required:
eliminating Tutorbots as manager and financial manager of the school, forensic
accounting of payments to Tutorbots, approval of all payments by a comptroller
who reports to the chartering school district and strict oversight by
the chartering
school district.

Here are excerpts from articles about Tutorbots and its mismanagement
of Einstein:
