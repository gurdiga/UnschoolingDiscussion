Jennifer,

A few summers ago, when I was visiting there in Houston, my mom, myself and my 2 children rented an RV for about a week. The kids didn't wear seatbelts. They mostly sat at the table playing cards or games. We didn't travel as far as you would be. If I remember correctly it cost around $1200 for the week. I don't know if that is good or bad but we had fun. I was a little nervous driving it but I got used to it.
