Here is a site by the Virginia DOE: http://www.pen.k12.va.us/VDOE/Instruction/wmstds/home.shtml

My DD went through the same thing. She wanted to make sure she was learning enough. So I gave her a test and told her if she felt that she really had to know she could take the test, but that she should trust herself. Anyway, she never took the test as far as I know.
