<<Do you make goat cheese?>>

I have a recipe for ricotta that I just love. Takes about 15 minutes to
make and good enough to eat with a spoon. We also make goat yogurt (which
makes a fantastic strawberry smoothie) and we like the milk just raw and
plain as well.

We also raise rabbits and use the meat. Tastes like the sweetest chicken
you ever had. I tried to tan the hides this time but I didn't get the salt
on thick enough to ward off the flies. Live and learn. We just got our
baby chicks so we are trying those for the first time this year.
