Hi -
I called the local County Board of Education (Sacramento, CA) to
request the R-4 private school affadavit that will allow us to
legally (as my research indicates) unschool without being enrolled at
a charter/independent school. The first person I spoke with
didn't ask any questions and said she would send the forms out. Then
an hour or so later I get a voice mail message from another person at
that office saying that she wants to talk to me to be sure I am aware
of the LEGAL options before I tried homeschooling via the R-4. I
didn't even say anything about home schooling when I requested the
forms! Does this sound like intimidation or am I just being paranoid?

I guess I could just ignore her message (she said the forms would be
sent out), but it kind of chaps my hide that she was so assumptious
(word?) and intimidating. I'd kind of like to call her back and let
her know that I know my legal rights!

Carla
