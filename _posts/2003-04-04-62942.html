I asked my son to comment and he just said "I don't know Mom it was all
great." For me it brought tears to my eyes to see other children like mine.
They fit in so easily and talked with each other and instantly started
playing with no gender or age separation. The adults all treated the
children with such respect. Not just their own children but all the
children. The children had freedom. Parents watching but not interfering.
Just allowing their children to explore. It was really great for my children
and when you see a room full of unschooled children you can really see how
they are different. Confident, with ideas and thoughts, questions and
answers.
Great for my boys.
Pam G.
