For the information of others who may want to go check out the new
discussion boards at <www.unschooling.info/forum>, there was, I am
happy to report, no technical glitch in the software, it worked
perfectly. The person posting the complaint simply forgot what she'd
put in as her userid and was trying to use the wrong one.

That said, sometimes technical problems DO happen and I'm sure some
will happen to us at some time. I hope people will understand that the
message boards, like this list, are a cooperative project of a group of
unschooling moms and that we're doing this as a public service, not
being paid. Even creating the message boards was quite a challenge,
none of us had ever done it before and had to learn a lot to accomplish
it.

So, support our efforts by joining the message boards and
participating. As great as this list is, message boards have some real
advantages over lists.

Instead of all messages coming into your mailbox, in the order posted,
they are all nice and organized for you, by topic. Since all posts on a
topic are together, it makes for far more coherent reading. The message
boards are also far more easily searchable (have you tried to use the
search function in yahoogroups to find some old post from this list?).
You can go back and edit your own posts, after you've posted them. You
can start your own topics. You can even "subscribe" to be notified
when there are new posts on the message boards, either any posts at
all, or posts in particular topics of your choosing.

So - come and join us at <www.unschooling.info/forum>.

-pam
