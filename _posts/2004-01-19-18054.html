I knew this knowledge would come in handy some day! I had a friend in high
school who often dyed her hair with Kool-Aid. She used two to three packets
(depending on how dark she wanted it) of the unsweetened Kool-Aid powder.
She dissolved it in 2 quarts of warm vinegar. Warm the hair first by taking
a warm shower (opens up the hair to receive the dye) then soak the parts of
the hair you want dyed for about 15-20 minutes. If you want all of it dyed
with no roots showing fill a bowl with the mixture and tip your head back
into it. The color lasted for about three weeks. I think a color like red
or purple would look great on top of already darkened (i.e. with henna)
hair.

As for the non-toxic part, I am not sure how I would classify the coloring
agents in Kool-Aid FD &C #6 etc. but at least you know they are safe enough
to eat! ;)

Good luck, let us know what happens!
