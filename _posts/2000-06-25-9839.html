I would like to add also that all of us are different,thank Goodness
:)We will have varying opinions,very diverse thoughts ..When you get a
bunch of woman together we are not all going to see things the same
way.Yes we do differ on things,which I think is healthy.Even though we
are all unschoolers we still are unique individuals with our own
brains,and we are going to have our own thoughts and opinionns on
matters.
I don't think it is good to agree with something or someone just to keep
peace.Thats not healthy!
I don't believe in bashing anyone if their opinion differs from mine,and
that is not a good thing,but for the most part I think we haven't done
that.We are all entitled to have our say .I am glad everyone is
different,this world would be a very boring place!I like diversity :)
Have a great day everyone...Deb
