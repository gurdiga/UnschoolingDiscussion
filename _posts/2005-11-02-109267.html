Wed like to invite unschoolers to join our new group @
_http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UnschoolingFOTR/_ (http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UnschoolingFOTR/)

Unschooling Families on the Road is for families that work/live/play/learn
while on the road, whether they're full time, part time, or those that wanna
be. We're hoping this can be a forum for unschoolers to discuss topics related
to traveling, cool sites to visit, finding employment on the road, sharing
cool stories and even to make real life connections.
We would also like to welcome unschooling families who are not on the road
but would like our traveling members to stop by for a visit. A network of
like minded families and a touch of home mean so much when familiar faces are
scarce.
Thanks! I hope this was appropriate to post this here. If not, I apologize
in advance. :)
