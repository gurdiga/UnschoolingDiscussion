Good luck with your new business.... Ultimately Steve will have his own
business where he works part time and I work part time. That way we can both
be with them. I did have to take a leap of faith. The other alternative was
to wait for the "safe" thing to happen. Sometimes I have had to create that
space to allow the good things to come along. At the beginning of the week
after writing about 7 preschools that my son had been too, the light bulb
went back on that he isn't meant to go there and to just jump in and do this.
We unschooled for 6 months last year, but I think I did it half heartedly
and hadn't completely changed my priorities. This time I scaled back my
business to only weekends (apart from the radio shows which are usually at
5am in the morning, when kidlets are asleep) and started to look at the
lifestyle hubby and I want to have and how we can craft businesses around
that.

It still takes faith and it sounds like that's what you have right now...
Good luck with your venture. By the way, what is it? Tell everyone what you
are doing because you never know who might need your services and where you
are going to meet those people.
