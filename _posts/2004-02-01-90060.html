The first time Marty talked to me about Harvest Moon I was surprised that
kids would be excited about a game where you have to remember to water the corn
and feed the chickens. But he and his loudest most violent friend were way
into it and still talk about it years later.

There's a fair amount of reading, some of which can be bypassed, and which is
repetitive and simple, so it really helped both Marty and then Holly in turn
to really need and use reading. Holly is right this moment playing a spring
season on the Super Nintendo version, humming along with the music. Her
character has gotten the second house expansion and has a pregnant wife. Good
going! <g>
