> Date: Fri, 12 May 2000 20:59:11 -0700 (PDT)
> From: dawn <dawn@...>
>Subject: Re: Notification
>Since you can't be held to what you include in your notification, what a
>lot of people I know do is to print out the World Book scope and sequence
>from their web site and include that as the "intended curriculum" and then
>go through their house and community and list resources they might use.


This is exactly what I did for our first year. I said we would refer to
this (and some other curriculum-sounding materials) as a "framework" for my
son's "independent studies", and included in the ending paragraph that this
course of study "will be subject to modifications" as new ideas and
interests are explored. Then I listed all the software, books, games, etc.
that we have available, either at home or in the community, by subject. Of
course, after all that stress of preparing this letter, the school system
returned a form to fill out, asking me to repeat what I had just said in
the letter, and requiring my signature. Sheesh! I am writing a lot less for
next year.

Olivia
