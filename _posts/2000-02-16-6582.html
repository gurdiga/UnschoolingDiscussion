I had recieved a brochure on the Earth Box a couple of months ago. It is simply a self-contained "garden" that uses fertilizer, potting soil, and a mulch cover. My interest is more in the self watering feature, for last year was very dry and even with watering some of our plants still died. I have found a little info on some of the garden searches but have yet to find anyone who has actually used one. They look like a wonderful concept but with shipping run about 36.00 (us dollars) each.
They do have a toll-free 800 number listed under Earth Box or if anyone needs the addy, feel free to e-mail me and I will gladly respond.
