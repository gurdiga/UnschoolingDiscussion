Dawn wrote:
"the whole issue of "coloring" really amuses me. My boyz HATE to color"

This is one of my pet peeves as well. My second child (who went to ps) was
quite the artist when he was younger - may be still, but doesn't do much
with it now - but when he was in 6th grade and even some of the younger
grades, whenever he did an assignment and drew a picture he got a lower
grade because he just used pencil or pen and ink and basically refused to
add color to his pictures.

I finally went to his art teacher and explained his situation and she looked
at his work and said he did such a good job with shading, etc. that he
didn't have to add color - duh! You can buy pictures like his and people
don't expect the artists to add color - why do teachers think it's
important??? Everyone sees things differently and coloring is not an art
form for everyone.

The more I remember about ps with the older kids the happier I am I pulled
my youngest.
