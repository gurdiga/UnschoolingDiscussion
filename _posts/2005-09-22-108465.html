This may have already been discussed, but this helped us this past year.
Occasionally and privately, I asked my two sons, then 16 and 12, if they would go separately to their rooms for a few minutes (20max) quiet time.
That gave the house that quiet feel and my then 8yo daughter would go to sleep. She loves being with her brothers and is always afraid that she will miss something. The 12 yo would sometimes go to sleep too : )
The oldest, who is a night owl and always has been, didn't.
If they both decided to do something together after she was asleep, they would keep the noise down. Painting warhammer figures in the basement is one of their favorite activities.
If the big kids in your house would appreciate some time without the little ones around, they might been really happy to do this, both for you and for their own reasons : )

I should point our that this was usually around midnight.


Beth in Maryland (with 3 kids sleepin' in)
