I just saw what I originally thought was a very cool commercial on Cartoon
Network. It begins: "The day is coming, when your parents will FORCE you to go
back to school, face the dangers of the school bus and avoid the dangerous
cheerleaders" ... (a blurb about a *fake* insurance policy) then, "join us for
Don't Go Back to School Day" a bunch of cartoons showing the reasons NOT to go
to school will be shown on Monday. Ending with a silly disclaimer stating
there is no such insurance policy and "kids, you need to go to school if you don't
want to end up a bum or in jail" AARRGGHH! We will definitely watch the
cartoons, as it is funny to see the cartoon horrors of school, but I'm really sad
the channel had to end it with *go to school anyway* propaganda ~ could they
at least proclaim their envy of those who DON'T have to go back to school???

diana,

crime against the human mind and spirit.  Let all those escape it who can, any
way they can."  John Holt
