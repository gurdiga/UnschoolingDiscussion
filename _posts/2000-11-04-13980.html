Instead of soccer camp, why not send your kids to "Action Camp" next year?
They'll learn important skills which will serve them well throughout life.

Better yet, why not make it a family vacation?

>>Since Ruckus was founded in 1995, thousands of activists have attended our
week-long training programs throughout North America. We've shared skills

World Report call it "Basic Training for Tree Huggers". The New York Times
asked, "Can Green Rads Be Tamed?" and a spokesperson for B.C. lumber
interests stated, "it's just insanity." Flattering praise indeed.<<
