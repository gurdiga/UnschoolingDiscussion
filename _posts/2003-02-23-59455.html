**Real quick question,

Jaiden turned 5 yesterday, but I don't have to file the r4 until he's 6
right?
**

Hi, Rebecca --

In California, kids must go to school for 1st grade if they will 6 by
December 2nd of this calendar year. Kindergarden is not mandatory in
CA, although some schools are trying to make kids go to K before
attending 1st.

I think the R-4 form, also called a Private School Affadavit, only
exists in that exact form, in California. The time to file each year is
after the school year starts, between Oct 1st and 15th.

Laws in each state in the U.S. are different.

Detailed California legal information about HS can be found at:

http://www.hsc.org/legal

Betsy
