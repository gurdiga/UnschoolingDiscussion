Both my teens, Nell 16 and Sky 19, went two summers in a row to NBTSC in
Oregon. It was an inspiring and enriching time for them. . . opening up
their hearts and minds a great deal. Mostly for them it was an affirmation
that the way they learn and view the world is shared by other teens, which
is surely not the case here on Kauai, where they are the only unschooled
teens we know. They both made some deep personal shifts and some great
friends.

I don't have children that have attended, but several friends have and
their kids LOVED it.
Cameron Lovejoy is going again this year, and will tell you all sorts
of wonderful things about camp. In fact, he was sharing something he
picked up at camp during conference this year...hug buttons!:)
He drew hug buttons on himself, or on anyone that asked for one. When
you push the hug button you get a hug of course.

Elissa's son Zack has gone before and enjoyed it. Oh, and Mary Gold's
son Connor is going this year. I encouraged my son to check it out,
but he's reluctant to fly on his own still...so maybe another year.

There's a kids discussion about camp over at unschooling.info.
