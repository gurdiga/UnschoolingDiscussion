Hi Folks,

Our 6 3/4 year old son sleeps with us. He does have his own room with
his own bed. We spent our first night away from him this last
weekend. He slept with his cousins, which he practiced a couple of
weeks ago on New Years weekend
when we were there also. People kept saying that it will be easier for
him to not sleep with you if he starts sleeping on his own at home.
This issue wasn't him not sleeping with us, the issue was for him to be
able to sleep with others such as cousins or friends when they come over
to spend the night. He had an okay night, cried a bit and worried that
we wouldn't be back. His uncle listened and talked to him, said he
bored him to sleep. Our son said he's willing to do it again. His next
chance will be five days with grandparents and two other cousins on a
trip this summer. We'll celebrate our 25th wedding anniversary then.

Blessings,
Sherry
