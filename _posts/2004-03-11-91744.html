When I was at University of Delaware, an astronomy professor had put
together a scale model of the solar system. The domed building at the
center of campus was the sun and then you had to follow the directions to
find all of the planets starting with mercury being a smallish picture
posted on the lawn out front. By the time you got to Pluto you had to get
in your car and drive way out to the other side of town to find it. What
fun!

I see, on their web site that they've since made it a permanent
installation. http://www.udel.edu/PR/UpDate/00/21/campus1.html

Krisula
