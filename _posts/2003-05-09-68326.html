Are you willing to take the challenge?
Each week an American family will travel to a remote
location outside of the United States. Forget the
creature comforts of home and prepare to embrace
another way of life.

http://www.nationalgeographic.com/channel/highlights/worldsapart.html

Is your family outgoing with lots of personality and
ready for an adventure?
If so, you need to meet the following criteria:

1. Have children (ages ranging from 6 to 18) who are
able to travel.
2. Be available to travel for two weeks.
3. Be in good overall health and have health
insurance.

Here's how to apply:
Tell us why we should choose your family.

1. In a home video tape no more than 20 minutes long,
take us on a tour of your lives. Who are you and what
makes your family special?

2. Please print this page, fill out the following
information, and send it along with your VHS or DV
tape to:
