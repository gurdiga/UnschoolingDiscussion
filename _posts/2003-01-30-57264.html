I have dreaded my friends hair many times. Some of it has to do with the
"nappy"ness of the hair ( for lack of a better term). Her hair is softer and
not as tight so hers loosened up at night at first. Also if it is shorter it
tends to loosen up more than if it is longer. You just need to keep twisting
it each day for a few days to keep it dreaded. Also don't wash it for a
while. The washing it will make it looser as well. After a while it will
stay just fine.
Pam G.
