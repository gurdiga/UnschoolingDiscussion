Hi, I'm Kara Bauer. I'm a sah, bf'ing, ap'ing, hs'ing momma to Drew
(2.5+) and Derek (15 mon)- they are the light of my life.
We have only recently found unschooling. I knew I wanted to
homeschool, but we meet other homeschoolers who were unschooling, and
at first I didn't believe. But then everytime I tried to teach Drew
something, he would balk, and fight it with everything he had and we
would both end up frusterated. So then we started just letting him do
what he wanted and he is learning all kinds of new things. Now Derek
is starting to pick up books and tell me what the objects are and I
just sit back in amazement!
It's nice to meet you all,
Kara
