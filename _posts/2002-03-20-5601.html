An SCA guy in North Carolina has written up something about the benefits of
the SCA for his homeschooled kids.

Home Schooling

Here at St. Columban's College Preparatory Academy, we have been treating the
SCA as a big unit study, integrating books and art and dress-up on medieval
themes, Arthurian and Celtic legends, etc.  
But, unit studies are not meant to go on for years.   My wife and I were
talking and we realized we had no clear idea of what we wanted from the SCA
when it came to home schooling. So we wrote out this mission statement for
using "living history" in our home school.

http://www.charlesfleming-sca.com/ (and when you get there and click on
"mission statement" you get his long details about the benefits of the SCA,
and it mentions unschooling.

http://www.charlesfleming-sca.com/SCA/homeschool%20mission.htm


(The link was sent to me by a friend whose kids are totally schooled.)
