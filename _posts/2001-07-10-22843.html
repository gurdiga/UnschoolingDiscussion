If you don't read Norwegian, there's a website translator at:

http://www.tranexp.com:2000/InterTran?

Give it the URL of the page, pick your from and to languages at the bottom
and hit translate. (Which I think is way cool :-)

It takes a while for the page to load and a while for the translation to
appear so you might need a bit of patience. The output is interesting if not
exactly fine English. ;-)
