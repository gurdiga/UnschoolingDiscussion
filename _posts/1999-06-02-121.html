I don't know if you know about this feature, but at Onelist they have shared
files. On my own list I run for parents who are raising angry children, we
all post our bios there. It's easy to do. You can write out your bio on
your notepad because that is a program everyone can open, go to the shared
files area of the list, and follow directions to upload it.

It's just an idea but it's done on my list plus two more that I'm on and it
works. Another thing you can put on the list is pictures of you and your
children.

My own problem with this is that even if the list is closed and restricted,
which it is not, people can still lie to get on the list and will have
access to the bios. I don't want anyone knowing where I live and who my
children are.

The best thing to do if you choose this forum, I believe, is to post your
bio but leave out last names and towns. Just first names and the state
should keep you safe enough.
