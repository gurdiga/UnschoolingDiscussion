Hello everyone,

I just joined this list - and although I'm *sure* there are people I know
here, I'm sending my intro too soon to know for sure...

My name is Paula Long, and I live in Dickinson, Texas, which is just a tad
south of Houston and the Clear Lake (NASA) area, with my husband Scott. We
have a daughter Alexandria, who turned 4 in November. We also have a son,
Kadin, who was born still July of 1998.

We parent in the style of attachment parenting, and I've known I would
homeschool since before I ever had children. It wasn't until about 3.5
years ago that I learned of unschooling - and I knew the moment I heard of
it that that would be perfect for our family as it's just an extention of
what we were doing already.

I recently became a member of a Houston unschooling group, but I wanted to
join together with more unschooling families as we continue our journey.

~Paula
