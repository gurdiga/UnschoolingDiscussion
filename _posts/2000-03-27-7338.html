Hi,
I also have small children. A 4 and 6 year old and a 20 month old. We are
expecting our fourth this June.
We made a decision to turn our cable off so that television is no longer an
option for the children. We go to the library once a week and they are
allowed one fun movie and the rest need to be educational.

What a change it has been in our house - so peaceful, no tv battles and they
truly don't even mention watching any of their old shows (I was very
selective and limited TV, but I still always felt as if it was too much).

Plus, now my husband and I get to actually talk at night since he isn't glued
to the TV:)

As far as daily unschooling - I do put some things on the kitchen table every
day and let them help themselves at leisure. Today I didn't as we are
getting ready to move and at dinner, they both had some "learning" books out
and were busy with them.

I will also ask them for suggestions throughout the day and when they have
one - I jump at the opportunity.

Warm wishes,
Tonya in Mass
