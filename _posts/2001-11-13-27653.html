Snow things: Igloos were one of my favorites, SNOW ICE CREAM . (ingredients: Snow, a little sugar, some vanilla or other flavor. Mix and eat.)
SNOW ANGELS, Dog sled? (all you need is the dog and makeshift harness, the fun is hooking the dog up, not if it works or not.)
King of the mountain (you just need a pile, smaller kids only need a small pile, the object is to land in the fluffy snow)
And if everyone is gathered inside around the stove, anyway, you might as well make some candy.

Most of my memories of snow were : Stomping around collecting maple sap, cutting firewood, feeding pigs, shoveling snow so the milk truck could get in the driveway, cleaning out the manure spreader which broke because it was frozen, cutting more firewood. All the 'fun' farm things.
