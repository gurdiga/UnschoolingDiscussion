> > How can I seperate my emails into different folders? This would be
useful
> inorder to keep personal and unschooling seperate.

It depends on what you're using for email. If you use Outlook Express,
right click on Local Folders and then click new folder. Make a folder for
each category you want. I make one for each list I'm on. Then click Tools,
Message Rules, and then Mail. Now you can set up your filters. Click New.
In #1, click where subject line contains specific words. In #2 click move
to specified folder. In #3 you click on the underlined words to edit them.
So for specified words, you'd click that and then type in
Unschooling-dotcom. Then click specified and choose which folder it will go
to.
Hope this helps.
Also, it would help everyone else if you delete everything but the specific
words you want to quote in your reply. (You left in all the yahoo baloney,
which we had to scroll past in order to get to your question.
