Pam Sorooshian set this board up. There are others involved in the larger
project, but the message board set-up is really new! So far it's not many
people, but it only opened a day ago! <g>

======================


The Center for Unschooling Information, as its very first project, has opened
new message boards at: <http://www.unschooling.info/forum>

We are excited to announce the creation of these new boards, which are
devoted to discussion of anything and everything related to unschooling.

Discussions will include such topics as: Getting Started Unschooling,
Deschooling, Supporting Kids' Endeavors, Finding Support, and much, much more. These
boards are easy to search and are conveniently organized to allow on-topic,
in-depth discussion.

You may be wary about utilizing message boards. But, these boards are loaded
with features that make navigation a breeze. For instance, you can sign up to
get new messages sent to your e-mail address. You can edit or delete your own
posts. You can add a hyperlink to your posts, track all activity since your
last visit, and search posts for specific topics. There is even a FAQ to help
you learn about all the forum features and how to use them: <
http://www.unschooling.info/forum/faq.asp#format>

Please join this new online community at: <http://www.unschooling.info/forum>

The Center for Unschooling Information
<www.unschooling.info>
