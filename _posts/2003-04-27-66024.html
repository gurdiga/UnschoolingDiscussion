Olga,
David Albert's two books are great. I just finished the new one.
They are: And the skylark sings with me and Homeschooling and the
Voyage of Self Discovery. I also liked Nancy Wallace's books though
it's been a while and I'm not sure if they are
completely "unschooly". They are: Better Than School and Child's
Work. Might have to check the library for them. Grace Lewellyn's
Real Lives gave me a hint of what to expect when the kids are older.
