Keith's been doing a lot of woodworking lately. Medieval boxes and
furniture. He's just new at it. He says some lumber is by the foot even at
Home Depot. Maple, red oak, white oak. Hardwood is really rare where we
are. There's a lumber place, local, called Paxton's that sells exotic
hardwoods by the piece, and less exotic stuff by the foot. Some of the
pieces are pretty small, but people are doing small projects with them too!

Mostly Keith's working with 1x10 or 2x8 or thereabout, and usually pine, and
that stuff's sold in 6', 8' or 10' boards and he buys the whole board. And
takes it apart with his table saw.
