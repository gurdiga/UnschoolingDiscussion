We here in NE are supposed to give either the name of a major publisher if we are using the whole curriculum (i.e. Abeka), or else give an outline of every book for every subject.  HA!  There is no way I am going to tie myself to that nonsense, so I list things like "self-directed research in various aspects of biological science" (we dissect body organs after butchering) and "student guided reading including biographies, local and American history".  I guess they are impressed with my outline because in four years we have never even been questioned.
