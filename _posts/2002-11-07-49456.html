1.  We believe that some parents are called by God to educate their own
children at home, while other are called by God to send their children to
private or public schools.

2.We believe that all ministries done in the name and service of Jesus Christ
are equally important in the sight of God.

3.  We believe that he best education encompasses a balance of training in
academics, in developing a strong healthy body, in cultivating a deep and
personal relationship to Christ, and in learning to love and serve others.

4.  To share knowledge concerning how to best educate our children according
to scriptural principles and the latest and best research concerning child
development and education practices.
