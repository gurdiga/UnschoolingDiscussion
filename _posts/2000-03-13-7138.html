Dear Friends,

This is the premiere (below) of a new weekly online newsbrief from the
publishers of Home Education Magazine. This newsbrief is specifically
produced for homeschooling support group leaders, newsletter editors,
website managers and others who work with homeschoolers in an information
sharing or networking capacity. Our goal is to provide a reliable source of
timely news, press releases, announcements, resources and encouragement for
the people who work for homeschooling families.

colleagues who may be interested.
