I'm pretty new here, but I thought I would share an
anecdote...

When my sister was about 10 or 11, she started
toreally gain some weight. She was eating a *lot* of
junk, especially fast food and it was getting to the
point where even my grandmother was starting to say
"maybe you shouldn't have that second burger,
sweetie". She was starting to look downright
chunky...

Long story short, over the next three months she grew
about 6 inches. She was *definately* not chunky after
that. As a matter of fact she was painfully thin
looking for several years. She probably could have
standed to eat even more during that time when her
body was getting ready to grow.

I think generally, the body knows what it needs, if we
are able to listen.
