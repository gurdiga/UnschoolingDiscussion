I loved reading about your home. About a dozen years ago, when my family

bedroom. My neighbors couldn't belive the lay-out, but their kids loved to
play in our space.

Trevor's, and life has changed drastically. Kids grow up and leave home, but
memories of really LIVING in the home are forever!
