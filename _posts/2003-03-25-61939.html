I am a lurker but just had to pop in and share my favorite way to keep a 3 y/o
occupied and happy while Mom is sick. Have her rub lotion on your legs, arms,
back and hands. A child that age usually loves the tactile aspect of it and it
feels good for Mom, too. <g> You can even let her draw pictures with the
lotion on your back while you try to guess what she is drawing. It is best if
you have lotion in a pump type bottle and keep a towel handy.
