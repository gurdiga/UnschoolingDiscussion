As I was browsing the 261 yahoogroups under "Waldorf",
I came across the following that I wanted to share
with this group. Perhaps these will be of interest to
some.

Waldorf-Recoverygroup
...for those experiencing post traumatic stress...

Waldorf-Survivors-Only
...support group for those who have had strongly
negative experiences with Waldorf schools...

Waldorf_Craft

...to discuss the Waldorf style of craft- doll and toy
making, felting, knitting etc.

JewishWaldorf

...discussion of issues facing Jews who choose to do
Waldorf education...the potentials and difficulties of
combining Waldorf and Judaism...

Anthroposophyparent

...caring for the home and family in the light of
anthroposophy...topics may include: homemaking,
festival life, health, nutrition, care of children,
family relations etc.

Lastly, and this was the one I was *really* looking
for:

Waldorfunschool

...a forum to discuss Waldorf Unschooling.

Unfortunately there are only 8 members to this group,
so I don't know how useful it really would be.

-Jennefer
