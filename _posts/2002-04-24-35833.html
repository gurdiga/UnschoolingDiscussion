Handwriting:
I learned with "ball-stick-bird". Where every part of a letter is a circle
(or part of one), a line, or a v thingy. It is pretty clear - and it groups
similar shape letters together, so it's easier to learn. My Mom is an
elementary school teacher, and taught me that way. I'm pretty sure they
have a website; I saw it advertized someplace(maybe in HEM). Now, while I
know *how* to write neatly, this is not to say that I necessarily bother to!
