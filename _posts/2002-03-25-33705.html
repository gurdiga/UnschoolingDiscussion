...from my own personal unschooling guru. :o)

I'm in the process of planning a trip down to NM and AZ (to visit people on
this list!). I was all set to go in April when I realized that I had signed
Conor up for a class on designing web pages during that time. (Conor has
been wanting to learn. He liked the class idea BTW)

I sent a frantic email to Jon (my dh) to solicit his advice. Would Conor
freak out if he got behind in the class, should I postpone or maybe cancel a
really cool trip, should we talk to the reacher and find out what he will
miss....? Obviously I should talk to Conor, but what did he think???!!!

This is the wonderfully calming wise note I received back.

>>He can always take another web design class. In fact it is assured that he
>>will be taking another one if he has any interest anyway, because it's not
>>like one little class is going to teach him anything beyond the barest
>>basics.

I wouldn't sweat it about missing one, even though we spent 8 million
dollars on it...>>

Ahhh, my sweet and wonderful high-school drop-out hubby. It just comes so
naturally for him.

Life is good.
~Mary
