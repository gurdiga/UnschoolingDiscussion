2001 California Home=Education Conference
August 17-19, 2001

The 2001 California Home=Education Conference will be held at the Radisson in
Sacramento, CA on August 17-19, 2001. The conference, in its eleventh year,
is presented by the HomeSchool Association of California, a grassroots,
nonprofit, volunteer-driven organization. The conference is a phenomenal
source of information for new and seasoned homeschoolers and for those
looking into homeschooling as an educational alternative for their family.
The conference attracts over 1,000 attendees.

For complete registration information, contact Pamela Mosher at (530)
758-0675 or at contact@.... You can also visit the
conference website at www.HomeEdConference.org.

Keynote speakers include Linda Dobson, author of Homeschoolers' Success
Stories, The Homeschooling Book of Answers, Homeschooling: The Early Years,
Art of Education, and Pat Montgomery with Clonlara School. Pat Farenga of
Growing Without Schooling will serve as emcee.

Featured and workshops presenters include David Albert, author of And The
Skylark Sings With Me; Cecile Andrews, author of Circle of Simplicity; Don
Asher, author of Cool Colleges for the Hyper-Sensitive, Self-Directed, Late
Blooming, and Just Plain Different; Mary Griffith, author of The
Homeschooling Handbook and The Unschooling Handbook; Marty Layne, author of
Learning at Home; and Laurel Schmidt, author of Seven Times Smarter; Sandra
Dodd of Radical Unschooling; Richard Prystowsky, editor of Paths of Learning;
Paula Harper-Christensen; EQUALS Family Math; and much more.

The conference also offers a program for teens and children programs, a
Recycled Resource Room, a vendor hall, and a full slate of programs and
presentations for parents throughout the weekend.
