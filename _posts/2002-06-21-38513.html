Lots of good recommendations on constipation - it seems to be a
common thing for little girls. When we were dealing with it with
madam there were 2 things that really worked well. First, water
matters more than fiber assuming a balanced (though bird like)
diet. Second, a simple reminder to go poop at roughly the same
time of day helps. Apparently the "poop now" signal gets ignored
or isn't recognized for some kids at that age. Too much other fun
stuff to do, LOL. I wasn't heavy handed about it, sort of a
"Sweetie, time to go brush hair and teeth and see if you need to
poop." A reminder was all that was needed usually.
