Arizona Republic : Home schooling Special Report

graphics:

http://www.azcentral.com:80/news/education/0701homeview.html
Article written by a 15 year old homeschooler.

http://www.azcentral.com:80/news/education/0701homeschool.html
"Arizona has no way to gauge the quality of home-schooling. Supporters, many
backed by conservative religious organizations, call that freedom; critics
call it irresponsibility."

http://www.azcentral.com:80/news/education/0701homeside.html

author of the first person article, "Why I like being homeschooled" above.
