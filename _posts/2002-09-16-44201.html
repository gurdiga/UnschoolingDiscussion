The Open University - now available in the US offers degrees you
can study from home, as well as Masters degrees. I started doing
a Masters but ran out of cash.
Doing an OU degree (they are very popular in the UK) means you
can take it as fast or as slowly as you want. Plus you have time to
be with your kids or work if you need. The OU also accept students
without formal qualifications like A level and whatever it is
Americans get at 18.
