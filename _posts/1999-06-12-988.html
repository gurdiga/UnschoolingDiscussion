After my last post there may be those on this list that
doubt my ability to teach algebra to my "so inclined"
children.

An amazing thing happened to me as an adult. I learned
algebra from a wacky lady on PBS, our local channel.
I tuned in for several weeks, nightly, and now I 'GET'
algebra...age 37 and still learning! Do I need it?
No. Do I want to learn it? Yes!

"And that has made all the difference in the world."

I want this for my kid's! I want them to want to know
whatever it is that make's a difference in their lives.

Weird to my folk's, but even Mom is asking question's.
Miracle's never cease!
Jackie
