Hello Homeschool Community!

Spread the news! Apple Computer, Inc. has opened it's Education
Store to Homeschools...

Apple is now offering homeschoolers full access to it's educational
products and solutions. As you may know Apple has provided
solutions to the education market for over two decades offering a
full range of computer hardware, peripherals, and educational
software solutions. This new offering marks the first availability
(for homeschoolers), to Apple's unique education solutions: Apple
Education Series (AES), educational software solutions; and Apple
Learning Interchange (ALI), Apple's online resource for teaching,
learning, research, and collaboration.

Homeschoolers who provide Apple verification of their homeschool may
purchase directly through the Apple Store for Education
(http://www.apple.com/education/store). To qualify homeschools must
provide information of their membership in a homeschool organization
who in turn will provide independent verification to Apple.
Alternatively, the homeschool may provide a confirming letter of
verification from a curriculum supplier or a local public or private
school.

For more information on Apple's homeschool offerings please visit us
on the internet: http://www.apple.com/education/k12/homeschool/ or
contact us directly at 1-800-780-5009. You may also email us:
homeschool@...

The Apple Education Team
