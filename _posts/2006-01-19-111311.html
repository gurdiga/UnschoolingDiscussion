Seeing Seamus' rendition of the Numa Numa song started a trend here at our
home :o)

My son first found the original video for the group. Does anyone know where
the group is from? I tried to find a fan page but no luck.

We started doing searches and have spent about 2 days off and on cracking up
at all the Numa Numa videos out there. Our favorites are Seamus', and the
one we got from limeware (I'd forward it but am not sure how to, but it's
titled "Numa Numa funny guys" (it's these 3 teenage boys who are singing it in
their kitchen, and their dad even makes a guest appearance...look like happy
kids!!).

And there's another with (I guess) a famous bad guy from a video game (I
think WarCraft?). It can be found here:


_Content Search_
(http://my.break.com/Search.aspx?SearchString=numa+numa&SearchType=Main&SEARCH1=SEARCH+SITE)

so if that offends you just watch the "numa numa WOW style" (third one down)
There's the original at the bottom of the page the first guy to do a silly
video to it and a compilation with a bunch of them put together.

We've found a ton of them and had a good time...even my husband started
searching for them. His favorite is the WOW one :o)
