5 Jun 1999, at 12:20pm

Something cool happened today!

Okay, so I was boooooored. Really, really, really bored. I'm not often
bored, but I had nothing to do today. I was so bored that about an hour ago,
I decided to work on fractions with my old high school CD-ROM course.
Anyway, I started doing the problems, and about ten minutes into it I
realized that (for the first time I can remember) I was actually stopping to
figure out the problems in my head, instead of guessing or starting at the
screen in despair. So the whole thing turned into a game. I'd calculate and
convert the problems in my head, and then type them in..score!. I got most
of them right, and the ones I got wrong I could understand why. This is
really cool! I've never been able to understand fractions before. ~Eryn
