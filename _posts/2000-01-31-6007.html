Greetings,

I'm Laura from Massachusetts, mom of six (15,13,11,9,7 and 2). We've been
homeschooling for seven years. Eldest attends private high school and 9
year old began ps last year by choice. We've used Oak Meadow and other
resources over the years, quite loosely. We seem to slide more into
child-led learning as the years pass by.

For those interested in combining waldorf with homeschooling try the waldorf
homeschoolers list WE_HS@onelist.com. I believe that you can access many
waldorf links through this site.

Warmly,
Laura
