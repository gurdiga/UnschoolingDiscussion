I have two boys...so I have divided up chores and the use of the computer and
tv and the nintendo...based on odd and even days....

My older son was born in an even month...this way I don't have to think about
it to much...whose day it is...

Once they get chores done, whatever projects we are doing done...etc...then
they have free reighn on the tv, nintendo, or pc...cuz I only have one
tv...as long as I don't have anything that I want to watch...the tv and
nintendo are paired up...so if it is say Scotti's day for nintendo...but
Kevyn wants to watch tv...Kevyn has to wait until his day for the tv...the pc
and nintendo are on opposite days...so that one can be on each of them...

Once I set this down as rules...everything got much saner...and I don't have
a problem letting them know that they have got to stop the pc or nintendo
when there is something to do...or dinner to eat...or whatever...
