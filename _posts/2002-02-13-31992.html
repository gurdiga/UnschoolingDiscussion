8:02 PM on February 20 this year will be an historic moment in time. It will not be marked by the chiming of any clocks or the ringing of bells, but at that precise time, on that specific date and will never happen again.

As the clock ticks over from 8:01 pm on Wednesday, February 20, time
will, for sixty seconds only, read in perfect symmetry 2002, 2002, 2002,
or to be more precise - 20:02, 20/02, 2002.

The only other time the clock has been palindromic was at 10:01 AM on January 10, 1001, and it will never happen again.
