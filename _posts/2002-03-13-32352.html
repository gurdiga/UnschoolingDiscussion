I have a 14 year old boy who has always unschooled, but I can't really
help you with the regs in your state. We're with a public school program
in BC that basically leaves us alone. We have to report 3 times a year,
but I just write a general, anecdotal statement of what he's been
doing. The last report contained nothing "academic" at all. (Well, I did
mention that we had discussed current events some.) My kid is doing
"vocational exploration" this year. (Means he's been working with his dad
a lot) He's majored in P.E. since he was a little guy. Academics? No
way! At least not yet.
