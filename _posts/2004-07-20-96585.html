Being the innocent child that I am, and being raised and still living
in the country as I do, the image of shoes on the wire made me think
of a movie we have called 'Big Fish'. For those who haven't seen it,

to and finds the town of Spectra. In Spectra, there's a little girl
that takes people's shoes and throws them up on the wire so they
can't leave. Guess there's all sorts of reasons for having your
shoes on a wire!
