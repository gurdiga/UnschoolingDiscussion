LEARNapalooza Homeschool Conference
Memorial Weekend 2004
www.kclearn.org


Hyatt Regency Crown Center
Kansas City, MO
Memorial Day Weekend
May 28, 29, & 30th

Key Note presenters
Linda Dobson
John Taylor Gatto


Families from all of these states have registered so far......

Iowa, Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska, Ohio, Oklahoma, Wisconsin

We are offering an unschooling, veteran, new homeschooler, kids, teens and other tracks.

Here are some of the workshops being offered.....

Q & A Discussion on Unschooling
presenter - Victoria Moran

Creating Your Family's Personal Education Philosophy (PEP)
presenter - Linda Dobson

Breaking-the-Rules Homeschooling
presenter - Victoria Moran

The Curriculum of Power - PART ONE
presenter - John Gatto
Two Part Workshop

The Curriculum of Power - PART TWO
presenter - John Gatto

You want to do WHAT? - When One Parent Thinks Homeschooling isn't a Good Idea.
presenter - Matthew Westra

The Future is Now! - Homeschooling with Younger Teens
presenter - David Albert

"Creative" Recordkeeping
presenter - Jane Van Benthusen

Special Schooling for Special Kids
presenter - Kelly Midkiff

Homeschooled Kids in College
presenter - Matthew Westra

Homeschooling Styles
presenters - panel

The World as Your Classroom
presenter - panel presentation/Q&A

Beans and the Curriculum of Creamed Corn
presenter - David Albert

Raising Children Compassionately
presenter - Jean Ryan

Writing, Reading, and All That
presenter - David Albert

Deschooling Q&A
presenter - Linda Dobson

So Your Kid Wants to go to School
presenter - TBA

Translating Daily Life Into Recordable Hours
presenter - TBA

What About Socialization
presenter - Kriss Miller

The Proof is in the Pudding
presenter - Panel Discussion presented by unschooled teens

What is Dyslexia?
Presenter - Wesli Johnson


There is a bunch of cool stuff planned for all of the kids too. :) Check out the website www.kclearn.org and help us spread the word. Thanks, Jane :)
