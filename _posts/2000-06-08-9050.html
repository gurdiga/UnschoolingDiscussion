Why do you un/home school?  I un/homeschool because I want the best  for my child. I believe he is a unique, and wonderful individual who has the right to learn at his own pace, and cultivate the skills that are inherent in him naturally.  So that when he grows up he may choose a labour of love, that best suits him, and that he will consider to be his life work, rather than serve his community in a position that does not encourage his spirit to be whole.  The public school system does not offer the means to achieve this goal.  His emotional, spiritual, mental, and educational health depends on his freedom to choose his own path of learning, and I am here to provide him with the resources to cultivate his dreams, so he can make them come true(we can go on forever with this one , eh'?)

Why do you un/home school? Because I spent years doing curriculum with my
kids and that's just not how they learned. I finally realized that most of
what they were learning was when pursuing their own interests but that I
wasn't leaving much time for their own interests. What I was teaching
wasn't sticking so why waste my time?

Lisa:11, Karl:9 and Ben:4

restrictions at all.

ANy thing else you'd like to add: We are at the beginning of our unschooling
