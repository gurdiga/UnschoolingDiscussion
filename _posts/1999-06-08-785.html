Dear Friend,

As you read this, logging trucks are tearing
through our National Forests, clearcutting and
destroying our last ancient and wild forests.

Please take 30 seconds from your day and go to
the Web Site http://www.OurForests.org to send
a free e-postcard for the forests.

Heritage forests provide us with:
--Hiking, Camping and Fishing;
--Homes for Grizzly Bears & Bald Eagles;
--Clean Drinking Water;
--Most importantly, our forests are a priceless
legacy we must protect for future generations.

But our National Forest roadless areas are
threatened by logging, mining, and other
destructive activities.

The Clinton-Gore Administration is deciding
RIGHT NOW whether or not to allow logging and
mining to continue in America's last
unprotected wild forests.

If you'd like to help us save these majestic
places as a legacy for our children, please go
to the web site http://www.ourforests.org and send
a free e-postcard today.

If you don't have web access, just email
howto@... (mailto:howto@...)
for email instructions.

Please pass this message on to EVERYONE you
know who might possibly be concerned about
the future of our environment. Don't forget
your mom, your dad, your brothers and sisters,
your old college buddies, your coworkers and
your hiking club!
