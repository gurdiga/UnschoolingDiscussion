on 4/27/02 3:02 PM, ILYASAH@... at ILYASAH@... wrote:

> How would I change this group to that address?

The easiest way is to just sign onto your lurningathome@... account and
send a blank email from that address to:

Unschooling-dotcom-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

The disadvantage to doing it that way is that if you have a lot of Yahoo
lists going to different addresses, you won't be able to see them all
together under one Yahoo user id. You'll have to keep signing in and out at
Yahoo to get to your lists.

If you never go to the Yahoo webpages of the lists (like to search through
the list's archives at the website or to change your preferences) then you
don't need to worry.

If you do use the webpage, then just write me back at
fetteroll@... and I'll walk you through the process of adding
another email address to your Yahoo ID. (Once you do that it's easy to
change all your lists to various email addresses by just pulling down a pull
down menu for each one.)
