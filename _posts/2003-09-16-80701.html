Hi, I am Cat (37) mama to L (5) and (J) 10 mos. We live (and unschool) in Pittsburgh, Pa.

I've been enjoying the threads especially on rages and dealing with same. L has very extreme emotions (may be the age, may be her personality) and is generally what I would consider challenging. Nice to see various takes on how to deal with that.

I've also been reading with interest the breastfeeding thread. I don't spend any time in places where I think it woudl be inappropriate to nurse my baby. I don't deliberately flash anyone, but I certainly dont' hid under a blanket -- and I frequently walk aroudn with my baby in the sling, nursing. I agree with the poster who said that society won't change to feel that it is acceptable to nurse children, until people get accustomed to seeing it done.

Right now we do quite a bit of inadvertant flashing when J. suddenly unlatches to look around. There is a great Hathor the Cow Goddess comic about that: http://www.angelfire.com/comics/hathor/dismount.html

-Cat
