As you may or may not know, there is currently a new homeschooling law
proposed in the Pennsylvania House of Representatives. I belong to a
group that, among other things, is gathering data concerning how the
current law works (or does not work) in practice for people in all of
the different school districts of Pennsylvania. If you are a
Pennsylvania home educator and could take a moment of your time to fill
out the form below, it would really help all homeschoolers know what the
state of affairs is in Pennsylvania school districts. Please forward
the completed form to the addy indicated and feel free to forward the
form to any other Pennsylvania home educators you may know.

Thanks much.

Jake
