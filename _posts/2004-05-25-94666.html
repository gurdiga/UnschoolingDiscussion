I recommend signing up with an umbrella school for the reduction in stress levels!:) AEI (Alternative Education Institute) is a great one, and only $15 per year, per child...less than an eval.

There are completely free ones too. Florida Unschoolers is one I know
and trust. But deciding between these two legal ways of complying with FL's
compulsory education law involves more than just cost or the annual
evaluation. For a comparison between the options, you can check out
www.ParentDirectedEducation.org and follow the links.

I've been comfortable for many years with the evaluation option as a
home educator, and my website co-owner uses the private school umbrella option
and helps others do the same. Either one can be great, depending on how it
fits your own family -- PDE doesn't recommend one over the other. JJ
