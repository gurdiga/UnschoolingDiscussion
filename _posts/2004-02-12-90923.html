How old is your dd? Perhaps she needs heavier thicker cord - not synthetic but natural material is easier to manipulate. Cross stitch floss is difficult to macrame for beginners because if its size and texture. Also - when I first learned I used cardboard with pins to hold the cord in place until i mastered a knot. Check out the local crafts shop for heavier cord and simpler picture books.

I would also write to the company and complain, especially if the beads are too small for the size of the cord and if the directions are lousy. I used to write directions professionally and am so often appalled by what i see, especially in craft kits for kids. Who writes this stuff? Not me <g>

Lisa H.
