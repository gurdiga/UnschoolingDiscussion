I know most of you already know, however alot of people have
*complained* that they have 100's of emails backed up. I have mail
sent in daily digests which are a few emails a day. It is pretty
quick to scroll through it and just as easy to skip topics you are
not interested in. If comething interests me and I want to respond I
just make a mental note of the time and go back and look it up
later. Also, it is much easier to erase 3-4 emails if you are
overwhelmed than to sift through your whole box! Anyway, I hope this
helps anyone who did not know they had the option :)
