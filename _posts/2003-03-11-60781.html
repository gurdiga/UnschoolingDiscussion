UPDATE:
2nd Annual Spring HOMESCHOOLERS' OVERNIGHT CAMPS
April 26 - May 7 (12 days), Louisa County, Virginia
Oriented for ages 8-13 (parent's attendance optional)

Since our May 12 Adventure Camp is full, we've opened the April 26-
May 7 Camp as a combination Drama/Adventure. Children choose between
the two themes, and for two to four hours a day the focus will divide

between theater and orienteering. Otherwise, meals and activities
will be shared by the whole group.

Children choose their theme of interest:
DRAMA Theater games, practicing and performing a play for an
audience.
ORIENTEERING: Learn the exciting sport of land navigation using maps
and compasses.

Whole group activities may include: Canoeing, swimming,
cookouts, cooperative games, field games (such as soccer,
capture the flag, badminton, croquet, volleyball) scavenger hunts,
bird-watching, juggling, evening camp-fires/skits/singing
