I know I have added fuel to the fire at times on this list. Sometimes, who I
show up as here is not my best. Sometimes it is. I guess I can only be who I
am at any given moment. If I have hurt anyone's feelings, I do apologize. I
don't think it has been my intention to do so, but possibly I speak without
really thinking about the effect of my words sometimes. I do not apologize
for stating my beliefs about various things . . . that would be very contrary
to who I am as an unschooler and just as a human be-ing.

I do not always agree with Sandra, and have no probelm saying so, but I like
most others, really respect her and what she has to say about unschooling. I
don't like the quibbling either, and sometimes I get sucked right down into
it. I do believe that most of the time Sandra stays focused on ideas and
unschooling. . . probably 99 percent of the time. Anyway, I didn't mean this
to be a focus on her, but rather on my part in it all. I think I get caught
up in trying to get others to "see" how they are interacting in the whole
drama/situation. . . it really is not my job though. It is a mirror to me
though and it shows me something I need to work on, which is really all
anyone can do. . . stop focusing on others and focus on the work within.
