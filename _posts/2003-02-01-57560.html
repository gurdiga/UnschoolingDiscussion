Easy to Love, Difficult to Discipline by Becky A. Bailey and Rebecca
Anne Bailey.

I'm not sure if it was here or another list that I read a
recommendation for this book, but wherever, I really like it. I think
it has a terrible title and cover photo but if you get past those it
has some great ideas. What makes it different from so many parenting
books is that it focuses on the parent - how do you act, how do you do
with self-discipline, etc. - how you can change so you can have a
better relationship with your kids.
