Even if she doesn't, it's another of those stalling and "back off" tactics
like "After you've read this book."

You can say "I will read your opinion in writing," and every time she starts
to discuss, hold up your hand and say "In writing, please."

If she DOES read a book or write out her concerns, you TOTALLY win! And if
she doesn't, you get a reprieve.
