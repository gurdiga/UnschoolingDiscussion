SandraDodd@aol.com at 2005-06-06 07:02:17 +0300 said:

> So, where will this lead him? What career fields and people can I
> "strew" in his path to come into contact with?



I recommend neither, as he's 12. Maybe all his puzzle and game interests
will lead to his being a ballet dancer or a truck driver who's really
interested in puzzles and games in his spare time. Maybe he'll be a mathematician or
an engineer or a designer of strategy games (with cards or books or
computers). Maybe he'll be an artist.

As they come up naturally, mention whatever career fields there are in the
world regardless of whether they seem to math his 12 year old fascinations.
Help him meet all kinds of people, not just those you want him to "grow up to
be like." If you channel him toward a career or specialty, you might be
guessing wrong, and you might accidentally cause him to want to do anything
besides what you're pushing toward (no matter how subtly the push).

Keep it general and happy and busy!

Sandra
