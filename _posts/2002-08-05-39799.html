> Very often, the so-called mistakes are part of the kids'

My husband and I loved our son's little speech mistakes. Things like
am-i-nal and maz-a-gine. I never corrected them...they were his last bit of
cuteness. (My son is intense, wonderful, etc., but not cute.)

I can still see the fury in his eyes the day he realized, reading those two
words in the same sentence, that he had been saying them wrong all this
time, and that I had LET him say them that way. It took him less than 24
hours to purge them out of his vocabulary.

It took just slightly longer for him to forgive me! <g>
