Here's my two cents worth on visiting Harper's Ferry and Gettysburg the same
day, I think you may find it a bit much. From HF to G-burg it is about an hour
or so drive, count it as two hours door to door.

town called Sharpsburg and they have a FANTASTIC old fashioned Ice Cream shop

Then I'd head up to Gettysburg and get settled for the evening. Gettysburg is

would love to do those two days with you, do you have an idea of when you
will be here?
