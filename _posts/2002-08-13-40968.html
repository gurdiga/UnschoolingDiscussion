The 12 yr old boy in this book would benefit greatly from unschooling. He
gets bullied by classmates because he breaks out in song at "inappropriate"
times. He loves to read, he's not up on pop culture and he looks different
(not cool). The author, a former school teacher describes British schools as
smelling of disinfectant. He doesn't seem keen on the traditional school
system. They made a movie from this book starring Hugh Grant. I can't wait
to see it. I think he's perfect casting as the irresponsible playboy. He's
honest despite himself. I'm only half-way through but I recommend it, it's
very funny.
