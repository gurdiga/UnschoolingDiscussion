Oh, you can buy them commercially. Little things that you can attach to
trees. I've seen build your own type plans too. I actually have a lot that
I could build one on. It's five foot logs and they lay criss crossing each
other before the final layer of logs was put on I put heavy plastic over the
bottom logs. Butterflies go into the crevices and roost at night. When the
sun comes up and the dew dries up they head out of there. It is across the
yard from the flowers they like so we have butterflies flitting past the
windows in the day.
