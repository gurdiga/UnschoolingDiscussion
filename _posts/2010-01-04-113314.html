My daughter and I were talking today about how cool it would be to have one
of several possible birthdates this year.

The 10th of any month this year will give a birthdate of X/10/10, at least
as we write it in the US.

01/10/10 and 10/10/10 will be especially cool. 8/9/10 as well.

I love the many patterns found in numbers. My youngest child's birthday and
my husband's birthday and my birthday and our wedding anniversary all fall
on the same day of the week in any given year.
