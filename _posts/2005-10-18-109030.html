Google is working on making books searchable on line.

http://print.google.com/

You can search the full text of the books they've scanned in so far.
Obviously not all books are scanned yet but if you search for
unschooling 245 books turn up which is a more than I would have
expected to show up if they were all on line.

For books still copyrighted you can see a few pages around your
search term and I guess you're limited to a certain number of page
views since it's intended as a search feature so you can find books
you didn't know about rather than a place to read books on line.
(Though, like the Gutenberg project you can read books that aren't
copyrighted.) There are links to places you can buy it on line and
eventually to local libraries that have it.
