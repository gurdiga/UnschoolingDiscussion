Drumming & Learning Weekend Campout

This event features a FULL weekend of fun at Magnolia Park in
Apopka, Florida for ALL homeschooling families.

*Camping - homeschool group tent camping, RV camping sites or
daytrip options.

*Drum Building Workshops for kids, teens and adults with Stan the
Drum Man. Homeschoolers everywhere love Stan and HCECF is pleased to
make his drum building workshops available again this year.

*Nightly Community Bonfire Drum Circles.

*Engineering Workshops with the engineers of the Central Florida
section of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) for kids and teens.

*Question and Answer session for teens considering careers in the
engineering fields.

*SWE Art Show & Contest.

*Free goodie bags full of homeschool information and discounts,
magazines, etc.

*Raffles

*Junk in the Trunk homeschool sale, swap, barter, trade or giveaway

*Demonstrations (more details soon)

*Guided Nature Walk & Talks, plus Crafts for kids & teens

*Remote Control Car Races - a simple track will be set up, plus
there is also a small pond for RC boats

*Spring Egg Hunt

*Planned PE activities - teens beach volleyball, teens basketball,
kids kickball and PE games

*Great sand volleyball court, basketball court and THREE
playgrounds. Other recreation activities you can enjoy at beautiful
Magnolia Park - fishing, boating in Lake Apopka, skating, bike
riding, skateboarding, hiking.
