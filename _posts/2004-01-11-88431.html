When I was a kid, I really loved Bach pieces, and we were able to find books
that had small excerpts (the violin solos) from concertos. Also Beethoven
melodies. I have taken my kids to the music store where they could sample music,
and let them listen to classics, then searched online or at the music store
for the sheet music. For instance, they loved the music from some of the
Disney movies, which was classical in style, and we were able to find simple
versions for piano.
