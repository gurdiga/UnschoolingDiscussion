This is my third time joining this list. I did about
six months ago. I don't remember what the argument
was, but I felt for the newcomer who was seeking
support and was getting some judgments from others. A
month or so later, I tried again, and it was somewhat
the same.

I think perhaps if all of us can remember to
interject, In my opinion, or what worked for
us...etc., that would go along way to help keep the
subject on unschooling.

Remember, we are here because we *don't* have the
support of the larger community. Perhaps we all get to
where we hope to be, at our own pace, and our own
unique way. Whether our children have OCD, allergies,
ADD, LD, whatever, they are special and unique. And
none of us is responsible for their difficulties.

We are all trying. I applaud all of you who are
working so hard to give your children what he or she
needs.
