Lynda

11 Sep 2001, at 10:02pm

That's cool, now I know two heterosexual communists <g> My stepdad's
stepdad was and he was so neat when we were kids. I never could figure out
how "communists" could be evil like all the teachers kept telling us when
all he wanted was good working conditions and a share of the pie. He had a
couple of girl friends when he got older so I guess he wasn't into a
"closed" marriage. But hey, who cares, he was a great grandfather and gave
us kids tons of good memories!

Oops, I'm wrong. I forgot my 3rd grade school teacher. She was
heterosexual and a communist also (I don't know if she was in a closed
marriage or not). So that makes three. I remember this because there was a
big stink and we all had a weeks vacation because she wouldn't make us say
the pledge of alligance every morning in school and all the parents were
whispering about her being "red." She was also a great teacher in that she
didn't believe in pigeon holes or stereotypes and she never punished anyone.
We had discussions about inappropriate behavior and then the class decided
the "consequences" of the "behaviors" but the child who had misbehaved had
to agree. I remember she said that we all "owned" our own actions and had
to be responsible for them. Funny the things you remember.

Lynda
