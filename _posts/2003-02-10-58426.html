Saw a cool little science demo yesterday:

About 1/4 to 1/2 cup of water poured onto a dinner plate -- a big slice
of lemon sitting (peel down) in the water and 3 matches stuck into the
lemon. (Could be anything that will hold the matches.) Light the
matches and put a glass over the lemon/matches.

What happens?

The water all swooshes up into the glass. If you have a good seal, you
can even lift the glass and the plate will lift with the water still in
the glass.

Very cool. Maybe not as dramatic as the similar hardboiled egg trick,
but this one can be done even when you don't happen to have a
hardboiled egg handy.

--pam
