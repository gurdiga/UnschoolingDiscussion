Shall we collect some gratitude for Pam statements?

I'm so eternally grateful to Pam because hers was the first writing about
unschooling, other than John Holt, so the first real in-the-trenches with
real kids writing, that I read. She was leaving a statewide e-list and I
asked her where she could be found online. She directed me to the then
iteration of Unschooling Discussion and the rest is history. And Jayn was
something like 1.5 years old.

I'm grateful to Pam for the energy that she has put into Dragontree over the
years. I don't get down there often, but it's so good to know it's there.

I'm grateful to Pam because, even though it's not a support list, she shares
stories of her daughters, who have grown up to be magnificent women, that
over and over give me hope and encouragement.

I'm grateful to Pam because for some reason of her own, that I can't fathom,
she always seems glad to see me. I'm always very glad to see her.

Happy birthday beautiful Pam. I hope you have a wonderful day.
