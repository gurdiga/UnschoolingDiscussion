3 Jun 2003, at 11:37am

I tried to teach

my child with books.

He gave me only

puzzled looks.

I tried to teach

my child with words.

They passed him by

often unheard.

Despairingly

I turned aside.

"How shall I teach

this child?" I cried.

Into my hand

he put a key.

"Come," he said,

"play with me." ~ Unknown
