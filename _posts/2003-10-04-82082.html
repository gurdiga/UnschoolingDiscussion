I've been on this list a while now and thought it was about time I said hello. I'm Emma, mum to Chloe (5) and Ethan (16 months), my partner is Paul a web designer. She enjoys drawing and maths and loves being out doors so we do a lot of this whenever she wants - my younger one is very easy going and is happy to be involved in whatever we are doing or else entertain himself.

I just wanted to say how I've enjoyed reading all the posts and I look forward to learning a lot
