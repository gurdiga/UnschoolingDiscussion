Oh, Julian and I went to the last one GWS did right as we started
homeschooling!

It was incredible! The workshops were wonderful, and the place was amazing.
Julian was just 8, and he had always pretty much been around an adult most of
the time all his life. There, however, he found things he wanted to do, and
met me at specific times. It was cool, because it felt safe to let him do
that, and he felt good. He spent one whole afternoon in a kayak, and fell in
love. Now he has his own, and it's still a passion.

What a cool conference that was!
