The Bob Books are published by Scholastic, not by a relgious publisher.

They are small books that are easy for young hands to hold. In the
beginning of each book there is an introduction to new sounds. Each page
has a picture. Each box comes with 8-12 books, a teaching guide and finger
puppets.

They can be found on the veg-source board, in local bookstores, most
anywhere books are sold. You might try www.scholastic.com or call at
800-733-5572.
