Here is a great vacation opportunity to Hilton Head SC. Sept. 24-Oct. 1
2005. We rented a second unit for another homeschool family on our
vacation, but it didn't work out.

Hilton Head is a great low key, family friendly vacation area. Numerous
bicycle trails and flat terrain make it perfect for bicycling. (it's a 12
hour drive from the Philadelphia area)

Where: Hilton Head Island, South Carolina
Egret Point by Spinnaker--gold crown resort
More details at www.spinnakerrentals.com

When: Check-in after 4:00pm Sat Sept 24
Check-out before 10:00am Sat. Oct 1, 2005

Unit: Full kitchen with microwave, dishwasher, washer/dryer, TV,
VCR, CD player, whirlpool tub (see floor plans for two bedroom unit at:
www.spinnakerrentals.com/floorplans_unit_layouts_hilton_head.asp?location_id
=1&prop_id=23

Occupancy: sleeps a maximum of 6
2 bedrooms with King sized beds
2 baths
1 sleeper sofa

Other info: Hilton Head Island is bordered by one of the last major
unpolluted marine estuaries in the East Coast. There are several wildlife
and waterfowl preserves.

Egret Point has an outdoor pool & hot tub, Welcome party, and weekly
children's and adult activities, exercise equipment. The villas, located on
a private cul-de-sac, have scenic views of lagoons, the pool or the golf
course. All villas have two bikes to help you further enjoy Shipyard
Plantation and beautiful Hilton Head Island.

Beach -- 1 mile
Golf--1mile

Raquetball 2--miles
