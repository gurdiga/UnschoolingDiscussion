Does everyone remember the young man who got the perfect SAT scores. Big
deal was made because they were homeless???

Well, he was on the O'Reilly Factor this evening. O'Reilly asked a couple
of times about his "dropping out of school" before the young man corrected
him.

Wellll, I was really surprised at O'Reilly who usually does his homework and
is quick to pick up on things but he sure missed on this interview. The kid
rambled on and on about religion, Bob Jones University AND how his religion
comes before his country. He went on about what a moral person he is, blah,
blah, blah and then proceeded to lie to O'Reilly about why they were
homeless while implying that his mother had quit her job and hadn't gotten
another one because she wanted to be home with the kids. O'Reilly asked a
couple of times about the homeless thing and mother getting a job and the
kid kept up with the she wanted to be home with the kids.
