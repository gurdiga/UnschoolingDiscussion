It seems everyday I find a wonderful reason to be unschooling. However
recently I have come across a biggie. I just found out our school
district is sending the kids entering 5th grade to the middle school.
It means an extra 2 hours a day for these kids. My youngest would be
with these kids. It seems the elementary schools crowded themselves
when they started all day kindergarten.

I imagine if I hadn't pulled him last fall I would have done it
this one. BTW the middle school and high school share a bus out this
way.So these 10 yos will be riding a bus with kids as old as 18 ,
seems kinda scary. (No offense to anyone who has teenagers)

When that old yellow bus comes rolling around 6:45 am on the first day
of school next year you bet I'll be holding my boys extra
close!!!!!!

I also wonder how many calls I'll be getting to ask about
homeschooling?? hmmmmm...
