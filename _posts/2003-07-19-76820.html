19 Jul 2003, at 11:44pm

We had temps over 100 today. Guess we're not alone, sounds like a lot of the USA is having a heat wave.

I joined the kids today in the wading pool and eating a lemonade popsicle. It was very nice :-)

We just came back from an evening bike ride, now that it's finally cool enough to exert some physical effort.

I just finished reading Never Too Late by John Holt. It has motivated me to make time for some of the things I've been trying to do, but haven't been spending enough time at. I will be spending less time here for awhile.

Huge thanks to everyone who has replied to my sometimes ignorant comments. I *think* I'm finally understanding unschooling, but still have lots of personal issues to work on so that I can help my kids grow up with fewer hang-ups. I will always be grateful for the ways this list has opened my eyes, mind, and heart to so many issues regarding living with children.

Hope everyone is having a great summer!

Mary Ellen
