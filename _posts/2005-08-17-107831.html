I've volunteered to organize the mentors and mentees.

After getting ideas from Kelly, this is what I came up with:

1. Mentoring - connecting people up according to interests, and kids' ages.

Mentors: Let me know how many mentees you would take, and your interests. (Mentors don't need to be regular posters, but you DO need to be confident in unschooling and willing to share.)

Mentees: Let me know your interests and kids' ages.

I plan on doing this as soon as requests come in so that you can have the chance to email before the conference. I will do my best to get you as close as possible to your requests.

2. Stickers to announce your druthers - There will be some colored stickers at the registration table for you to put on your name tag (changeable throughout the weekend as you please) signifying things like:

"I've been to several L & L conferences and can help you"
"I'm shy, please talk to me."

Please send your info to me jparmer@...
