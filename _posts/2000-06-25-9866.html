Yes, I have been, Nanci! Congratulations!!! We adopted our oldest son who
will be 6 in Sept. Our circumstance was different in that we went through
the pregnancy with the birth mom, and were in the delivery room. But, we
still had to go through the preliminary workup: the home study, meeting with
the lawyer, etc...In our neck of the woods, we had to wait 1 yr before
finalizing the adoption, even though our son was with us from day 1. He did
not have to go to foster care or anything like that, but instead, came home
from the hospital with us, which was beyond great! After a year, we went to
the juvenile court, and became finalized and permanently legalized adoptive
parents! Then we had a reception! It was a great event! Best of luck to
you! I know you must be SO excited!!! I wouldn't mind adopting again one
day down the road... we'll see! :) ~Karen
