AND NOW FOR TODAY'S SITE... Ye olde story

Create your own medieval story. This Flash site lets you create a
story by adding people, buildings, animals, trees and text. You can
add as many as scenes as you want. It's like creating a historical
comic strip.

You might choose to write a story about hunting beasts or a kindly
king. After you're done, upload your story to a central gallery or
e-mail it to friends.
