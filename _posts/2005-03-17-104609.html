Today my 8 yr old discovered the wonderful world of skateboarding!
I have had them for a few years because I always liked to mess
around on them and today (despite it being damn cold out) my eldest
decided he was going to take the scooter out and then traded it in
for the skateboard which was more challenging.

A few lessons on ballance and gravity were learned with the ever
important pain reflex and fabric stability tests. He is so proud of
himself.

It's funny to me because the preconcieved notion is that boys "do"
that sort of thing and he never really got into it, while my
daughter (last year 1 1/2) got her first serious road rash from
boarding and had to be taken to the hospital. She has a fantastic
scar that will tell quite a few stories when she is older. I love
my goofy kids.
