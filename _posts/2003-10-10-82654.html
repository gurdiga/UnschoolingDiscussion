***Christopher read this last night and today he went spider hunting. ***

We made frames out of lath and put them on stakes in different places in
the garden and yard. Spiders moved in right away and built beautiful
webs.

If you put a little box or hollowed out chunk of wood on the frame the
spiders like it better. They have someplace to get out of the rain and
hide from predators. The frames can be any shape, spiders don't seem to
care.<g>

You can put them in good lighting for picture taking, but they need to be
a little sheltered from wind.
