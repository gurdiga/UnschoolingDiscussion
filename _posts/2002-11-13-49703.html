> The following poem caught my eye as I browsed through a product catalog.

> The author is unknown but appears to have been published in a book

> called "Sensory Secrets: How to Jump Start Learning in Children". You

> may already be familiar with it. It reminded me of your work. I hope

> you enjoy it:

>

> PLAY WITH ME

>

> I tried to teach my child with books;

> He gave me only puzzled looks;

> I tried to teach my child with words;

> They passed him by, oft unheard.

> Despairingly I turned aside;

> :How shall I teach this child!" I cried

> Into my hand he put the key;

> :Come," he said, "play with me."
