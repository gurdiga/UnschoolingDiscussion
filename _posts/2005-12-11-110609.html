Please join us for the next Boston Area Whole Life Unschooling
meeting. This monthly meeting is for parents who seek respectful
(non-authoritative) relationships with their children, throughout all
aspects of daily life. Letting go of parental control is an ongoing
transitional process that takes time. As unschooling allows us to
uncover our unconscious beliefs about education, whole life
unschooling prompts examination of another set of pre-conceptions
about childrearing.

The group is intended for parents who believe that all problems have
solutions and that while we still occasionally resort to coercion out
of desperation, it is not right or necessary. Therefore we are coming
together to support each other in finding peaceful alternatives: ways
to live in cooperation, focused on consensus as the ideal.

Next meeting date: February 2, 2006

Time: 7:00-8:30

Place: Sharon, MA - For location information, please join our
announcement-only yahoogroup at
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/BostonAreaWhole-LifeUnschoolers/

Discussion Topic: Sharing ways we've learned to stay present
with our kids and have fun while also being able to get our other
responsibilities handled.

Recommended Reading:
http://www.scottnoelle.com/parenting/child-centered.htm

In addition to the discussion topic, a portion of the meeting will be
set aside for group members to bring up other issues that they would
like to discuss.
