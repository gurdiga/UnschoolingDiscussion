Our son has a strep-related autoimmune disorder that was exacerbated by
his five-year vaccinations. We've run the gamut of emotions over this
one and also feel like we're often on a ferris wheel of food concerns,
therapy concerns, medication concerns . . . etc. It is a tough road,
but unschooling has made a mountain of a difference in all our lives.
It causes us to step back, and remember that our little guy is NOT his
disorder - - rather, he is his own unique person, with ongoing interests
and curiosities! Child-led learning, for him, has been so beneficial
because it gives him a lot of confidence and reassurance in an often
illness-ridden childhood. I empathize with those of you who are dealing
with autoimmune disorders in your families. My thoughts are with you
all today. God bless!

Kerry in NC
