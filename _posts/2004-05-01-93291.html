A Welcome from Dona Reynolds, Deputy Editor


The children are waiting for us. Yes, we adults need to

step up to the plate. The gifted and mystical children

of today are waiting for us to become the pillars of

strength and counsel they need to begin the work they

have come here to do. Are you ready? Are you

prepared? Children of the New Earth Magazine is

endeavoring to assist you with all the latest information

and guidance available today not only from experts in

the field but from the parents, grandparents, and

caregivers who work daily with these amazing, but

sometimes frustrating children. Together we can clear

the path the children need to move forward on.


In the

next few issues of Children of the New Earth magazine

you will see articles from experts in the field of

ADD/ADHD, Autism, and other developments in the

children of today. These symptoms classified

as "disabilities" just may not be disabilities at all. We

intend to bring you information that will change

your mind. Are drugs such as Ritalin really the

answer? Or are there new healing modalities that our

children will respond to that do not include drug

therapy? Yes, there are and we will bring you the

newest life-changing information coming into the field

today. Are you ready? A new day and time is upon

us. We intend to brighten your path with the latest

information available for you in your quest of standing

strong for the children of today.


Please check and be sure you have renewed your

subscription so you won't miss out on any of our future

issues. You can do that by going to our website and

clicking on My Account, enter your username and

password, and you will see your subscription

information. You can also use the website to make any

changes in your address if you are moving. The post

office is not obligated to forward magazines, and in

some cases they simply do not bother. It costs us an

additional $1.29 plus the cost of the magazine to mail

you a replacement. If you have not been receiving

your magazines, please contact us immediately and be

sure to change your address through the CNE

website.



We believe this magazine is critical. As we do not earn

big bucks from advertising because we feel bringing you

information is more important than advertising, we have

to be very careful to conserve every penny and many

of us do not take any money in salary, preferring to

make sure all the profits go back into ensuring the

magazine continues to thrive and grow before we do

so.



Remember to forward the Newsletter to your friends,

family, and clients by clicking on the link at the bottom

of the Newsletter.

NATURAL FAMILY ONLINE

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

There's a gaping hole out there for families who are

somewhere in between "mainstream" and "crunchy-

granola." Where do you find support if you're out there

in the middle? And where can you get solid information

about moving toward a more natural lifestyle - without

being clobbered over the head by the "granola police"?

Natural Family Online magazine shows you how easy it

is to fold natural, holistic practices into your daily

family life.


"There are so many aspects of natural parenting,

attachment parenting and natural family living, and not

all of them are practiced in any given family," says NFO

Editor Lisa Poisso. "All sorts of different combinations

work - but the result is that sometimes parents feel

out of place in places that seem predominantly

mainstream or strongly alternative. Natural Family

Online strikes a balance between those philosophies,

while always offering families the tools and strategies

they need to learn more about and move toward more

natural, holistic ways of parenting."
