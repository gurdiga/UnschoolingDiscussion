Hi listmates-
I have always encouraged my children to draw and draw and draw and draw . .
. we keep the ones we really love - and those that might be considered
"waste" - we cut into fourths and use as scrap notepads, clipped together
as a pad . . .or we use the back side and write letters on them . . . or,
we put them in the scrap paper recycling bin and bless their passage into
something else. . . but I could never assume for a moment that it was
wasting to see them creating in such joy.
