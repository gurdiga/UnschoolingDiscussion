Mary Hickman

16 May 2002, at 7:24am

I want my children to grow up at their own pace, free from continual peer
pressure. I want my children to capture the spark for knowledge they have as
toddlers and burn long. I see so many kids by 2-3 grade losing the fire of
desire to learn. I want my kids to know themselves and their family. I want
them to hold a fond memory of childhood. I want their childhood to be long.
So many kids grow up so fast, by 9-10 they are into looking for boyfriends
and girlfriends. I want my children to know they teach themselves now and
forever.

What I can come up with right now,

Mary
