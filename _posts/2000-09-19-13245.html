Here's a heads up as it is only getting very small print coverage:

There is a possible recall of taco bell corn tortilla shells because they
may include GMO corn that is only approved for use in cattle feed.
Independent labs have confirmed that the material is in the tortillas which
are not produced in the U.S. Taco Bell owned by Kraft who in turn is owned
by Phillip-Morse Tobacco Company says that they will be doing their own
testing.
