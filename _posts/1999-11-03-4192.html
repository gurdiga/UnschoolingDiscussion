Reptiles generally don't need to eat all that much, since they're cold
blooded. We used to feed my snake once every 3 weeks and he did fine. So,
he may not be very hungry. Often a pet store with some exotics will have
some good ideas. Can you figure out what kind he is from a field guide or
something?
