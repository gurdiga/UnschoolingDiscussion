This tread could not have coome at a better time!! thank you thank you
thank you!!! I was thinking about how much tv we watch around here and
kind of justifing cable because we have a big screen tv (DH X-mas gift)
but since we just moved we haven't had cable for about a week and life is
so much calmer! So last night as we were watching The 1900 House I
casually made the suggestion that we not get cable again, Dh agreed right
away (I never in a million years thought that would happen) and the boys
argued untill I explained that with the money we saved on cable we could
get a membership to the Zoo, then they agreed too. So yeah... my house is
now cable free!!

And since I just posted that novel maybe I should introduce myself, I've

classes because he would not sit in his chair after he had finished his

improvement in the younger one. Soon after that I found "the Unschooling
Handbook" (thank you Mary) and knew that was the way for us. Let's see...
anything else?? I work from home (Creative Memories, yes I do a LOT of
scrabpbooking! <g> ) and We live in D/FW Texas
