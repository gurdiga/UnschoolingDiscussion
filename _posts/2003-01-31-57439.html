I am one of the few native american homeschoolers with a wealth of information regarding homeschooling (been at it over 12 yrs) There are a few movies i can recommend the first being native based on true events, THUNDERHEART with val kilmer, based on the 70s pine ridge rez events, also LAKOTA WOMAN, same era, they are of great significance to my ppl. Another would be GREY OWL, based on facts. Apart from those we have watched and found of good information to spark conversation was AMISTAD, TITANIC, SAVING PRIVATE RYAN, WINDTALKERS (navajo codetalkers), INSTINCT (shocking how much empathy was brought about by this movie. WE WERE SOLDIERS, GETTYSBURG, THE WALL, THE TUSKEGEE AIRMEN, HAMLET (MEL GIBSON) AFRICANS IN AMERICA, ZULU, LORD OF THE RINGS, CELTS,SPARTACUS, ULYSSES, SANDS OF IWO JIMA, KIGHTS AND ARMOR, PATTON, ROBERT E. LEE , CAMELOT, GALLIPOLI, BEN HUR, GREASTEST STORY EVER TOLD, NAVAJO CODE TALKERS. We have so many more after yrs. of collecting that there are too many to mention feel free to ask me. For the younger ones I recommend BEN AND ME, ASTRONOMY 101, SECRET OF ROAN INISH, SQUANTO, A WARRIORS TALES, WHALES, WINTER WOLF, GREAT EXPECTATIONS, FLY AWAY HOME, ROBIN HOOD, and JOHN HENRY. I wish i could go on but im sure ive already overwhelmed you. Linda Blackwolf SalbThe Internet Movie Database ( www.imdb.com <http://www.imdb.com/> )
