For all those moms who love to read outloud and their children think their too old to be read to, (we are NEVER too old to enjoy good storytelling/story reading); try taping it. There are several ways you can go with this. Put the tapes on the bookshelf next to a recorder and those "too old to be read to children" may pick it up and listen. Or donate them to a learning disability class in your local PS. Or to the library??? I have often wanted to donate book tapes to the blind center for children who do not have books in Braille yet, but never had the opportunity to look into it. Good intentions are always on my mind, but getting my fingers to move on the thoughts is another thing.

Just a few ideas........... I would be interested in hearing from anyone who follows through with some of these ideas.
Thanks
Suzanne
