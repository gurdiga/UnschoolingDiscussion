I addressed the general question, but I need to mention the particular: I
don't "get across" anything to my kids. I'm there while they get it
themselves. It doesn't cross from me to them. I might throw something out
on the table, into the air, but they pick it up themselves, turn it over,
make sense of it, and store it in themselves.

There's a gigantic difference, and this is one of those times that
terminology matters.
