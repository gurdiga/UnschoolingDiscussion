It's good news that you will be coming to the States. I don't know all the

site but I'm afraid I don't know the addy. They can give you specific
information on how to start a small business, what licenses you might need,
phone numbers & addresses you might need, etc. And it's all free. I used
to work for them long ago, they have lots of info about lots of things, they
don't just register complaints and/or conduct mediation/arbritration.
