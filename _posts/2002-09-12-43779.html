12 Sep 2002, at 5:22am

Hey everyone,

I was just wondering if everyone came to some very stark realizations when you started unschooling.

My gosh, it touches so many angles in my life right now, not just schooling/learning. But all sort of things! It makes you question practically all aspects of our lives. Wow!

The Continuum Concept also explains alot about our society and thinking too, IMO,(hey I included an abbreviation!) Anyway....

Thanks to all that have started to make me think and have opened my eyes! Unfortunately not everyone in my life is ready to look at these things.

Kelli T.

I think I have probably read these exact sentiments from someone else, and you regulars have probably heard it millions of times, but I needed to vent it! Thanks!
