This is from an interview with Russell Means. I was actually looking
for a different unschooling quote from him but found this.

"A school system -- a government school system -- is in place to turn
out dumb people; they're more easily controllable by centralized
power than independent thinkers are. They don't want you thinking
independently in this country."
