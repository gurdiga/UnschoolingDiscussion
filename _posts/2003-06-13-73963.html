**On my list already are
Sandra's parenting tapes...any other ideas???!!! **

PamS has tapes from her two workshops on Mathematical Joy. Those'll
fire up your brain. Also available from www.hsc.org is a keynote speech
by Linda Dobson on Homeschooling Success Stories. I find that to be
nicely cheerful and uplifting.

I personally love talk radio, so you can download radio programs on
topics of interest from www.npr.org, if you have the right technology to
transfer it to your car.
