Post Secondary Enrollment Options. It's a program where juniors and
seniors in high school (public, private or homeschool) can attend
college and the state pays for it. My third son is now enrolled and
the other two really enjoyed it. One went on to graduate from the
school he started at and the other is going to transfer to a
different school after 3 semesters and two years off (he's in CA
now).

Janet in MN
