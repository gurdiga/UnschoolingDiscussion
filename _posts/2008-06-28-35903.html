There's a new game advertised from GameHouse. I have several of
their games. They're great with customer service and all that.

The new game is not for a Mac and Holly's too old, but if she were
younger, I would get this in a heartbeat.


Magic Farm
Grow and sell magical plants in this colorful adventure that's full
of fast fun. Help Iris earn enough money to search for her beloved
parents by growing and selling a wide variety of enchanted flowers
and charmed bouquets. A captivating journey through a wonderful land
of fairies, foes, and helpful little dragons, Magic Farm is blooming
with family fun.

It sounds kinda like Harvest Moon, but less expensive. Maybe less
reading.

Has anyone with younger kids seen it?
