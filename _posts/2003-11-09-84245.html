IIRC, _Titans_ was the last movie made with special effects from the famous
Ray Harryhousen, who had done the effects for the original King Kong
movie. Harryhousen was the guy who inspired the people who made _The
Nightmare Before Christmas_.

They do look cheesy in light of today's computer-generated standards, but
for movie buffs they are an important bit of history.
