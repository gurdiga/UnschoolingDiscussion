Dover Publications, Inc.
31 E 2nd St
Mineola NY 11501-3599

Dover has lots of inexpensive paper dolls, coloring books, classics, paper
models, etc. (most under $5). We carry some in our catalog and special order
others. If you order directly from Dover during December expect a delay.
Over the next couple of weeks they are installing new "Y2K compatible
software" and aren't processing orders (talk about waiting until the last
minute!)

Write and ask for a copy of their catalog of juvenile titles. I believe they
also have a catalog of all their titles arranged alphabetically. They have
many other catalogs arranged by subject, such as the juvenile catalog, and
these are nice because they show pictures of the books (their complete
catalog is text only).

In my catalog, I saw these:

Russian Imperial Costume Paper Dolls $4.95
Anastasia from Russia Sticker Paper Doll (smaller format) $1

There may be other titles too.
