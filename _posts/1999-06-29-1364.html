Linda,

No forms needed. Your certified teacher friend just needs to
identify your child (name, date of birth, address) and state
"this child has made progress commensurate with his ability."
sign his/her name and list their certificate number or however
they track that certified thing. Or, at least so I've been told
by a hs mom who is certified and does the evaluations.
You're friend can do whatever he/she wishes to determine said
progress. I guess they are supposed to review your portfolio,
but I am not aware of any specific guidelines for that. I
personally favor - just talking to your child. Hopefully, they
already know him and it doesn't have to be a big deal for any of
you.
