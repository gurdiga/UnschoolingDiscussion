> I can't walk the dog because she pulls so hard. She actually caused me
great pain.

I can't help with the training part, but you might want to get a Halti.
There are a couple of other brands as well. In fact we have a different
brand. I just can't remember the name of it. It's lasted better than the
Halti.

Anyway, the Halti goes around the muzzle so the dog feels the pull on the
side of the mouth rather than the neck - kind of like a horse halter but not
*in* the mouth. Dogs don't really mind the pulling around their neck. In
fact they'll just keep pulling and choking and pulling and choking. But
they *hate* the pressure on the side of the mouth. So they don't pull.
With the Halti on, I can just move the leash to one side and he'll turn
instantly. He doesn't pull at all after the first minute or so. They cost
around $20 Canadian, so maybe $14 US.
