I thought I'd share... We just cleaned up the living room, and Gracie brought a puzzle in and sat down by the marker board which is leaning up against the wall because I haven't managed to hang it on the wall yet.

It hit me that her puzzle pieces would probably be pretty darn cool with magnets on the back to play with on the board...so we stuck some on them - some animals...and VOILA! a "new" toy! :-)

I just added magnets to a couple other puzzles, and Gracie asked me to put them on her Strawberry Shortcake letters and numbers puzzles as well so I'm working on it. Thought I'd take a moment to share...maybe someone else has little ones who would enjoy this. I bought the roll of magnets at Walmart in the craft section for less than $3, and it has a peel-off thing on the back so you can just stick it onto whatever.
