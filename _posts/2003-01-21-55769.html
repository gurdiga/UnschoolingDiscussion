Have a Nice Day!

21 Jan 2003, at 3:00pm

I came up with an idea that might work for both me and my daughter without all the "learning to sew" stuff. 4H doesn't start again till next year I don't think anyway. And I have ZERO sewing ability LOL.

We are going to go the mall and really take a look at whats "in style". (And watch the style channel, etc to get ideas for the "cutting edge" styles).

THEN...we can go to thrift stores and find similar looking clothes, with similar material and modify them in simple ways to make them fit the current trends or to set new trends.

THEN maybe we can sell them at consignment shops.
