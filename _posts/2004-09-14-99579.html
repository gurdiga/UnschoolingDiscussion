The next generation GameBoy, the Nintendo DS, is being released November 30.
That generally means the older hand helds will come down in price. And you
might be able to get a used on either at Game Stop or online at eBay or
Half.com.

You might want to get the GameBoy Advance SP rather than the regular GameBoy
Advance. It has a much clearer screen that's easier for 2 people to see at
the same time and it's rechargeable.

The advantage of the new system, the Nintendo DS, is that Animal Crossing is
being released for it. (Yeah!) The disadvantage is that its listed price is
currently $200. (Boo!) It's also backwardly compatible with all games for
the previous incarnations of the GameBoy.
