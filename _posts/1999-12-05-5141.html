Hi Charlotte,

Welcome to the list! I have an 11 year old boy also. What kinds of things is
he doing for math? One thing my son has enjoyed is a math club which meets
once a month. Another homeschooling mom hosts the meeting at her home, and
about 10-15 children show up, ranging in age from about 7-13. They are doing
activities from the Reader's Digest book "How Math Works."

You might enjoy the Natural Math website for more ideas:
