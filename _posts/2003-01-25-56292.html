Julie, and everybody:

I'm wary of posting anything I don't know FOR sure. I have an appointment
with the hotel on Tuesday morning. If we can work it all out then---iron out
all details, I'll post next week. It will be in Columbia, SC TENTATIVELY
22-24 August 2003 at the Holiday Inn-Coliseum.

Prices aren't set yet---I have to figure out the expenses before I set the
price. I'll make it as reasonable as I possibly can. Last year we had family
discounts and early bird discounts, so the earlier you register, the better
deal you get. That's the plan this year too. Room rates are $79 plus tax, the
same as last year---in a better (and safer<G>) hotel . And kids under 5 eat
free.

Presenters are: S-P-A-M ---Sandra Dodd, Pam Sorooshian, Anne Ohman, and Mary
Gold. <G>

You can check out last year's website (which NEEDS to get hoppin' SOON for
THIS year!) in my sig line.

We welcome dads and children. Non age-discriminatory---although some things
are more age-appropriate than others.(and we'll have a room 'specially for
toddlers this year). And Ben will have some "Dads Only" sessions (at their
request).

I hope you can make it. "Shot in the Arm" is an understatement! Many said
last year was life-changing!! (I'll blow my own horn! <G>)
