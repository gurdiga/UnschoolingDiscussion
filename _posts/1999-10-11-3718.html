Mary Ellen,
Do you mean husband and wife take turns nurturing each other?

I tossed this question in here because SO many evenings I feel like I NEED
some nurturing. And my dh feels this same need....and wants me to nurture
him! I sometimes feel drained.....like if I give one more thing before I
receive some nurturing, then I may just BURST :-)
Anyway, I suppose taking turns might work- thank you for the
suggestion, I hadn't though to try that
I have also begun my own self-nurturing, though it's difficult to find
the time. I've decided this has to be Priority!! I have been getting up
earlier than everyone else 4-6 mornings a week. On these mornings I take a
vigorous 45 min walk. This is my fifth week. Some days it's tough to get
up and make myself get going.....but my marks on the calendar show me how
much I've accomplished (I track the mileage/days). I'm finally starting to
feel stronger.
And this week I'm adding focus on eating healthy. I think these basic
things make SUCH a difference in energy/attitude. Wonder why it's so hard
(for me) to keep these healthy habits???
Suz
