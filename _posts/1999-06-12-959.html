I picked up the coolest Hallmark graduation card for a friend's
daughter.


The outside said:

"Congratulations!"


The inside said:

"Now you get to read the books you really want to."


I thought to myself, "What a *perfect* card to give to someone who has
just escaped the world of conventional schooling!"
