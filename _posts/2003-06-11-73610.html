I was concerned about all the weird inventions they use in training - lights, bells, applause - but it's a WONDERFUL laboratory for learning a PRACTICAL life skill - I use the skills every single day!!!

Find a club with a time that works for you. Some clubs are 1 hr, some are 2 hr. I like one hour in morning myself.

Oh, and it's cheap too! Ours is $51 / 6 months Canadian, but only AFTER you join up. You can go as long as you want as a guest. You CAN EVEN refuse to talk as a guest. I did. I was crapping brix!

So, go for it. Next to unschooling, it is the best thing that I've done (for my self).
