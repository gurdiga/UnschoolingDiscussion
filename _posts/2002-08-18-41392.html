Just thought I'd share this funny story that just happened today, as I was
cooking spaghetti.
Kiere is 3 and loves to help. So she was next to me browning the meat for
the sauce.
My hubby in the other room asked if I was leaving the paper strewn all over
(you have to understand, I ask him this when he leaves stuff out and goes on
to something else.. yes, we are both ADD..)
Anyway, getting back to the story, I went in the other room to get the paper
retrieved and left Kiere stirring the meat.
Meanwhile, my hubby asks, "Is she okay in there by that hot stove?"
I told him she helps me do the meat often. So yes, she was okay.
I then finished up the paper, walked it to finish the meat up, and low and
behold
Kiere had added her idea of spices to the meat...
A full canister of pepper, just heaping over the meat..
I started laughing .... she didn't know why..
Hubby came in and said, "I guess she is too young, huh, and need
supervised.."
We laughed a good one!

Happy cooking and adventuring this week with your families!

Bj
