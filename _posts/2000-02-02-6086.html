Hi,

I have been remembering what my mother told me about what happened
when she was growing up and she and/or her brothers and sisters
got into fights.... (You will laugh at this, but it works!!)

Whatever two kids (or more) were in the midst of a fight or
disagreement,
their mom would give them some rags and soapy water and set them to
washing the windows - one on the outside and the other on the
inside. This was supposed to be punishiment - and perhaps in a way
it was,....but before long, they would be making faces at each other
and laughing together - their fight all forgotten!! I think my
grandmother was one smart lady!!! I know she was.....I loved her
very much and miss her more than words can say! My mom, too!

Karol
