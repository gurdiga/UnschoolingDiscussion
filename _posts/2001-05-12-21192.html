Would anyone like to share their most special Mother's Day with the group?

Here is mine:
We were on vacation driving up the California coast a few years back and we stopped at a beach, I think it was called 'Agate Beach' near Trinidad. While DH and I were relaxing on the warm rocks on the beach, our 2 daughters were searching through the rocks picking out the best ones. Then they went through the baggage searching for a receptacle to put their rock treasures in. Then that evening after dinner, they presented their Mother's Day gift to me! The most beautiful rocks they could find that were polished and rounded by the waves! That was my most special Mother's Day gift! And I still have them and take them out and look at the precious gems from time to time!
Kim
