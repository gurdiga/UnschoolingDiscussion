#1 easiest video to understand is "Live at Hollyw. Bowl." H. Grail is a absurdly gross. Life of Brian a bit sacrilegious (sp?), and Meaning of Life I saw in a theatre with 8 other people. 2 left. I sat with 2 others and laffed til I cried. Just me. By myself - the others were too floored!
