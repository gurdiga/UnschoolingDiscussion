Hi. This is a wonderful list! I've been reading and wanting to jump in, I
m ready now but thought maybe a little intro would be appropriate...or not.
lol We are Tammy (me), Keith (hubby), Haley (9), Kayla (8), Katy (5),
Denver (2). Sometime around 32 (I agree with you Sandra, my mom also
started looking for answers around that age) it hit me that how we were
living was just yuck. We moved to NW Mississippi after 2 years of Memphis
(TN) City Public Schools...ugggg. What a bad taste that was! We started
home-schooling but I hated pushing, they hated "doing school". As a teen I
saw no tangible reason to have all this info pushed on me without my
approval, then I saw it happening to my kids. No wonder we rebel so much as
teens. NO more...we are free, and you all came at just the perfect time.
Support is here! :-) I'm so excited. My two oldest still have a bit a
de-schooling yet to get their trust in me back...I was so pushy and
irritable. I finally let it go and it's amazing to see all the discoveries
being made all by themselves....all I had to do was open my eyes leave them
alone! Amazing. The only support groups that I am aware of in my area are
not for us, and I don't know of un-schoolers in my area, but surely there
are...I'll find them. Anyway, great to be here. :-D

Tammy
