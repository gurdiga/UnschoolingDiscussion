How about Gen 1:27-28?

"So God created man in his own image, in the image of GOd He created
him; male and female He created them. God blessed them and said to
them, "Be fruitful and increase in number; fill the earth and subdue
it. Rule over the fish of the sea and the birds of the air and over
every living creature that moves on the ground."

Having said that, I won't say that means that we today have a
commandment to be fruitful and increase in number, otherwise every
infertile couple would be breaking a commandment. I believe that
commandment was to Adam and Eve specifically. I do, however, believe
that the general attitude of the Bible is that children are to be
treasured, that they're wonderful blessings from God, and that we
should enjoy them and desire to have them.
