I've been asked a few times why the kids aren't in school, they've been
asked a few times also. We say we homeschool and most people drop the
subject. Robin does however like to go into details about how great it
is and they should do it too. People smile at him and are polite (well
he looks like the Little Prince - I'm sure that doesn't hurt).

A few times people have told us about their experiences with
homeschooling or homeschoolers (so far it's always been positive).

Last fall while the kids and I were goofing off at a pumpkin patch, a
private school pulled up and one of the moms asked me if we were with
their school. I said no we homeschool. She said their school was so
progressive that it was the same thing. I just looked at her and said,
no it's not. That was the end of that.
