The + and - amounts are relative to GMT. This is not something Yahoo
does but something each mailer program does.
Since you, in Germany, are +0200, I'd assume that GMT does not switch to
Daylight Savings Time as most of Europe and North America do!

There is a lot of information in a full mail header - we see only a
portion of it usually.

Cindy
