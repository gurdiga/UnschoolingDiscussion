Lisa,
I also have children with food allergies and I was able to come up
with great info simply by doing internet searches for food allergies. You
should also check out the Food allergy network's web sight. I'll look up the
address in a minute and send it along. FYI, this was just my personal
experience, but I wouldn't bother taking your baby to an allergist (if you
were thinking of that route). Ifound the allergy tests to be both painful
and inconclusive. dd #3 has had sevre reactions to eggs and nuts but skin
tests for both were neg. The allergist then went on to tell me that no test
is fool proof and we should treat her as though the tests were pos and that
the nut allergy should be treated as Life-Threatening. Ialready knew all
that before she was stuck with 8 needles. Oh well, live and learn.
Amy
