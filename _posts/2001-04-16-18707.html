I just wanted to let everyone know about a website we love called
www.webshots.com. You download a free desktop organizer, and then you
can choose whatever photographs you want. They'll be your screensaver
and your wallpaper. The wallpaper changes daily, unless you change
it, and the screensaver changes as frequently as you want it to. What
I think makes this of interest to unschoolers is that we chose mostly
animal pictures, and the kids are getting interested in all sorts of
animals they hadn't really been familiar with before. There are other
categories to choose from, too, like insects, nature, water and sky,
holidays, people, fire, storms, etc. And you can put your own
pictures on there, too. Just thought some of you might want to check
it out. Juli
