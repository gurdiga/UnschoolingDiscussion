Zak went to soccer practice last night and one of the kids on his team asked
Zak to come to his house to play for a while. The dad was talking to my DH
and asked which school Zak went too. DH said "he doesn't we homeschool him".
One of the older brother's whose 8 look at Zak with a look of awe and said
"you mean you never have to go to school?" Zak said proudly "No I have school
at home I get to do what I want to do everyday and do it at home isn't that
great". The other boy turned to his dad and said "why do I have to go to
that school, why can't I have school at home"? Dad didn't know what to say
but said "we'll talk about this later"... I thought "I bet they won't"!!!!!!

Score 1 for keeping kids home from institutions.
