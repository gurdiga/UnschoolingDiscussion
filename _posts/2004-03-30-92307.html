Billy or Nancy

29 Mar 2004, at 8:45pm
Just a reminder that the MHEA conference will be from 8:00 am to 5:30 pm
this Saturday (April 3, 2004) at Anne Arundel Community College in Maryland
(midway between Annapolis and Baltimore). The keynote speaker is Daniel
Pink, author of "Free Agent Nation"
(http://www.fun-books.com/books/8791.htm). Mr Pink makes a strong argument
for homeschooling as an excellent preparation for jobs in the future.

In addition to the keynote speech, there will be lots of workshops for new
homeschoolers as well as veteran homeschoolers with topics on unschooling,
mathematics, learning styles, high school, college, early years and more.
There will be a talent show, a panel of homeschool graduates speaking about
their experiences in the real world of college, work and the military and
much more! Lunch will be available on the premises.

Also, there will be a Local Support Group fair with representatives invited
from every support group in Maryland, and a Resource Fair with a wide
variety of vendors offering homeschooling materials.

FUN Books, www.FUN-Books.com, will be there with lots of great materials. In
celebration of our 10th anniversary, we will have special discounts,
clearance items, scratch and dent items and other special offers. You can
order in advance and pick up at the conference to save shipping and ensure
the items you want will be available, or you can request that we bring
specific items for you to review. If you've been thinking about learning a
new language, Rosetta Stone has announced new pricing that goes into effect
April 1st, but you can pre-order now at sale prices and pick up your order
at the conference.


You can get conference details and registration forms at
http://mhea.com/conference.htm

Please come out and support Maryland's oldest homeschooling conference and
meet a lot of great homeschooling families!

Billy
