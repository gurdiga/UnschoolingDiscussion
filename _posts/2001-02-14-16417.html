UNHOUSEKEEPING!!!! Now I have a name for it that really fits into my
lifestyle, thank you!! I hated calling myself a "bad housekeeper"
or "domestically challenged" or thinking about how I've always,
always hated housework. One of the ironies of my life, as I point
out to my husband every now & then, is that part of my "job
description" <g> would be housekeeping, which I'm horrible at and
HATE!

I have thought of what it would be like to give myself some kind of
motivation (other than having lots of company), like chocolate or
something else indulgent but I can't fool myself like that! Knowing
me, I'd just walk into the laundry room and eat the candy, never
looking at the 6 FULL laundry baskets (some dirty, some clean, some
folded, rarely put away)!

I do have to say that this nesting thing is getting out of hand,
though (baby's coming in a month!) and things like clean ceiling fans
seem really important right now! Go figure...
