I want to say a thanks to all the many voices on the various sister
unschooling lists and this one especially. I have been soaking up the
words they've been speaking for over 3 years now. Not a long time but
enough to make a difference. Let's just say it's been a journey I
have no wish to forget. Rather I wish I could remember all the words
that help the most, more often and with greater clarity than I
actually do. Sometimes I haven't felt able to breathe, at least not
freely. But I'm beginning to and beginning also to feel confident
that I can do this thing called unschooling.

Thanks,
Katherine
