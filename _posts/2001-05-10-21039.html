Lynda, our experience has been very similar. We found that dairy and wheat
were big culprits here (our son has SID - that's sensory integration
dysfunction, for those not used to the acronym), and then when we removed
wheat and dairy from his diet it exposed a host of other food allergies we
had no idea were there. A couple of useless rounds with a pediatric
allergist later and we stumbled on to NAET (Nambudriped's Allergy
Elimination Technique). Since starting that, we've begun eliminating our
son's food allergies completely and the most of the behavioral and sensory
issues just went away and we don't have to deal with the restrictive diets
anymore, very nice!
