Hello there all,

I just wanted to share a link to a great online multiplication
resource:
http://www.gdbdp.com/multiflyer

Multiflyer is a free game to play online (or you can register and
download the game and also get cool extra interactive
multiplication tools and fun stuff.) The premise is that you are a
pilot trying to save the solar system. You have to use your
multiplication skills to find navigation coordinates to get to the
next station. The game also has a practice feature for drilling.

Enjoy,
