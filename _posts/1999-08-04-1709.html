Thank you to all who responded to my message about my son. He is not
attending school - though he did last year. He struggled every day and
summer school really opened my eyes to the lack of individual attention he
gets in a classroom setting. I did homeschool him one year while we moved,
but he wanted to go back. This year, we are both excited about our upcoming
school year together. Getting him motivated is my biggest concern. He has
very little interest in anything related to school - even when it is just fun
(cooking, art, etc.) I think some of this is related to his depression as I
go through those moods as well. Any suggestions on motivating reluctant
learners would be helpful as well.

I have thought of food allergies - does anyone have any resources on an
elimination diet? I am unsure of how to approach finding out if he has food
allergies and our doctors here are of very little help. I am also looking
into many different forms of natural healing (like aromatherapy, massage,
sound therapy, etc.).

Thank you again for all of your thoughts and ideas - just feeling like I'm
not alone in all this is helping tremendously.

Thank you!
