Have a Nice Day!

16 Dec 2002, at 7:05pm

My mom teaches college English classes at several colleges in the area. Over the past few years, she has had several students choose homeschooling as a topic to write about.

She generally requires them to interview people for research and present both sides of the issue, or support one side or the other after doing research on both sides.

Anyway, I have to laugh because she always gets peeved when the papers come back slanted with the usual crap: socialization, parents can't teach what they aren't expert in, yada yada yada. Several times she's told me that they only interview one side, and spend very little time investigating the homeschool side.

Well, she asked me if I'd like to come into her classes and have the students interview me and I could talk about homeschooling, etc. I said yes. Should be fun :o). I think it will be this spring and next fall.

Kristen, the one who was the teenage queen of debate at home ha ha ha. My mama done told me I should have been a lawyer!
