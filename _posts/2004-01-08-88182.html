Pam already gave the first thought that came into my head - drop out of
HSLDA. Requiring an ISP is going very far beyond what is required by the
State of California, and I second Pam's recommendation of looking at the
regulations at hsc.org. Don't let HSLDA make you feel afraid of unschooling
in California (or anywhere else!). Since we are without testing or any kind
of evaluations, we are one of the easystates to unschool in. Having said
that, there are "umbrella" schools in CA that exist entirely for
administrative purposes, as against providing curriculum materials.
