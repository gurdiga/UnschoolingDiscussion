A friend of my eldest (Katie the one in Highschool) sent this to me
yesterday.
She such a sweet kid and so philsophical. Shares some classes with
Katie. She is a wonderful friend to my daughter.
Not all Highschool kids have their heads in the clouds,
so I pass it on to you all.....
Buzz


To realize the value of one year:
Ask a student who has failed a final exam.

To realize the value of one month:
Ask a mother who has given birth to a premature baby.

To realize the value of one week:
Ask an editor of a weekly newspaper.

To realize the value of one hour:
Ask the lovers who are waiting to meet.

To realize the value of one minute:
Ask a person who has missed the train, bus or plane.

To realize the value of one second:
Ask a person who has survived an accident.
To realize the value of one millisecond:
Ask the person who has won a silver medal in the Olympics.

Time waits for no one.
Treasure every moment you have.
You will treasure it even more when you can share it with someone
special.
