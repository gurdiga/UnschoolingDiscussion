This isn't really a baby list, and lots of people here haven't had babies for
years, so I went to find other sources for heping babies so the list isn't so
gummed up.

One I had forgotten is on one of those pages. In case it's teething (or even
if it isn't!) give them a washcloth which had a wet corner and has been in
the freezer. I remember keeping frozen wet washcloths in the freezer, in ziploc
bags, for owies or for chewing or just for soothing play in the summer.
