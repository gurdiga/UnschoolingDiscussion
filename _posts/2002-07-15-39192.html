In fairness to the idiots, the question does contain "to public
and private schooling," though most school parents opt for one OR
the other at any given time, LOL. When I first saw the poll this
morning - and meant to post it here - it was pretty evenly tied.
As it has made the internet rounds, the results have moved in a
steady hs direction. Knowing that on-line polls are neither
scientific nor secure from those who know their cookies, I still
think they are interesting for the sociology of who finds them,
cares about them, and uses them.

Here are the semi current numbers

Do you think home schooling is an acceptable alternative to
public and private schooling?

Yes (1123)
71%

No (462)
29%

Total number of votes: 1585
