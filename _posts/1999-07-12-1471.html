I picked up Seen and Heard : Teenagers Talk About Their Lives by Mary
Motley Kalergis at the Library a few days ago. Even though my kids
are still very young, I thought it looked interesting.
I highly recommend it to anyone...parent, teacher, grandparent, citizen
ect.... I wish teenagers were given more of a voice in this world.
It is a special book, and I wanted to share...
