Shannon Nicoletta Manns

5 Sep 2000, at 8:12am

- "Getting the Love you want", Harville Hendrix (good one to start with for
adult intimate relationships)

- "Conscious Loving", Gay and Kathlyn Hendricks (same as above)

- "Love and Awakening", John Welwood (really good)

- "Love - An Inner Connection", Carol K. Anthony (based on principles drawn
from "The I-Ching", great chapter: 'Coming to Meet Halfway')

- "Calling the Circle", Christina Baldwin

- "On Dialogue", David Bohm (quite intellectual, but very valuable)

- "The Dance of Anger" and "The Dance of Intimacy", Harriet Goldhor Lerner
(both excellent!)

- "The Celestine Prophecy" (couldn't put it down) and "The Celestine
Vision", James Redfield

- "Conversations With God", books 1,2,3, and "Friendship With God", Neale
Donald Walsch

- "Gesundheit!", Patch Adams (loved it!!)

- "Something More: Excavatinig Your Authentic Self", Sarah Ban Breathnach
(my favourite pages 307 - 312: 'The Queen of Sheba')

- "Parent Effectiveness Training", Thomas Gordon (great not just for
parents, should be titled 'People Effectiveness Training')

- "The Teenage Liberation Handbook", Grace Llewellyn (outragous and fabulous
not just for teenagers)

- "Indigo Children", Jan Tober and Lee Carroll (really good for parents -
not afraid of 'new-age' stuff - with high-need, intense, 'ADD'/'ADHD kids)

- "Rich Dad/Poor Dad" and "The Cashflow Quadrant", Robert T. Kiyosaki
(excellent for exploring your relationship with money)


This is my own personal list of favourites. Let me know what you think.

Nicoletta
