<< Why can't I just play the piano and SUCK but love it?? >>

Hey, *I* do that!!!

Right now I have a little pump organ, probably from WWII. I play hymns and
Clementi sonatas and easy Bach stuff. When they go off the end (too high a
range) I just jump down an octave until it will fit again, and if I make
mistakes I don't care, and I play really loud, and I don't practice the hard
parts and then I walk away smiling. It's like running just for fun, without
a destination, without a witness, without timing yourself. It's like
swinging on a swing, or playing a game.
