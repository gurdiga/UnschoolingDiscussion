I forgot to mention Lionel's Traintown as a computer game. You can build
your own train layouts and run all sorts of trains on them. They also have
more "schoolish" things like train games that feature math, matching games,
etc. All of it is done with trains. Both of my kids play like this CD-Rom,
but if grandpa (a bonafide train fiend) comes over, he might take over the
computer :-)
