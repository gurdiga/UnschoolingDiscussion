Yep, I always called them pickled eggs, but then my ex-Amish friend told
me that Pickled Eggs were just eggs in some kind of vinaigrette thing,
and that what I make are Red Beet eggs! Oh well, whatever they are
called, here is the recipe.

Mix: 1 cup Cider Vinegar
1 cup of beet liquid (I usually just dump in a whole can of
sliced beets)
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 t salt
1 small onion sliced
4 whole cloves
1 cinnamon stick
Mix all in a jar. Good job for kids...SHAKE-SHAKE-SHAKE-
Add your hard boiled eggs. They taste best after sitting in fridge a day
or two. Last up to two weeks.
Enjoy!
