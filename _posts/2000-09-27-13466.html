My kids are likely to say "I don't go to school," which causes the direction
of the conversation to change. As long as the questions are "what grade are
you in?" or "How do you like having your mom be your teacher?" and such, the
other person is controlling the dialog.

My kids have said, "I don't have a teacher--I learn from everybody," and
"It's like a big field trip" or "We play a lot," and those are all good
answers as far as I'm concerned.
