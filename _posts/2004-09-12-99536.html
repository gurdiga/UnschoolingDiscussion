Hi Pamela,

I'm trying to do this right now in Louisiana. I only knew about 3 families
in my general area who were unschooling, and wanted to try to build an
unschooling community. I set up a Louisiana Unschooling list on yahoo, then sent an
announcement/invitation out to several local groups letting them know about
it.

It's only been a couple of weeks and there are 27 members! Not all of them
are radical unschoolers (in fact only a small handful are), but people are
coming... and reading...and asking questions. I'm hopefully we'll convert them -
LOL. I'm finding that there are a lot of people who 'think' they know what it
means, but then discover they were totally wrong. But the ones who stay are
interested in learning. I put up lots of links on the homepage, with articles
by Sandra, Danielle, Anne, etc., so the info is there for them.

I learned how to set up the site by looking at other state unschooling lists
on yahoo.

Warmly,
