Love it, own it! I can quote the entire thing as
well.
Indigo Montoya, to the man in black climbing the
cliffs below him "Hello down there...you do not think
you could...speed things up?"

"If you're in SUCH a hurry you could lower a rope or a
tree branch or something!"

"I do not think you will accept my help, since I am
only waiting around to kill you."

"That does put a damper on our relationship."

I love Monty Python's Quest for the Holy Grail. It's
hilarious when the chaste Sir Galahad stumbles across
the castle full of frustrated virgins. The Black
Knight Scene, and the Viscious Rabbit scene are
classic Monty Python.

We also really enjoy action fantasy and sci fi, like
the Matrix, 13th Warrior, Long Kiss Goodnight, Enemy
of the State, Stargate, Armegeddon, etc.

As for guys in movies, I really like Harrison Ford,
Sean Connery, Brendan Frasier and Wil Smith. But I
MOST go for the more quiet, shy or broody types -
Gabriel Byrne, Tim Robbins, Ben Affleck, Jimmy
Stewart, James Spader, David Duchnovney-(who everyone
says looks just like my husband! :-)) Val Kilmer,
Russell Crowe, Liam Neeson. Gary Oldman in Bram
Stoker's Dracula is to DIE for! LOL We am also
anxiously awaiting the Mummy Returns. The previews
look Awesome!

Some of my favorite actresses are Minnie Driver, Drew
Barrymore, Sandra Bullock, Jodi Foster, Audrey
Hepburn, Geena Davis, Winona Ryder, and Clair Danes.

Nanci K.
