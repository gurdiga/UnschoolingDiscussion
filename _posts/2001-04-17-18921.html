17 Apr 2001, at 11:03am

The Family Unschoolers Network ( http://www.unschooling.org )
announces the following interview opportunity for April 17-24, 2001:

The New York Daily News is working on an article about homeschooling
and is looking for families to interview from Brooklyn, Manhattan,
Queens, Staten Island, and the Bronx. They would like to have
families in each of the boroughs that they can spend a few hours with
to observe a "typical day." If that is not possible, they may settle
for an interview by phone. They want to be able to present a variety
of perspectives and we would like them to see that the "school-at-
home" crowd isn't typical of all homeschoolers. With the pending
legislation in New York, this may provide a good opportunity to
influence public opinion.

If you are interested in participating in this interview opportunity,
please contact Greg Gittrich at the New York Daily News. Here are the
details:

Contact: Greg Gittrich
phone: 718 822-1174
pager: 917 616-4119
e-mail: gittrich@...
deadline: 4/24/01

All contacts must be made soon as interviews need to be completed by
April 24, 2001. Please do not respond after this date.

As usual, this information may be forwarded to any individuals or
groups you feel may benefit from the information. We ask only that
you include this entire message.
