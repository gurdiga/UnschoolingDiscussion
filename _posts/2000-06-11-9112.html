Lorrie, Amy and all: Way to go, Amy! La Leche league is mother to mother
breastfeeding support, through group meetings and telephone support. I have
been involved in LLL for 3 yrs, 2 kids, 4 moved, & the decision to stay at
home. Almost all of my best friends I have met throught LLL. I have been a
leader for 1 1/2 yrs and learned about attachment parenting through there.
Although, not everyone there (involved in LLL) AP's.

If you have any more questions, I would be happy to answer them. They do
have a great site, though. I don't read anything unless LLL has approved it
for their libraries. It saves me tons of time, weeding out books that don't
mesh with my personal philosphy, plus I get the books from the group and I
get to keep them for a month. If the library is slow, sometime two months.
hee hee hee

You can find a local group by checking out their site or calling
1-800-laleche. BTW, their address and other great websites is in my signature.
