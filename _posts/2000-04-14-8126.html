Our local library's website has a new link to this program, but anyone with an email address can join.  Here's the website's description:
 
Rediscover the joy of reading with Chapter-a-Day, my free e-mail service that delivers chapters from best selling books. I've divided the chapters into daily segments that take about five minutes to read. After you've read 2 or 3 complete chapters from a book, I'll start sending a new one. Chapter-a-Day is for everyone who enjoys reading, but can't find the time. I've selected books that will make you think, move your heart and heal your soul. Sign up today and start receiving great books in your daily e-mail.
 
The website address is www.chapteraday.com
