THanks! She went to Gladwynne Montessori. This was the first experience with
any type of Montessori. She actually enjoyed it and made friends, etc. but I
was not going to send her back there. I did not realize when I was checking
out schools for her that this area was probably one of the most wealthiest
around. I have nothing against wealthy people at all, but it was not a
situation I would have kept her in. What I did like about it was no testing
(except Standardized) or grades. However, it was a school and very
structured. She decided on her own to homeschool this year. .  in fact, I was
quite shocked when she told me she would. SHe has told me during the year
this year about how she realizes now how that school was so stifling. . .
.having to do things at a certain time. . . needing permission to go to the
bathroom, etc. etc.  

I understand your wanting to have order, etc. but I think if you are really
interested in unschooling, you might want to check out Open Connections.
Email me privately if you would like phone number, etc. I would be happy to
talk  with you about it.
