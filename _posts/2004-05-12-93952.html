***a high wooden fence on the back***

Look at garage sales for different colors of exterior latex and paint a
mural on the fence. It could be dinosaurs or a spaceship invasion or
jungle animals or racing cars or a Marc Chagall scene or anything.

You can buy paint for concrete and paint a habitat or a roadway for Hot
Wheels or a twister mat or something on the concrete, if it's a wide flat
surface...I'm not picturing the yard well... sorry if that's a goofy
idea.
