inclusive group, and none of us is very schooly although I think I'm
the most radical unschoolish of all of us. Also look for LEAF support
group in middle and southeastern MA. I'm not familiar with many of
the members there but I believe they're pretty welcoming too.
http://www.livinglearning.homestead.com/support.html

Also I know of LOTS of people who would jump at the chance to attend
an unschooling conference in New England.

Namaste to you all,
cris (who has very nearly stopped worrying about schoolish issues
