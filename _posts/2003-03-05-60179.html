myself.
I even get a good laugh sometimes, because as I clean, I never finish ONE

entry way is messy, I pick up a bag in the entry way, notice that the desk
has a bunch of papers, start in on those...sigh.
It's like a big circular thing. I always end up back at the beginning
eventually.

I like our five minute clean ups best. The kids and I tackle the living room,

done in a short time and feel better the rest of the day.
