Here are some recommendations from my 12 yo son:

The Enchanted Forest Chronicles (quartet - Dealing with Dragons/Searching for
Dragons etc.) by Patricia C. Wrede

Minds Quartet (Of Two Minds and sequels) by Carol Matas

The Castle in The Attic (and sequel) by Elizabeth Winthrop

Narnia Chronicles by C. S. Lewis

Fantasies by Edward Eager

Also ones by E. Nesbit

(the last 3 authors we've been reading out loud from during the last year -
and both my 12 yo ds and very sensitive 8 yo dd have enjoyed them),
