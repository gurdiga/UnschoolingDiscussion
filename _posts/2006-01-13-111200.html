Besides using the recipes on the web, you can also buy cheap boxed cake or
cookie mixes and let them use them a little at a time. Look for the ones
that just need water and oil. Sometimes we break an egg and keep it in a
bowl in the fridge and use a spoonful of it as needed too. It's pretty
cheap and easy that way.
