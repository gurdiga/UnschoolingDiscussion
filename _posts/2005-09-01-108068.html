On August 23, 2005, ZoloWear voluntarily recalled 177 slings sold
between
May and August 2005 due to a manufacturing error. ZoloWear slings
should
have three rows of stitching securing the webbing and fabric together.
Some
of the slings in the recall lots have short webbing, so only one row of
stitching holds the webbing in place. This stitching can break, posing
a
fall hazard to young children.
