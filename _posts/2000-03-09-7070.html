Homeschooled Girls are Invited to Submit Writing

I am a 17 year old homeschooled girl and enrolled with Clonlara's home based
education program. How would a book titled, You Are Not Alone: a book for
and by adolescent homeschooled girls, sound? The title isn't definite yet.
I want to be able to help girls close to my age get through tough parts in
the homeschooling experience. Girls born in 1980-1990 are welcome to submit
a story of their homeschooling experiences. I am looking for essays, poems,
song lyrics, art-work, and journal entries on the following topics*; family
relationships (grandparents too), homeschooling processes (ie: how you do
it, your goals in life, your dreams), transition process (ie: institution to
homeschooling), self images (physical and emotional), peers (ie: best
friends, guy friends, girl friends), mentors, personal Interests (art work,
songs, whatever!!!).

*If these catagories don't offer what you need. Please do not feel
restricted with the above choices! But your contribution has to be focused
around homeschooling/unschooling. Please spread the word to other
homeschooling girls.
