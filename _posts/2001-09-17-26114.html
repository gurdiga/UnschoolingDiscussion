17 Sep 2001, at 1:20am

I watch so many of my friends display gratitude for the end of summer and
I do as well. However, while most of them are happy to get a break from
their kids I am thrilled to get mine back! During the summer they are so
busy with friends, outdoor activities and unlimited invitations to spend
time at the homes of friends who are busy cramming in as much "play" as
possible.

Fall and winter mean time in front of the fire, rambling chats, arts &
crafts, baking, family meals and every other aspect of our unschooled
lifestyle. I thank God for these coming months of intense kid time.

Kris
