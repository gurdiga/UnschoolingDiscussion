Unschoolers Circle, I think. Geez, its been so long since I thought of it
as anything but the UC. It was started after a big war on the UL
(Unschoolers List) and some members were kicked off and others were told to
shut up (UL is moderated). At first it was more a sounding board for a
particular couple of folks who are/were rabid unschoolers! And I mean
RABID!!! There way or no way or you weren't an unschooler. Then about 6
months ago it blew up with one list members boyfriend coming on as a troll
and then another list member going balistic because he didn't believe in
medication of any sort and then wanted another list members hubby (a
doctor) to do online prescribing for the teenage girl he married (he's 40).
The feathers, fur and just about everything else hit the fan. Two other
lists were off shots of the UC, SO (Schools Out) and NC (No Class, as in no
classes). Then NC blew up because of off list posting and parties that
live in LaLa Land.

And there you have the UC, which is really quiet right now, saga. And, to
cut to the chase, yes they are on OneList.
