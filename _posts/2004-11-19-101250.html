salsacecy@...

I am a graduate student at Brooklyn College. I am doing a research
project on Home schooling. I am having a hard time finding home
schoolers to interview. I need this information by November 30, 2004
or sooner. Here are the questions I need answers to by parent and
child. Please have as many home school families as possible to answer
the following questions. Their personal information will not be
included in my research paper. Thank you in advance for your help.

Questions for parents:

These questions are voluntary you have the right to refuse to answer
any and all questions. Your personal information will not be included
in my research paper.

What were you thinking when you decided to home school?
What dramatic event occurred to prompt you to home school?
What is your personal experience with the educational system?
What research did you do?
What are your expectations?
Was this a family decision?
Did you resign a position to home school?
What type of career did you leave behind?
Are you connected to the same school for a curriculum or another source?
How does your child react to you as an educator?
How long do you plan to home school?
How many other parents do you know that home school?

Questions for the child:

These questions are voluntary you have the right to refuse to answer
any and all questions. Your personal information will not be included
in my research paper.

How old are you?
What grade are you in?
What level of education are you in?
Do you have brothers or sisters?
How do feel about being home schooled?
Did you ever attend public or private school?
How many other children do you know that are home schooled?
Do you plan on going to college?
What do you dislike about home schooling?
What do you like about home schooling?
What did you dislike about going to a school?
What did you like about going to a school?
What is the hardest subject to learn?
What is the easiest subject to learn?
What subject do you feel is a waste of time?
What are your hobbies?
What type of sports are you interested in?
Are you in a sports team?
