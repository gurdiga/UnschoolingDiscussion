Melanie Freisinger deGonzalez at 2004-02-09 18:05:57 +0200 said:

Clearwater Beach is a nice spot. The beach itself is lovely and fairly unpopulated. Sand Key is just up (or down?) the road, a great place for shelling, especially sand dollars. There is a bird sanctuary that we found fasinating. There used to be a steel drum band at the big hotel at the south end of the beach....but that was a few years ago.
Hope you have a lovely trip!
