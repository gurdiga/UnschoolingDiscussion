Well, Joe is younger and technically better looking. But the difference
is this: Joe seems like someone who hopes he looks sweet and innocent
for the camera. Steve seems like someone who really is sweet but wishes
it didn't show so much.

Also, Steve is special for us because my husband and I started watching
Blue's Clues when our first son was born, before he was even old enough
to focus on the screen, because we wondered what it was about, and what
kids were about. We've watched it every single day since. Blue's Clues
with Steve Burns will always remind me of those early days of parenting
when my husband and I had no idea what to do, but we just hoped we were
doing it right. He has been a fixture in our home for five years. We
have all the videos and we were pretty upset last weekend when one
finally wore out. It's one of the first ones, and I'm not sure it can
be replaced. Joe's okay for a host of a kids' show, but he'll never
mean as much to us as Steve.

Of course, my kids both love Joe.

Sorcha
