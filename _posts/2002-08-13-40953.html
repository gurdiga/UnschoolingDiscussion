The Victoria Homelearners have been doing this for a number of years
now. (We stole the idea from Vancouver.) We meet at a wonderful local
beach around late morning of the first day of school. Not only is it a
great chance to re-connect with home learners we haven't seen for a while,
it's also great for newbies. They get a chance to see a lot of kids and
parents who seem pretty normal. They get to see a lot of kids of all ages
mostly getting along well. Even though my teen doesn't like going anymore,
*I* still go. <g>
