My daughters, 11 and 13, LOVE the Harry Potter series. They have created a
very involved mystery game that they play together regularly that has gone
on for months.

Now they are busy writing their own "books" that are take-offs from the
originals. Are there other "Harry Potter-Crazed" Unschoolers out there who
would enjoy sharing stories they have written that were inspired from the HP
books?

Francesca and Sierra would love to be members of a Harry Potter Club where
they could share their stories with others of like-mind along with all other
ideas and games inspired by the HP books.

Please respond if you know of such a group or if you know how we could
create one!

Linda, the mom.

Hi Linda,
My DD's (14 & 10 yo) are Harry Potter doozies too!  They are joined to a role playing list.  They pick a character, either from the books, or make up their own and they interact with other people in an ongoing story! If your girls are interested, you can find it at AHarryRPG@egroups.com My DD's user names are Hunefer Horus and Shauria Orcinus (not their real names!)
