Shaving cream in the bathtub, a bin full of rice for her to scoop and pour
with, mganets (just keep her away from the computer!!), movies, playdough,
fill a sink with water and give her a bunch of scooping and pouring gadgets,
make her own recipes (put out cheap ingredients like sugar/flour/water),
scissors and paper, fill a small plastic pool with popcorn, use the same pool
for a warm bathtime right in the kitchen....
If you have an air popper, put it in the middle of the pool without the lid
on and watch the popcorn fly EVERY where! Great fun but make sure you stand
back a bit as it's very hot when it comes out.
