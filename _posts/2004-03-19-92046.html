Pamela,

Hi! I'm in VA and would be happy to talk with you on or off-list. I've
been homeschooling/unschooling for 14 years here and have done it both under
the homeschool statute and with religious exemption. I have helped lots of
people get started in whatever way they felt comfortable with (curriculum or
not, structure or not). You can definitely learn a lot more about
unschooling from this list and the recommended websites, such as Sandra's,
than any one person could ever share. But I can help you in understanding
VA law, alternatives to testing or alternative tests, and possibly answer
any questions that refer specifically to this state.

Where in the state are you? Some areas have more unschoolers than others;
most locales have support groups, but not all are very open to unschooling
ideas. There are many groups that have a focus, such as various Christian
groups. Have you met any homeschoolers/unschoolers in your area?
