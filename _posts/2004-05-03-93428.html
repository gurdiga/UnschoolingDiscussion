Hello Everyone ~

I don't post much at all........but I just wanted to thank you all for your
honesty and wonderful insights into homeschooling. Most of all your REALNESS
in life and homeschool.....I have been homeschooling for two years. I was
involved with local homeschool groups and just didn't fit in ~ when I found this
list, I pretty much found the support I needed, and left the local groups to
superschooling...... My boys are happy and very well adjusted, and seek their
education with excitement.......(we built a campfire in the front yard today
and discussed what being a cowboy must have been like..... last night we watched
the mystery of the bermuda triangle) We do workbooks with math.....but other
than that, we just explore and learn together........and it's wonderful. This
way of life ROCKS!!

This group of Emporer's in PJ's is the best!

Thanks again ~
Michelle
