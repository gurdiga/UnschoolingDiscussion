ROTFL at the day I've had. I've labored for well over a year now to get me
to relax. It's been an uphill battle each step of the way. Just as I
thought I'd made major progress my 10 yog brought me the days
'schedule'!!!!!! Ack! I feel like I've gone back 5 years!

This was no joke - she made up one of those every 15 minute schedules for
us to follow for our day while we played school! They set me up with a
teacher's desk and all. Moan and groan. :o( So I played along and worse
yet they both liked it. My only hope is that they get really tired of me
holding them to their schedules (tomorrow's has already been brought up to
me - it is to include question asking time between subjects).

Someone tell me this is a phase, pleaseeeeeeez.
In His Service,
