Pursuant to on-line shopping suggestions.

I just got my first copies of the Global Children's Art Gallery Calendar and
I am letting everyone I know, know about it again. I have been telling
people about it for a while now. It is in support of the Natural Child
Project which is a non-profit society promoting a "vision of the world in
which children are treated with dignity, respect, understanding and
compassion."

It is one of my favorite progressive websites. I would encourage everyone
to have a look at the calendar at

http://www.naturalchild.com/calendar_pictures.html

and send some to faraway friends. The calendar looks even better in hand
than on the web site.

Even if you are not interested in the calendar please bookmark the site for
a real treat when it comes to the subject of children.

Merry Christmas, Happy Chanukah, and a Wondrous Solstice.
