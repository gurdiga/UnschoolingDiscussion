This morning, Logan was figuring out size comparisions with strips of bacon. We had three left, so I split one so that we each had one and a half slices. I showed him how it was equal..we both had one big and one little slice, and I explained how the little one was half a slice.

He looks at them and says "I'm like this one" holding up the large slice. "Megan is this one," he then said while holding up the small one. "Oh, yeah?" I asked. "Yeah. And Jeremiah is a big one, and Gracie-baby is a little one!"

Wow. Lessons learned with bacon...who'da thunk?
