IF your child is going thru the teething stage, DO NOT invest in an
appliance for splinting his/her teeth. I have been a Dental Assistant for
almost 2 years, and the Orthodontist at my office says to get the moldable
mouth protectors (the exact ones the kids use for football), follow the
instructions on it, and use that until your child has the adult teeth in for
at least the front 8 (top and bottom 4)
