7 May 2004, at 6:34pm
We registered our 50th family for the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference
today! So far we have over 180 people coming from all over the US and several
from Canada!

The conference will be held August 27 through 29 at the Marriott Hotel in
Peabody, Massachusetts, just 15 miles north of Boston.

Speakers will include Sandra Dodd, Anne Ohman, Pam Larricchia, Ben Lovejoy,
authors Barbara Sher and Valerie Fitzenreiter, FUN Books owner Billy Greer, and
more.

Some of the Funshops that will be available are Making Fairies, Wire Jewelry,
Geocaching, Ham Radio, Raising Guide Dog puppies, American Sign Language,
and, because we'll be right next door to Salem, Witches. (There'll be many more!)
The Family Resource Center will be bringing in Museum Educators from all
over the area to do fun things with us throughout the weekend.

There will be a storyteller, Beth Toolan, and a Children's Music performer,
Robin Blair. We'll be holding the world premiere of Stirling, a movie written,
acted by, and made by homeschooled teens.

PLEASE COME! I think the conference is going to be a blast. Deadline for
Early Bird conference rates is June 1st. The web site should have a big update at
the beginning of next week. Please pass this along to anyone you think will be
interested.

Kathryn Baptista, Conference Wizard


Come to the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference August 27-29 in Peabody, MA!
For more information, go to www.LiveandLearnConference.org
