This is a really neat name site. It's a fun place for statistic nuts
and geography buffs.

Do a search on any name (person's first or last name, town name, etc.)
and get a list of town names with that name as well as other
interesting stats. Lots of great links, maps, etc.

Here's the address: http://www.placesnamed.com

Enjoy!
