5 May 2000, at 12:16am
Tonight's "Nightline" on ABC focused on the ILOVEYOU computer virus that today has done so much damage to computers and systems all over the world.  Mention was made that another virus is now also going around.  It has Very Funny as its subject.   Therefore, I wanted to warn everyone about this additional, potential problem.  DO NOT OPEN EITHER E-MAIL!  It is also recommended that, to be safe, no attachments to any e-mails should be opened right now. 
 
Please be especially careful with any e-messages that contain ILOVEYOU and/or VERY FUNNY as their subjects.  The viruses contained in both e-mails are terribly destructive!
 
Good luck!
 
Linda
