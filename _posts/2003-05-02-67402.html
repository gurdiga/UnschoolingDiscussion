E-mail Announcement: 5/1/03
Are you a shutter bug? Do you take an occasional picture? Are you a
serious photographer? Whatever your level of photography experience, I
would encourage you to consider submitting your best photograph to our
first-ever Photography Contest!
Submit your Color and/or Black & White photographs via email to Homeschool
Connexion by June 1, 2003. All entries will be posted and winners
recognized on the Homeschool Connexion website.
Prizes are yet to be determined, but are sure to be great!
Rules
1. Photographer must be a homeschooled student under the age of 19. No
member of the Homeschool Connexion Team or their immediate family are
eligible to enter.
2. Your photograph must be submitted to webmaster@... in .jpg
format by June 1, 2003. Entry should include photographers name, age,
number of years homeschooling, state of residence, and email address.
3. Entrants may submit photographs in either of the two categories, Black &
White and Color, but may only win in one category.
4. In order to obtain prize, winner must provide a valid mailing address
upon notification of award.

Note: Entries posted on the website will contain first names and location
only - no last names or email addresses will be given out. These are used
for identification and notification purposes for the HC Team only.
We look forward to seeing your photos!
The HC Team
