Unschooling is an odd bird that way.

It's easier than to get up, get folks dressed, fed and at school by 7:05.
Volunteering at school for several hours. Picking up by 3:00 and working on
homework, doing projects, helping study for tests, fixing supper, and signing
papers for the next day. Planning vacations around well...vacations! All the stress
and worry and STUFF.

But then folks think unschooling is easy. It's not. There's a lot of driving
and playing and doing and thinking (and, for me, conferencing---but that's
extra). We're VERY busy---and at the same time relaxed and laid back.

In some ways, school is easier---ship 'em off and forget about them until
3:00 when you then yell about their getting things done. Hands OFF.

Unschooling = hands ON.

It's hard for folks to understand. It's an odd bird.

~Kelly
