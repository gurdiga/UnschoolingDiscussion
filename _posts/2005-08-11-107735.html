Hello all! I'm working on a webzine/comic/animation/games type site
for unschoolers. I was curious if there were anyone (especially
kids) out there who are:

1. Cartoonists
2. Web Designers
3. Flash Animators
4. Musicians (especially whole bands of unschoolers)
5. Writers
6. Game Programmers
7. Other Creative Stuff

Additionally, if there is anyone out there interested in LEARNING
any of those things, let me know. Apart from the music, I've been
doing all those myself, and I just thought it might be better to get
the POV of the target audience and the site's characters involved
rather than just one guy's ideas.

My Art:
http://www.daysevenstudios.com/jaleho/
http://jaleho.deviantart.com/

-Jason
