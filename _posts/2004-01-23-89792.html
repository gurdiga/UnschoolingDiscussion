I haven't read all of the responses yet... I'm just going down the
line..We got here yesterday evening, got settled in.. (Fairfield
Kingsgate Timeshare - AWESOME place!) Today, we got groceries,
explored around the activity center, went to a candy shop and just
hung out. Tomorrow ( weather permitting) we are going to Col
Wlmsbrg. We are SOO excited ! As I was driving down yesterday, it
just hit me.. We are going to the birthplace of America! IT ALL
STARTED "HERE".. (more or less) The impact of the historical
magnatude of VA, Jamestown, Yorktown, etc, just hit me like a
revelation. We are going to where the first settlers lived! We will
be standing on the same ground as Pocahontas, Capt. John Smith.. many
of our founding fathers. It is indeed wonderful.

We have watched "the movie"..looked at maps and brochures, and
generally prepared ourselves to have an awesome time. I wish I had
known about dressing up. I would have had a grand project of making
our own costumes.. I have patterns for period dress for this time.
Oh, well, maybe next time. Thanks for all of the suggestions and
tips. I am taking notes :-)

LOL, you sound like me. My Mom, my 12yro and I all visited Blennerhassett
Island this past fall. Harmon and Margaret Blennerhassett were the people who
were involved with Aaron Burr when he was accused of treason, Blennerhassett
Island is an Island located in the Ohio River on which the Blennerhassetts lived.
We took a ferry over from Parkersburg, WV. I became quite fascinated with the
whole story after attending the Ohio Chautauqua this past summer and hearing
Debra Conner portray Margaret Blennerhassett. Anyhow, as we were walking around
the island which is not very big I was quite taken with the thought that
Aaron Burr had walked across the same lawn.

OK, now you know how easily I am impressed, LOL
