While we are talking about finances and stuff... I'd like to recommend a book that has helped us: "Financial Planning for Dummies" (don't laugh.. it is a good book, really!). I am also currently reading "Learn to Earn" by Peter Lynch. It is a very easy read... written more for the junior high/high school level to explain the basics behind investing in stocks, funds, etc. I will probably make sure my daughter reads it when she is a bit older. I am finding out how crucial it is that our kids' education include personal finance.
