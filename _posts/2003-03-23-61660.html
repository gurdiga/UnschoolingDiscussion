No, I swiped it from the lingo of the English homeschooling terminology.
"Otherwise" as different from the school. "Education otherwise," which I
think is taken from the wording of the law in England. Then Japanese
homeschoolers borrowed it. Then I used it because I needed a one-word
indicator to cover all the kinds of things I had put on that page early on,
and when it was expanded the address stayed the same so links would still
work.

When and if "a Christian viewpoint" keeps unschooling from working, as it
sometimes does, though, then it is less worthy from my radical unschooling
point of view, and in my personal opinion.

Sandra
