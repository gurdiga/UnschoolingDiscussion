out, and then applying it to anyone in the water.

if you're trying to work out as the muscles need more blood. It leads to
cramping.

you shouldn't swim for an hour after eating. Actually, you shouldn't eat
right before working out, that's all. And every person is different, so some
people could!!
It's all about knowing your own body.
