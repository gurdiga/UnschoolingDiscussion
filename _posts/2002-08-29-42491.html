I just saw a strange picture in a magazine of people planning some movie
stunts moving little cars on the ground and making chalk marks. So, you
could act out the rules with props (unless it would seem really stupid
and babyish to your son).

I think you might also take every rule and find a street location, not
too far from your house, where it applies. Then maybe you could drive
the route and talk about each rule. Maybe your son could drive the
route and you could tell each rule at the pertinent point.

There also must be curriculum suppliers who sell materials to public
schools and public school teachers. You could maybe find something on
the web that sells driver's ed materials. Or perhaps a website that
rents videos or DVDs by mail that might have the kind of video you wanted.
