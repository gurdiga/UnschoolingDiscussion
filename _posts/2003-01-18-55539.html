At 16:37 1/18/03 -0500, Shyrley wrote:
>Just got back.
>It was great, amazing, fantastic but boyohboy was it coooooooold!

*That* is an understatement. But, wasn't it GREAT!! What a turnout,
considering how frigid it was. It seems that we were toward the beginning
of the march as it left the Mall. Do you know where you were, Shyrley? I
was marching under a large pink homemade banner that said something like
"Warwick Peace Action Group" and "No Blood For Oil" and "Not in Our
Name." It could've been about 10 or 15 degrees warmer and then that would
have been great. The cops were a little scary at one point though,
right? Did you see when the parade went under the highway how the police
were lined up maybe every six feet or so on either side holding their
night-sticks?

I can't wait to see your pictures. I snapped a few too, but I have no idea
how they'll come out. I just held the camera up as high over my head as I
could and snapped away.

We didn't get home until about 1 in the morning, and it was 2 below zero
when we got back to my car. Brrrrrrr.

Glad to be home!

Marji
