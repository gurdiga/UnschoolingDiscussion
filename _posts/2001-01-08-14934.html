I did this when I was younger. My instructor told me that is I looked for a
new location to beware of anyone who said you'd be a black belt in X number
of years or for X amount of money. Locally one of the big organizations
puts on presentations at the local mall. I'm amazed at the "black belts"
that can do no more than we did & we were only "blue or yellow" belts.
