Alan & Brenda Leonard

22 Dec 2002, at 5:34pm

My son got a Christmas present today from someone at church. He's been
restless about the presents sitting under the tree, so he opened this one
tonight.

She bought him 10 rolls of adding machine tape. He loves cash register
reciepts and is always trying to grab them ahead of me. I never imagined
these would be so much fun! He's been occupied for over an hour making
little paper rolls.

I'm still laughing about the look on his face when he opened the bag!

brenda
