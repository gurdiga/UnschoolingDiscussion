Ann,

I had a landscape company down in Louisiana. . . but I was not very
knowledgable about the ecology of ponds, however, I know the fish ate the
mosquito larvae. . . as far as what I did when the pond got green. . . I
pumped the water out to water the garden and started over :) It was not a
huge pond. . . I guess about 9 by 9 maybe. For the most part it stayed pretty
clear. We pumped it out a couple of times a year I guess. As far as research,
I would probably go to a search engine and type in ponds and go from there.
And I'm sure the library would have some books you could take out on it, and
of course, your local book store. And try your local nursery. . . you may be
able to get some good advice, although some of it would be about chemicals I
suppose. . . maybe Organic Gardening has a web site with some info on it too.
Let me know how it goes. . . I plan on putting in one here at my new house
sometime next year probably.

lovemary
