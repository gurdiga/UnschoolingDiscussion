Google is as reliable (or not) as any part of the internet. Learning to
judge reliability on the internet is an interesting study. You've got to be
skeptical. You've got to think about who it is that's putting this info out
there. You've got to check more than one site, and if you really want
reliability (in publishing, for instance) you'd need to check the info
against other sources. I often use Google or Yahoo as a starting point to
see just what's out there.
