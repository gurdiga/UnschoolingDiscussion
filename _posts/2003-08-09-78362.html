Hi, Ingrid --

On the west coast this Friday, Saturday and Sunday there is a fabulous
inclusive homeschooling conference that many unschooling families enjoy.
It's in Sacramento, CA, which is, I admit, pretty far south for you to
travel unless you are making other stops.

Details are available at http://www.hsc.org (look for Live and Learn
conference)

I think the hotel is fully booked at this point, but nearby hotels,
campgrounds, or friends to crash with are still an option.

The conference has many speakers, including Pat Farenga and our own Pam
Sorooshian. (We even have at least one Canadian, Marty Layne.)

There's going to be dizzying array of stuff, including kid and family
stuff and informal round table discussions. (A new addition.)

It's very energizing to be surrounded by masses of enthusiastic
homeschoolers. For those of us who are too "geographically challenged"
to go to the fabulous all unschooling conference in South Carolina, I
strongly recommend the HSC conference in Sacramento.

Six more days!
Betsy
