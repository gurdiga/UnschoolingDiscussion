If somebody is reading a website like Sandra's and finds something that
just really hits them and they have that urge to broadcast it to the
world, you could try sending it to me for the Highlights list. I just
wouldn't want really lengthy stuff - the point of the highlights is to
keep things pretty short and sweet and non-demanding, but inspirational
and useful.

If it is from Sandra's site, I'd be able to easily figure out if I can
repost it on the highlights list --- but if it is from other sites, I'd
appreciate having permission granted before it is sent to me.

I know that's not the "daily dose" that was being suggested - but just
another idea - to include things off websites as "highlights" along
with posts from the unschooling lists.

-pam
