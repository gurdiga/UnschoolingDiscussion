Hello All !

For those in Brevard County, Florida.

I just wanted to let everyone know that our 4-H club, the South Brevard
Shamrocks, will be starting 2 new projects in 2 weeks: one will be
"model rocketry" and the other will be "rabbits". Anyone interested in
one or both of these projects can contact me at Diane@...
We are now meeting on Friday mornings, 10am for most projects-this seems
to be a better fit for most of us. Our meetings have been switched to
Grant Park (1st St in Grant) for the time being to allow younger
children play time in the playground while the older kids are involved
in their projects. Bring your own drinks and snacks!

Our solar car project and photography project have both been delayed due
to the project leader being out of town for a bit. I am sorry about the
delay-both should be starting early May, but will keep everyone updated.

Our dog care & training project continues.anyone interested, let me
know!
