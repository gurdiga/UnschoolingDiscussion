Stephanie Elms

14 Nov 2002, at 10:09pm

My husband did this for 2 or 3 years in high school and loved it. When I found out
about it I was really bummed that I only played clarinet. There is a corps up in Delaware
that I hope is still around when the boys get older (I also hope that they are interested!).
I love going to the shows...they have a small one at a local high school and we have
taken Jason the last two years. He loved it (although he hates the open stands we had to
sit in!).

Two years ago the National Championships was held at the University of Maryland. Jeff and
I went with Kyle who was only 5 months old (Jason was only 3 and would never have made it
through all the corps). Kyle sat and nursed in the sling the whole time
and managed to even fall asleep finally. We had such a good time....the music was awesome.
Such power.

Stephanie E.
