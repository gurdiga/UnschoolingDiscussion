Hope this is OK to post:

My brother (OK, he's an elementary school teacher) is a TOTAL horror nut + a writer (INFP/ISTP). Funny thing is he's TOTALLY self-taught, but cannot see that his sons would be better off self-teaching their own passions. His eldest is bullied mercilessly because he's an NF too. Soft male in school - not good! :O(

Anyway, he hosts "Flashshot: the Daily Horror Flash Fiction" and since there are so many NF's maybe you want to play too! Write 'em or read 'em.

Here's the gig: Scare someone, give them a chill, in 100 words or less. He posts them on an email list. (Yes, he promo's his e-books that way, just so you know. The writer gets to plug their own ventures/bio too.) I read it each morning while typing missives to you folks. Some are good. Some stink IMHO. He stopped guessing what is good/bad because like all art, 50% love/hate it. I've even sent in three flashes of my own.

He was recommended for nomination for a Stoker award for the service to horror fans (Stoker is like an Oscar in horror).

Let me know if you want the email address to sub to.

My (horror-ible) thots
Tim T
