Wonder if he knows my cousin Rob Oakes, who airbrushed T-shirts in
Daytona for years. He was given the rap of "beach bum" within the
family; yet I envied him. He did what he wanted and found a way to
make it productive. Maybe in some way he planted or nurtured that seed
within me. Now I wonder why most people don't pursue their dreams and
live the life they want to live. Oh, yeah, they went to school and/or
had parents who "broke" them of that dream.

My own Mom constantly discouraged me from pursuing arts as more than a
hobby because "you can't make any money doing that." I'm finally
beginning to explore those ways of being creative.....thanks to the
unschooling mentality I have been developing for the past several years.
