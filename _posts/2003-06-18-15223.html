Hey Alyce.

DragonTree HomeLearners meets weekly for play and conversation. Our
primary goal, as a group, is to provide plenty of freedom for our
children to have time to develop their own games, make complex
creations, explore new relationships, and develop close friendships. We
believe strongly in the value of free play and we try not to schedule
too many activities for the children on park days so that they can have
long uninterrupted hours to play. However, some of the activities that
have been offered, at the urging of the children, are: candlemaking,
papermaking, bubble shapes, origami, woodworking , electronics, and
soldering. We meet every Wednesday at El Dorado Park in Long Beach. We
generally meet from around noon until dark during the winter and we
often stay late and have an evening cookout and campfire during the
summer. For further information, contact Pam Sorooshian at
pamsoroosh@... or Jocelyn Vilter at jocelyn@... .
