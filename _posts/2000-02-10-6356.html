Sandy,
I too have noticed that there are a lot of "pagan" families on this
list.... I myself am Christian. Unschooling is a style of learning where the
parent lets the child learn in a way that is best for them (very
oversimplified I know). I know many, many different types of people that
unschool that represent many different religions (or have no religion at
all). My point is that unschooling is not a religious style, it is a
learning style (however, some particular religious groups may be attracted to
unschooling).
