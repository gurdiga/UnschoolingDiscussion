coyote's corner

13 Apr 2003, at 9:12pm

Late this past December, I came down w/ a cold. It developed into a cough. a very bad cough.
One morning I awoke with a horrible pain in my back right side. I thought it was my kidney. Brianna & I were at my doctor's office 5 minutes before she got there!
The coughing had caused muscle spasms - severe!!
I was told to spend no more than 60 - 75 minutes per day out of bed. In bed - I was to stay flat....as in no sitting up. That my friends is hell. There is nothing to do for 23 hours per day flat on your back (well, there is , but I home school :)
How long can you hold a book up??
Even watching TV was hard and very boring!
If there is a hell - this would definitely be a version!
Janis
