The Audubon Society and Cornell Lab of Ornithology sponsor a backyard bird
count every year. You just count the birds in your backyard (or elsewhere),
and submit it via their website at:

http://birdsource.cornell.edu/gbbc

Just thought some of you might be interested in doing this with your kiddos.
