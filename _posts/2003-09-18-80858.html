My oldest son enjoyed Tales of a Fourth Grade Nothing by Judy Blume
as well as the sequels (the ones with "Fudge" in the title).
They were a little school oriented for my taste (talk about not
wanting to be seen walking to school with the "kindergarten babies"
and such) but my boys laughed hysterically and could relate to the
many exasperating little brother stories as well as to the more
heartwarming parts where Peter realizes that Fudge isn't so bad
after all.
