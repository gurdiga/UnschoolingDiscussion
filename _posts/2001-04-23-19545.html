I've had several ISP's, including our local phone co.
and a small local ISP business, and of all six I've
tried, the two I had the most trouble with were a free
one and AOL! Unless your computer is brand new just
as the technology comes out, it is unlikely to be
beyond the capabilities of most ISP's for more than a
month or two... the technologies move fast, and they
have to keep up if they want to survive. Good luck!
