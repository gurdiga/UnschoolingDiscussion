What you might have already totally done, but I would try:

Needle might be bent a little.

Bobbin tension might be too tight (there might be a screw you could loosen
just a little, 1/4 turn on mine)

bobbin might be in backwards/upsidedown

Try a different bobbin (in case this one is just too tight or tangled or
something)

clean out the bobbin area totally for extra thread or lint or stickiness

I would try rethreading everything, and try with different thread and bobbin.
The maybe try changing the needle.

I hope it's something easy.

Sandra
