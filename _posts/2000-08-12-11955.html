And ..... to add to Shelley's great post ......
As the Mommy of two beige and white hooded girlie rats... Do some
reading up first ... they prefer to be in pairs or larger groups ....
boys sit still and don't run all over the computer desk while you're
reading e-mail .... but boys smell more and I like the inquisitive,
active nature of females.

All the single rats we've known have not been very happy compared to the
ones who live in same sex pairs / groups.

They are much more entertaining than hamsters or gerbils (we've had both
),
more like little dogs who eat fruit and veggies and rat feed.
