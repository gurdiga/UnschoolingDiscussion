The kids and I saw a commercial for a show that is on CBS this fall in
the U.S.. (I'm looking to see if its shown anywhere else but have not
found any info yet). Its called Kid Nation. Apparently, they put 40
kids ages 8-15 on an old mining town in New Mexico for 40 days.
Children only, no adults.

My 7 year old was so excited to see the preview. It will be fun to
see how the kids live, build their world and relate with each other.
I'm sure we will have great conversations about the show when it starts.

Here is a link about it if hasn't heard of it yet..

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kid_Nation

I'm not sure if I like the "Gold Star" thing (I think its just the
"schooly" gold star thing) but I'm sure my kids would like the $20,000
that each star is worth.. bwg

It would be pretty neat to see an unschooled kid on there!
