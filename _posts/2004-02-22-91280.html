Someone was asking about dealing with relatives who interfere or disapprove of unschooling...there was a thread awhile back because my mother was asking that my daughter Sarah, age 7, NOT email her as Sarah prefers but hand write her a letter. Sarah did agree to it, but there was a big go around between my mother and I over it and unschooling.

I just thought I'd post that it's been some two months now I think...maybe just six weeks...and my mother has STILL not written Sarah back.

Sarah did send a handwritten letter to Paul's mother, who has written her back. She's still emailing, but thankfully hasn't brought up the fact that my mother hasn't written.
