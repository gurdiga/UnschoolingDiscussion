There is no "option" key on PC keyboards. Hold the "alt" key, then press
0162 on the numeric keypad, release the "alt" key and you will get this: �

Even easier, if you open the "system tools" folder (under "programs,
accessories in win95/98) you will see an icon for "Character Map." It will
let you easily view and select the printable characters for any font. You
can also run C:\WINDOWS\CHARMAP.EXE if you can't find the icon.
