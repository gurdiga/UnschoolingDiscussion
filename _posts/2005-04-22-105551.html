luv_my_three_kids

22 Apr 2005, at 9:13am

I was in the other room and my three kids were in the living room.
Things had gotten quiet so I went to go check on them. They had gotten
out the art supplies and were making crafts with construction paper.
My youngest,3 1/2, was making a barbie bed according to her using the
construction paper. Now my other two were were making something too
but I decided I wasn't going to get mad over it. They had taken
construction paper, cut out a house, grass, sun, and cloud and used a
glue stick to glue the cut outs onto the living room window. I asked
them why they made the picture on the window instead of using paper
and my oldest,7, said cause the window was boring. I have to give her
that one since the view from our window is a giant wall from the
building across the street. I took a picture of the picture they made
and said "here, let's put the cut outs on paper before the glue dries
and this way we can save the picture." I was also impressed to see
what my daughter used for glitter on the house. We use rock salt and
have a salt grinder, so she put glue on the house where she wanted
glitter and sprinkled salt over it. I wouldn't have thought to use
salt for glitter. I just had to say the nice creative moment we had in
our house yesturday.
Elaina
