The San Diego Good Vibrations Unschooling Conference is only 3 months away! If
you're interested in attending, check out the website here:
http://goodvibrationsconference.com/
Register and book your hotel room today. It really is so much fun to stay at the
hotel if you're able to. There's lots of really fun Funshops happening. My
daughter, Olivia is hosting a Ninjago Lego Funshop. There's also going to be a
full art room hosted by Spramani E'Laun and Nature of Art Kids. Many great

Dodd, Ronnie Maier, and Tiffani Bearup. The chat circles are a great intimate
opportunity to ask questions about unschooling . And, you're sure to have lots
of fun at the many activities planned; beach potluck, pool party, surfing
lessons, artisan bazaar, & more!

If you have any great ideas for a Funshop, something your kids are passionate
about or that you are passionate about be sure to sign up under the Funshop
page.
