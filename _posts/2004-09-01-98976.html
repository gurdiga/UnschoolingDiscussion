1 Sep 2004, at 4:30pm
I'm still recovering a bit, so this won't be long, but I wanted to mention a
few quick things about the Live and Learn Conference in Peabody, MA.

It was amazing! Very cool people everywhere you looked -- 573 of them!!!!!!!

Lots of connections made, great speakers, a fun raffle (thanks to Kelly
Lovejoy), and relatively few glitches. I got to hang out with Barbara Sher!

240 people went on the WHale Watches, and no one missed the boat! (There
was, however, some vomiting. Let's see, take lots of kids from home, have them
stay up late and eat unfamiliar food and sleep in a strange place, then at the
end put them in a boat on the Atlantic. What WAS I thinking??!!) However,
we saw whales, and that was COOL!

I think people had a good time, and the hotel was very happy to have us.

Kathryn
