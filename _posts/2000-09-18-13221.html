Just a quick comment based on my own experience getting
discounts.......
Sometimes it's easier to ask for an * educator's * discount. Not a
teacher's discount 'cause they will almost always ask to see your union
card or state/provincial teachers card.
A lot of places don't want to give a discount to Hs'ers justifying it by
saying that any parent could ask for the same discount whether they are
homeschooling or not.
Sometimes a support group membership card is good enough, sometimes it's
not.
