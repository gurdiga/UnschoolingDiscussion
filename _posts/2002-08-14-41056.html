I've been noticing some posts today that might not have been written if the
poster had read all their mail first. The one I'm thinking of was about
letting Ned stay. As far as I can tell, the "Ned Problem" has already been
resolved.

One way that I use to avoid this kind of thing is to read through all the
posts first, saving any that I think I'll want to respond to. Then when I
go back to respond, I usually delete at least half of them because someone
else has said what I wanted to say, or the issue has been resolved one way
or another.
