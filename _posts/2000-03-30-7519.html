My brother was diagnosed with irritable bowel syndrome and
for 5 years was unsuccessfully treated by doctors. I don't
recommend anyone self treating except when all options have
been exhausted and no relief occurs. After doing a great
deal of research, and knowing to consult a doctor who was
experienced with alternative herbal medicine, he found
relief from many of his symptoms and he is much happier. He
will always live with it but traditional medicine did not
work for him. He is now able to earn a living. I am all for
self treating for minor illnesses. I know of one family who
are treating their son for ADHD with herbals and they love
the results without the ritalin induced state he was once
in.
