Hi, Karin!!!!

Sic is used when quoting someone else's misspelling or questionable use of
a word. It's the editor/QA person in me (it's what I do for a living as a
medical transcriptionist). Since the word "truly" was misspelled as
"truely," the editor in me used [sic] to show that I was quoting the
person accurately and acknowledging the misspelling. It's pretty goofy now
that I think of it; I mean, who cares, for Heaven's sake? But, it's in me
blood; I can't help myself! Maybe there's a support group out there for
folks like me who edit everything! :-/

I hope you're well!

Marji
