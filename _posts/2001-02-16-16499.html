When I checked my email this morning, the first message I opened was
from a good friend with the subject line reading: ER!!!!!! Oh no,
something happened to one of her kids or something!

Oh no, what really happened is that she watched the show ER and had
to immediately contact me to question my decision to not give the
baby the HIB shot at the hospital. Not seeing the show, but reading
her email and the posts out here, the propaganda would suggest that
parents who don't immunize are stupid and have, in effect, killed
their children.

She also stated that the there were commercials for immunization and
a news report on her local news after ER about a child dying from
meningitis & lack of immunization.

I asked her how many drama shows, commercials, network news reports
or local newspaper articles she's seen about the adverse affects of
immunization or the death of a child DUE to immunization. Or the
countless times, in the medical experiences of my husband and myself,
the doctor didn't "always know best".

Thanks to the many who have posted on this subject and raised my
awareness re: immunizations and have encouraged me to continue my
research into it!

Chris
