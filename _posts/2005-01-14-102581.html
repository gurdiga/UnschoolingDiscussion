Hi all,

My name is Barb, aka Skateboard Mom. I'm unschooling with my 8 year old son in Laguna
Beach, CA. It took me a bit to find this unschooling path, but we're lapping it up now. We
tried school but it was actually a pack of teachers (!) who talked us into
homeschooling.

We're hoping to hook up with some of you--we travel quite a bit. I'm a children's author
and will be taking my son on the road this year as I do the book tour, so we're hoping to
meet other unschoolers en route. My son is into Legos, Legos, and did I mention Legos?
He also reads like the dickens. I skateboard. He doesn't.

I'm supposed to be on NPR today. Here's the link if anyone's interested:
http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=4283628

ciao for now,

skateboard mom
