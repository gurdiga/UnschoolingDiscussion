Lemme 'splain. On Sunday after church, we had a few things to do at
the church and at the parsonage before we could go home, so my kids
waited and played with some neighborhood kids while we did those
things. There were a five year old boy and two year old girl, and
sidewalk chalks. We left them playing and did our chores, then came
back and chatted with the mom for a sec while the kids wrapped up.

By this time, they had moved to the grass where they were playing
tag. My "socially clueless" 10 year old boy slowed down his own pace
so the littler kids could catch him, and when the two year old got
into chasing and pushing, he let himself fall down with great
alarming shouts of fear, and be romped on. It was heart warming to
watch his sensitivity to others come out in his play like that.
