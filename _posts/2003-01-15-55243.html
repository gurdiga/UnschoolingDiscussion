The day we were there, we had to choose between Oklahoma, Lion King,
Les Mis, and a few others. It was tough. If we ever get the
opportunity to return, we'll try to go to a show every night!

During the show, I was worried the entire time (Egads - I'm going to
be walking around downtown NYC at 11:30PM with my kids. . . etc.) but
it turned out great. We took a cab to Penn Station (it was all of
about 2 blocks - I'm sure the cab driver was pleased about the long
fare :-) and then hopped on a train to Trenton (we were staying with
friends there) All clean, well-lit, train and station filled with
theater-goers, cops everywhere. . . sure wish I had known that BEFORE
the show - I would have enjoyed it so much more. . . .

After the show, my ds looked at me and said, "If I lived in NY, I'd
go to a show every night"

I said, "YUP - so would I - we'd need a well-paying job, though" :-)
