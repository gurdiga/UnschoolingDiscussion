The VHEA conference will be in Charlottesville, VA on April 21, 2001.

Details are available on-line at: www.vhea.org

FUN Books will be there and we will be bringing our discount bins of
overstocks, clearance, and "scratch and dent" items that will have
discounts of up to 50%. In addition, we will be offering 10% off the
marked prices of just about everything we bring to the conference
(including items in the discount bins that have already been marked
down!)

Unfortunately, we will have limited space and can't bring everything
we have in our catalog. If there is an item that you have been
wanting to see in "real life" or something that you know you want to
purchase, send us your list. We will make every effort to bring the
items you request and we will even hold them in your name until at
least noon, so that you don't have to worry about us running out of
the items you want. If you like, you can place an order in advance
and pick it up at the conference so you won't have to pay any
shipping costs. You can see our selection at http://www.FUN-Books.com

Send your requests by e-mail to fun@.... If you would like
to special order any items not listed in our catalog, and to help
make sure we have what you want in stock, please place your order by
4/16/01.
