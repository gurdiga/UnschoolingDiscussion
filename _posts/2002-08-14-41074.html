Hi everyone,

My name is Sherri and I home educate my 10 y/o dd. She has never been to a
ps and has never expressed the desire to give it a try. Thank heaven. I have a
15y/o ds who happens to attend ps because he lives with his Dad. I wish I could
change that but his Dad is dead set against "homeschooling" because it will not
give a child the education that ps does. HA HA!!!
I recently quit my job to be at home full time. This is a scary experience. I'm not
sure that we can financially survive on just one income. I have been searching on the net
for a legitimate work from home opportunity but I'm leary of most everything I find.
Does anyone out there know of something that I could check out? Any suggestions
would be greatly appreciated. By the way, I live in Louisville, Ky.

Sherri

for a legitimate work from home opportunity but I'm leary of most everything I find.

There are many companies that you can work for independantly. I am a distrubutor for Pampered Chef and Longaberger. I am able to work from home - but do home parties about once a week - and an occasional fair. Check out this website: http://www.thewahmconnection.com/ - it gives different ideas about work at home opporuntities. Whatever you do, there is no easy get rich quick way :) Mostly it is finding something you are good at - and finding a way to market yourself...
