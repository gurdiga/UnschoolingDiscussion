Teresa,
You haven't been under a rock. Alot of people haven't heard of Robert
Jordan, including me until a friend turned me onto him. He is a writer of
Fantasy Books. The Wheel of Time is one of his series. If you like fantasy,
you will love his books. They are all about a 'light side' and a 'dark
side' (kind of like Star Wars but not in outerspace with spaceships) and each
has powers. There is, of course, a struggle for good to win over evil. They
are written for adults, although teenagers usually really enjoy them also.
However, they are very thick books, with alot of heavy reading. They are too
difficult for your 8 and 10 year olds, (unless they are geniuses! *smile*).
My 17 year old loves them, he has caught up with me, on reading book 6,
however he does have more time to read then I do. My 13 year old is trying
to read book 1, but I think he is going to give up and wait until he is
older. He is
having trouble getting through such a thick book. You may enjoy them though!
Tami
