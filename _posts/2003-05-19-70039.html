Yipppeee! This has been our first year at unschooling. We went for our
first homeschool assessment and everything went very well! The woman
actually said I had too much information and kept very good records of
what my two boys and I had done for unschooling. I am SO relieved that
the process went so well.
When I first wrote in to this group it was with a sad heart. My 15 year
old has dyslexia and being at public school had caused a mood disorder.
He has learned more in the passed few months than he did the last 4
years at public school. I am so happy. We are all so happy. He is calm
95% of the time. He is able to learn and enjoy it at the same time. I am
so grateful for this site. It was what I had prayed for and was guided
to, I believe. Thank you. Michele
