I babysit my 7-mo grandson and unschool our 16-yo daughter. I subscribe
to about seven lists, all but one in digest form.

Digests are a lifesaver, IMHO. I read through them every morning with a
cup of tea (this is essential LOL). If I see a post which I would like
to read again/ponder, or one to which I want to respond, I drag them
into one of two folders I created: "To Read" or "To Reply."

It works for me!
