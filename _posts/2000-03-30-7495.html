Ann,
Isn't it funny how kids are always suprising us? Just when you needed it
too. I have also been worried about my 11yod lately. She didn't seem to be
interested in anything that I saw as learning. Then we decided to do a
nature journal like Charlotte Mason recommends. Well, she is a different
kid! The first day, she decided to write a beautiful poem for the front page
of her journal. [ This from a kid who swears she hates to write]! And even
though we always go for a daily walk - the dog insists-, now she's eager to
go to see what she will find to write about in her journal! She also
informed me the other day that she really doesn't need to know all that math
& history that I have been trying to teach her! She's going to be a writer
and an environmentalist! I'm just happy she found something she really wants
to do. I guess there is hope for all kids if we Trust! jackie
