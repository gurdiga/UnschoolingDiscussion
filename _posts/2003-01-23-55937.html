It's a Japanese Anime show, on the "Adult Swim" program.....which is on the

Adult Swim is one of those programs with the warnings up front about
violence, rude behavior and language etc...
We love it!! hehe
InuYasha is fairly tame...it's about a 1/2 demon 1/2 human and a girl that is
a reincarnation of a demon slayer. They go about slaying demons and having
adventures, it's somewhat dark at times, very mystical/supernatural and
interesting. I love Inuyasha's long, white hair. He's decidedly immature at
times though.
