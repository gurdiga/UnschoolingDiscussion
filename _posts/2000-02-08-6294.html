Hi All,

Has anyone read this book? - Roots, Shoots, Buckets, and Boots by Sharon
Lovejoy. I checked it out from the library last night and it is just
gorgeous. It's about gardening with children. The illustrations are just
yummy and the gardening ideas sound like they are magical. The sunflower
house is described, along with several other cool things. I think we are
going to try to grow the Moon Tent. It was fascinating to read about night
time gardening. I'm going to go buy the book tonight. I just LOVE finding a
really terrific book!
