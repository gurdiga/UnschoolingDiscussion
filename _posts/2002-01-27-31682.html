My two have read since they were 4 or younger so I've never had the
problem of enticing a reluctant reader, but I have had to entice ds
over to a different subject matter. There are only so many times that
you can read a Nintendo Adventures book before they fall apart. My
trick, leave other equally enticing books lying around. They will
pick them up and read.
I was so happy to find the Animorphs series. Ds had read all of his
Nintendo books over at least twenty times when I saw "Animorphs" on a
book. I bought it immediately. Mighty Morphin' Power Rangers were all
the rage then.
