Hi, Luanne!

This list is for unschooling support. In general you'll see this comment when
someone says, "I make my kids do this or that because it's educational." Most
unschoolers believe that, at root, this is not unschooling. Dividing life into
educational and junk categories is fundamentally not our philosophy.

If you post that you so divide the world and love it and so forth, yeah, you'll
probably hear that it's not unschooling. If you post that you have questions,
you'll probably get support, but if you only want support for declaring some
things junk, this is not the best place.

Hope this makes sense.

:-) Diane
