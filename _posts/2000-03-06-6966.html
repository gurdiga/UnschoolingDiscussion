My older two kids are 15 months apart too, the oldest being my daughter then
my son. It was a lot of work to begin with but lots of laughs too. My older
two kids are like the best of friends tho and hardly ever fight. I have
always been totally amazed with this as my sister and I hated each other
until we were in our 20's. One of my favorite memories of having a baby and
a toddler was my daughter being so curious about the vaseline I used on the
diaper table. It got incredibly quiet one day while I was reading and I knew
I had to investigate. I go into my sons room and there's my daughter up on
his diaper table slathering herself us with vaseline. I was worried about
her falling off so I rushed over to pick her up and couldn't.... she was too
darn slippery. I had to rush off and get a towell to wrap her up in to carry
her. I laughed the whole time. (I think it took me the better part of a
week to get all the vaseline out of the tub.) Cherished memories.

We had a Vaseline moment when Andy was about 1. We accidentally shut the cat

Here is another tale;

WHEn Ave was two we were visiting my mom and had set up a makeshift crib
in the very small spare bedroom. We listening for her little girl noises as
she was getting ready for her afternoon nap..and then suddenly it became
VERY quiet. Too quiet.
I became suspicious and crept upstairs to find that she had reached over
