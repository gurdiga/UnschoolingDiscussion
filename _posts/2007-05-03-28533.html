Erica at 2007-05-03 06:01:09 +0300 said:

I have been pretty much unschooling our boys for the past couple
months. I honestly just started it and didn't really say anything to
DH. He would ask what we did in a day and I would tell him what we
did. So after dinner tonight he asked me "so, are you unschooling the
boys now?" I almost fell out of my seat! I said "yeah, pretty much".

He then says, "I've read about it and it seems like a good concept as
long as you stay on top of it". I told him that I plan on living a
full life full of experiences with them (and him!). He then asked to
read one of my unschooling books! I was so happy! I gave him a
couple to start him off and Sandra's "Moving a Puddle" is next to our
bed for him to read too.

I guess I can now say we are officially unschooling!

Erica
