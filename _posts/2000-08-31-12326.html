Hi All,
I just had to share this.  My friend who is an avid unschooler, just lent me a book titled, 'The Unschooling Handbook' by Mary Griffith.  It is a wonderful book!  I couldn't put it down!  I have already read half of the book today!  I recommend it to anyone who is unschooling, thinking about unschooling, unschools but has doubts about their kids not learning anything, READ IT!!!!  I wish that I would have found this book a long time ago!
Has anyone else on this list read it?
