about it. It is a web site where you play 20 questions with a computer. My

The first computer game I ever played, in 1972 or 73, was one of those, on
the mainframe computer (all there was in those days, punch cards for
programming) at the University of New Mexico. There were so few people playing on the
computer (and one of the least of them, I always was tagging along with a
smarter, mathier friend) that we could tell whose some of the "answers" (new
questions) were.

It's an ancient and honorable computer program (about as ancient as they can
be <bwg>).
