Eleven REALLY COOL Things about the Live and Learn Unschooling Conference

1. Barbara Sher, author of Wishcraft and other books, and PBS Pledge time
star, is doing her workshop especially for us. In addition, because she is so
intrigued by Unschoolers, she is staying for the WHOLE conference! (She usually
jusy goes into towns for the day.)

2. The World Premiere of Stirling, a comedy-mystery feature-length movie
written, produced, directed by, and starring homeschooled teens.

3. True Story Theater will take our experiences and ideas and turn them into
a performace for us.

4. Amazing speakers like Sandra Dodd, Anne Ohman, Pam Laricchia, Ben Lovejoy,
Valerie Fitzenreiter, etc.

5. Really cool stuff in goodie bags for attendees.

6. The Talent Show! Unschoolers are a talented bunch!

7. Late Night Rooms for Folks who can't get enough! There will be two program
rooms open until 3 AM (when the Security guy goes home) -- one for adults
(and kids with them) and one for Teens.

8. Concert by Robin Blair (Followed the next morning by a Funshop.)

9. Still some space in a whale watch with lots of cool unschoolers, at an
astonishing price.

10. Come see how Julian's chainmaille shirt came out, and try it on.

11. The possibility that Kathryn just might reprise her performance of The
Daisy Song.
