Brenda,

In Calif. you simply turn over the form they give you to list their shot
records and there is a small box that says that you can sign there if you
consider yourself exempt for personal or religious reasons. No one ever
turns over the form to see that it is there because if you immunize you
simply sign the front portion. I would imagine most if not all states
have this waiver...she just has to keep asking for it.
