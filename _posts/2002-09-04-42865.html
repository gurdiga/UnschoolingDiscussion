The next issue of Paths of Learning (www.pathsoflearning.net) will focus on
the question Does America Really Love Its Children?

As part of our discussion, we are soliciting brief essays (around 250 words)
from children/teens in response to the following questions:

For younger kids: What would make your world more friendly and fair to
kids?

For older kids/teens: What would it be like to live in a society that was
child-friendly, that put kids first?

Submissions should include a first name and an age and can be emailed to
pathseditor@... by Sept. 30.
