When our now 24 yo boy was 9 he got into re-enacting/LARP, which
in turn led to many of his passionate interests and a few brief
side trips into things like archeology (hot dusty work that
doesn't let you got to the bathroom in comfort when you want to
according to him at age 14). This site has some links that will
get you started on Civil War re-enacting, which by my memory is
the most active of the "real life" groups in Iowa:
http://sunsite.utk.edu/civil-war/warweb.html#reenactors

Your SCA kingdom is here, if that seems more appealing (it was
briefly for our boy, though he moved on to full fantasy LARP):
http://calontir.sca.org/
