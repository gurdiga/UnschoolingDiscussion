Regarding this comment: "I have been on the other list for over 8 months,
and never has anybody there
ever criticized the way anybody unschools. I was shocked that you all think
your way is the only way and everybody else is wrong. Laurie"

There is no consensus on this list as to what is the "only" way. It's
nowhere near that organized. People here represent a spectrum of beliefs and
ways of life. But we are often challenged by some members to think about
what might happen if we let go and let the kids make more of their own
choices. You shouldn't take it as criticism, but just as an invitation to
examine beliefs you take for granted. Some of the suggestions here I have
rejected as unworkable for my family (ahem, no one is going for three weeks
without washing their hair in my house) but others have made me re-examine,
and change, some things I have believed all my life just out of habit.
