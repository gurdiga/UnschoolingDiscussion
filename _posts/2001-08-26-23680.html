The Hot Topic of Home Schooling

By CBN News
August 23, 2001

Amy Hollingsworth discovers a road less traveled in homeschooling.

CBN.com - Amy is a Regent University Graduate in counseling. She has
written numerous articles on home schooling that have been published
in "Home Education Magazine." She's also had her work appear on
various web sites. She wrote a chapter for a book called "Christian
UN-schooling." Next week, Amy begins teaching psychology at Mary
Washington college while continuing tohome school her two children,
Jonathan, age 9, and Emily, age 7.

Amy says the "hot topic" that everyone including "Time Magazine" and
"ABC News" is talking about right now is that home schooling is no
longer a movement of the religious right. Lots of people, both
religious and non-religious, are doing it.
