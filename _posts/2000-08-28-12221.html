Hi Dawn,

Love and Logic is shorthand for a book called Parenting with Love and Logic
by Foster Cline and Jim Fay. The book is based on the premise that our goal
is to raise responsible children into adulthood, and that we do this by
giving our children as much control over their lives as we are comfortable
with and the freedom to experience the consequences of their actions.
