My little eighty-one year old mama is stopping by for coffee on her way
to a painting session with her friends. She took up painting last year
and is having a wonderful time.

My little eleven year old boy is happily "drawing" artsy thank you's for
gifts he got for his birthday. I didn't remind him to, he just got his
drawing stuff out and everyone is getting an original work of art with a
brief note.

My dog is asleep in my rocking chair.

That cat's are sunbathing in the window.

The garden is calling my name, softly.<g> Wait, it's getting louder!

There are Robins building a nest on the ladder hanging along the side of
my garage. Mrs. Robin has a line of mud across her breast where she's
been pressing and molding her nest.

House Sparrows have moved into the ends of the clothes line poles.

A squirrel is dangling by his toes stealing sunflower seeds from the bird
feeder.
