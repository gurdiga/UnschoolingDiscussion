Hi Folks,

My son Rowyn is 6 years old. He enjoys writing his name in all capitals
but will start with the R small and make each letter progressively
bigger. He says the bigger the letter is the louder it is. I think
that is really neat. Of course I have all the paper where they are
suppose to make the letters even but I haven't introduced that yet. At
some time I may tell him that I love the way he writes his name but at
times it might be easier to read if the letters were all the same size.
I know he can make them all the same size though because I've seen him
do it. He's just doing it this way for fun.
