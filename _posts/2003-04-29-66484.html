In addition to magazines, you might also like Linda Dobson's book, The
Homeschooling Book of Answers. It includes lots of info about relaxed
homeschooling and unschooling. The book is divided into 83 questions
with 2 or 3 answers from experienced homeschoolers, including Sandra and
others on this list. Typical answers are less than a page long, so it's
an easy book to pick up and dip into. You could even bookmark the
questions that come up over and over. <g>
