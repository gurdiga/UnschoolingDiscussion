11 Mar 2002, at 11:31pm

A member of our HEM-Networking list posted the following this evening:

>After the rumors that we then thought turned out to be "only" UK, I hunted
>and found a yahoogroups for list owners and subscribed. Well, tonight they
>sent out word that YahooGroups in the USA will also be down, from Friday
>night thru Sunday morning (so at least it's supposed to be less time than
>UK).
>
>If you want to see the official message, go to the home page of yahoo
>(http://www.groups.yahoo.com) it's a service announcement on the right hand
>side.

I followed the link, clicked on another link "for more information,"
and found this notice:

>Dear Yahoo! Groups Members,
>
>The Yahoo! Groups service will be down Friday March 15 9:00 PM PST
>(GMT-8) as we move our servers to a new facility. We expect the
>service to be back up the morning of Sunday March 17.
>
>During this time the web site will be unavailable and email will not
>be delivered. (Some users may experience email non-delivery notices,
>but all email should be delivered once service has resumed.) Please
>note: once the service is back up, there will be email delays due to
>backlog. We expect these delays to last no longer than 1 day. Please
>do not re-send email to your group as it will only add to delays.
>
>We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
>
>The Yahoo! Groups Team

Nice to have a little advance warning about this.
