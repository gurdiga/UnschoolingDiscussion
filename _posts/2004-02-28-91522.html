My children aren't yet old enough to have to register (8 in PA), so I haven't yet had to deal with the laws. My understanding is that it varies greatly depending on where in PA you live. In Pittsburgh where I am, it is very easy as I understand it, there are several unschooling friendly evaluators and no one wants to see your portfolio. If you are considering the Pittsburgh area, the email list for the homeschooling group that I am in is palscommunity@yahoogroups.com. It is non-religious, tending toward the crunchy and has many people unschooling.
