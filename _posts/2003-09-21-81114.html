Netflix is fast. Also - you CAN see their listings without signing up.
Here's how:

On their front screen (netflix.com) go to the very very bottom where
there is a little line of words/links just above the copyright and
terms of use information. On the far right it says "categories" - click
on that.

They do have IMAX movies.
