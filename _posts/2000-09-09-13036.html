We are part of a coop--we meet every other Friday for half a day. Parents or
(usually) older teens share their passions and/or skills. I taught hands on
art history last session--this time i'm doing Cool Art Stuff--art history,
science and art and more! One of the dads isa chemist and he's doing
chemistry. We also have Spanish from a native born Peruvian, watercolor,
German, swing and ballroom dance, baseball skills, creative wrtiting,theater
for kids, Harry Potter stuff. We plan on having a thing where kids can share
their passions with everyone--my dd wants to demo dog obedience, another
kids wants to show his claymation work, another wants to play the bagpipes.
It is neat to see how this evolves...
