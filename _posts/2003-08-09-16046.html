Home School Conference



Mark your calendars for Labor Day Weekend!



After more than a year of planning, the National Christian
Homeschool Conference (NCHC) will take place on August 28-30, 2003. This
Christ-centered event will be held at the beautiful Orlando World Center Marriott Resort
and Convention Center. A combination family retreat and homeschool
enrichment conference, the NCHC will feature lectures, workshops and a wonderful
exhibit hall.

Many well-known homeschool experts have collaborated to make this
new event a reality, including Dave Exley, producer of the Florida Homeschool
Convention (FHC) from 1991-2000, and Rev. Ron Heffield, registrations manager
for the FHC from 1994-2000.



Registration Information:

<A HREF="http://www.christianhomeschoolconference.com/">www.christianhomeschoolconference.com</A>



Contains all details on Workshops and Hotel Information. (Hotel rate will be
$105 per night.)

Make it a family vacation in sunny Orlando!
