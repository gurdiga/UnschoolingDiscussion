Has anyone read the book called: and the children played by Patricia Joudry? It was published in 1975 but her children were unschoolers in the mid 50's. It is a riot of a book and a crazy, fun, loving adventure of unschooling. It is however out of print and I haven't seen it on used sites for less than $45. for a book that once sold for $6.95. If you ever come across it grab it up, it is a delightful book. So, I guess people were unschooling way back when...Carol

I HAVE that book. It was one John Holt really enjoyed and recommended. I can even remember when GWS was offering $3 for used copies to recycle back to readers when new ones no longer available. Oh, for the days.....

Yes, if you see it in a garage sale snap it up.

And if ANYONE ever sees a book called "The Children on the Hill" let me know and *I* will buy it!!!
