In California if you use the Private School Affidavit (R-4) method of
being legal, you must have a name for your school. Some people have
fancy sounding acronyms that stand for things like Freedom and Fun for
.kid's name.Academy. Other folks use their street name, or something
else that the kids like. I hear that the use of Hogwart's had to be
sadly discouraged by numerous parents as being a definite red flag for
officialdom.
