Pamela,
I know this is not what you are really looking for, but in May I
joined a group Hogwarts Summer Correspondence School. They are a group of
homeschool moms doing things for their kids through the summer. Yes, I know
it has the word "school" in it. But Bj has had a lot of fun with this. The
first morning he woke up to envelopes scattered from the front door all the
way to our bed. They were from Hogwarts, some from Hagrid, some from Pro.
Dumbledore, some from Pro. Snape and all the rest of the teachers at
Hogwarts. He made a Owl post bag that we hang in a tree in our yard and at
night our owl picks it up, in the bag is things he has done in the day.
They(the owls) send more things for him to do or every flavor beans or maybe
chocolate frogs. He made Hydro vomit as one of his potions one day. He has
had a lot of fun with this. And he does only the things he wants too. As
some of this is very schooly. He gets email's from some of the teachers.
We have had alot of fun with this, and I was thinking that you might want to
check it out.
