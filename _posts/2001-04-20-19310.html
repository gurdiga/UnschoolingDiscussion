A computer tech friend told me that it's ONLY when the file is "opened".
Since it is an "exe" file it will begin "running" when you open it. He
said the file could even be downloaded with no harm as long as it isn't
opened.

I will post a very good outline of what to do if infected. It will
include the file names to look for. You could do a search to see if they
are on your computer.

One clue is, don't shut down and restart your computer until you know you
don't have the virus. It will automatically open itself when you
restart.
