> I was at Bread and Circus today, buying meat when they gave me a card on
the Organic Restoration Act. It seems that some of our government
representatives decided at the last minute to add a rider to the
Appropriation Bill that would allow livestock growers (Poultry, Beef -- This
means DAIRY too, Pork ....) to feed their animals 'conventional' feed and
still label and sell it as organic.
>

'conventional' feed and sell it for top dollar as organic. This cannot be
allowed to happen.
