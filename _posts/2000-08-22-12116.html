US News & World Report is preparing to do an article on unschooling. This
article will focus on unschooling and not just homeschooling in general. If
you would like to contribute, there are actually two opportunities. Caroline
Kleiner is the reporter working on the article and she would love to hear
from you about your unschooling experiences and she may be interested in
interviewing you (by phone or e-mail, so your location is not critical).
Please see her contact information below.

In addition, if you are in the DC area (including Northern Virginia or
Maryland, but no more than about 90 minutes from DC), she is interested in
observing your family for a day. If your family is available on August 29,
30, or 31 (a Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday), and you are willing to share
your day with her, please contact her right away.
