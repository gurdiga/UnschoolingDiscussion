Our county parks dept. came up with a great idea (what a surprise). They
offer HS Sports Day once a week, 9-11:30 for the kids (K-5) to do team/group

the kids don't know the team sports rules but they only have to tell them
once. They are well behaved, everyone plays together and polite. While
their after-school program they play by age groups, constantly ask questions
& probably don't know the phrase "thank you".
