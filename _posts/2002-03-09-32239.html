Hi everyone

I'm receiving many copies of an email purporting there to be a Yahoo
downtime from March 15-March 18. This is NOT the case (though I
appreciate the heads up from those who were just trying to do a good deed
:)

Yahoo UK will be down for that time, but at this point there has been no
indication that Yahoo US will suffer from the same problem. Yahoo UK is
moving its servers to a different facility, hence the lengthy time to
break things down and get them up and running again.

If you receive this message from a US based group, please let them know
it does not apply to Yahoo US groups.
