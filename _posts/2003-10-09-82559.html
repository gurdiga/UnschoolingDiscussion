One woman walked out, saw Lexie and Janene and said "Oh, I'm glad I
wasn't the only one to bring my child." The only difference I saw,
which was significant, was that her child was outside climbing a tree
while she was inside voting. My child was climbing the same tree while
we were in line but when the line went inside they went in with me and I
read to them out of our current read aloud. And the both went into the
little voting box with me, with Lexie actually participating in the
process.

I don't think this woman brought her child with her to vote. She
brought him with her to the church where she voted and he climbed a
tree. Not that tree climbing is not important, but both were possible.

And yes, I do realize I sound a bit ostentatious.
