Hi

I'm Samantha mother to Jade 5 and Corbin 2 and
partener to Stephen. We are young in the unschooling
groove but loving it. I am so glad I found my way to
John Holt.

After a big day of video watching, Jade decided to do
some work with playdo. She had made some playdo food
then she wanted to write a menu. So she asked me how
to spell a few words. I spelled them out and she
wrote the words herself like it was the most natural
thing in the world. Its amazing to watch the learning
unfold. She is learning how to write and read just
like she has learned to walk, talk, ride a bike, and
tie her shoes.

I am looking forward to learning from y'all.

Peace,
Samantha
