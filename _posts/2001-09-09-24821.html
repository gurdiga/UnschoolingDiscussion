Yes I do mary and constantly... When I'm talking to clients and they have
children I always ask them what they are hoping to achieve by putting their
children in institutions? It's interesting to hear the answers and then to
give them information about homeschooling/unschooling and hear their fears
(which is basically about having no control).... I usually tell them, it's
just something to think about. I remember that movie with Billy Crystal and
Meg Ryan where he said "it's out there, you've already said it, you can't
take it back, because it's out there"... That's the way I feel about giving
information out. My clients may chose not to do anything about the
homeschooling/unschooling but now they know about it and can't forget that I
said it.
