May 2
Education Week : Decision Leaves Iowa Student Diploma-less
http://www.edweek.org/ew/ewstory.cfm?slug=33home.h20
In-depth article on the issues involved in an Iowa school district's refusal
to grant a diploma to a student who was homeschooled for her first two years
of high school.

Cumberland Times : Board gets update on home-schooling
http://www.times-news.com/stories/2001/may/day02/953708.html
Allegany County, MD, school board hears an update on homeschooling. The
number of homeschoolers there has grown by 64% since 1996, while school
enrollments have dropped by 5%.
