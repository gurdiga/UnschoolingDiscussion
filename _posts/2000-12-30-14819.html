Hi Angela,

I think I know what you're talking about, but I've always known them as
unbaked cookies. Here's my mom's recipe:

2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk

Take from stove and add:
2 cups oatmeal
1 cup coconut
6 T cocoa
1 t vanilla

Drop on waxed paper and let cool.
