Now I see why you were looking for uplifting reading material. I wondered
what your trial was. I'm not doing the homeschooling after divorce thing
now, but I did for a while. I think my situation was probably so different
that it wouldn't be much help to you. I pulled my son out long after we
were divorced. After a year and a half, he threatened me with court if I
didn't put Noah back in school. Being 8 months pregnant with Lars and
knowing that Noah wanted all the fighting to end, I caved. One thing I
found out during that period was that in BC anyway, joint guardianship
gives both parents a say in educational matters. Don't know if that's an
issue for you.

Have you tried searching on yahoogroups? There might be something
there. If there isn't already a group, there should be. I know that it's
very common for dads who were supportive of homeschooling to do a reversal
when the marraige breaks up.

Hang in there, Brook. I'm thinking of you.
