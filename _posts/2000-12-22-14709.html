Well, I am the original skeptic when it comes to the press and I thought
nothing would surprise me but this comment from the following actually
brought me to a screeching halt! "the point of journalism is to preserve
the legitimacy of those in power" OMG, I am flabbergasted! Does this
person not know the whole history of the press and American revolution???

> > > the legitimacy of those in power: "George Bush is going to be
