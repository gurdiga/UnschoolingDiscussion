For the benefit of of some of the new and enthusiastic list members, I
wanted to say that this is probably the quietest time of year for homeschooling
discussions of any kind. Those who might want to homeschool next year aren't
feeling the pressure yet. Those who are thinking of changing to unschooling
from a more structured kind of homeschooling were planning to have the summer
"off" anyway, and will ask "when school starts."

I haven't tracked it really closely, but there seems to be a surge in late
summer, and then (for unschooling, at least) around October and November, when
people have tried school again and quit in disgust, or bought a curriculum
and kids and mom all are in tears about it all. Then it's kinda constant until
spring again, when it slacks off.

Sandra
