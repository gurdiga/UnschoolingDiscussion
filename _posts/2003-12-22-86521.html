...I have a story.

My oldest daughter, Kristen, is a 4th grade teacher in town. ( I gave her
a ride home tonight and we were chatting in the car).
Well..the other day a friend of mine picked up my two young kids at home
and went to the elementary school to pick up her daughter ( a second grader)
before heading back to their house to play.

While they were at the school, they stopped in to see my daugher, (the
teacher, their older sister). The mom went to get her daughter, and Kristen took
Chad and Chloe with her to bring the school children out to the bus.

She told me tonight that it was a riot. Chad skipped the whole way and
Chloe was jumping the cracks in the walkway as she held her hand.
She said Chad and Chloe seemed so HAPPY and all the other kids were
walking single file and were like little "robots".( of course, only doing what they
have been taught to do).
She said she soooooooo much noticed the difference...and when she got out
of the car as I pulled into her driveway tonight ,she said "Mom, don't ever
put them in school"

~Marcia..who is so happy she has the support of ALL her children
