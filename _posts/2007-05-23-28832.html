Sandra Dodd

23 May 2007, at 12:12pm
I was working on a medieval outfit for Holly (one of three she needs
for a big campout) and got a call from the Children, Youth and
Families Department of the State of New Mexico. I had been expecting
the call, and had tried to call back and had left my number yesterday.

I figured it would be about homeschooling. I figured it wouldn't be
much trouble, but I wasn't thrilled about the prospect of explaining
it all to someone.

Turned out it wasn't about that at all, but someone not liking my blog!

Well, then. It was a complaint about the (now) fourth (then third)
post down, May 9.

http://sandradodd.blogspot.com/

The comments were mostly moved to another page, which is linked
there. And the "comment deleted" only said "Mine wasn't
irritating." You can figure out whose it was, if you look at the
comments.

I'm not trying to spook anyone at all, I just think it's a
fascinating peek into how much some people want to control others and
how desperately some people want to justify their own fears and
"safety" rules.

So this post might've been more about unschooling if that's why the
social worker called. <g>

Sandra
