It is really beautiful, though all of the resorts are down at the ocean. There are a few B & B's up here and the Kula Lodge. Other than that it's really quiet. Of course if you want to visit the volcano (Haleakala) you are a lot closer. It's definitely worth a visit some day! Kristin
