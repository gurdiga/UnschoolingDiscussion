Diana -

I got weekly emails from www.icelandair.com and they often have cheap
international rates... you have to fly through Iceland (can even stay
for a few days without a penalty), but I've seen it as cheap as $200
roundtrip.

Also, pick up a Sunday New York Times and check out the Travel
section - great specials listed in there frequently.

Finally, visit www.travelzoo.com and sign up for their free weekly
email. It lists the top 20 travel deals each week.

Once you know the cheapest prices out there, you can try
www.priceline.com and bid... you can't choose flight times, just
dates.

Good luck!
