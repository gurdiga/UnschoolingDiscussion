We had Junior Webelos which is younger than Cub Scouts. What we really did
was what I did when I was a Brownie <g> But what the hey, they didn't know
and they had lots of fun.

Mainly it was crafts that they wanted to do. Each meeting we would spend
the last 5 or 10 minutes coming up with what they wanted to do next meeting.
Sometimes it was a field trip and sometimes it was other fun stuff.
