Always Learning has lots of new members lately. Is it coincidence,
luck, or was it mentioned somewhere? (If anyone knows why there are

Along with that we're having about the quietest month ever. I think
every day or two that I should come enliven things and play good
hostess, but then I don't...

Let me think of some things you might want to look at while this list
is boring. There's a new list that was announced at unschooling.info
for photos of unschoolers learning. That's a fun thought. <g> You
can see the photos here:

http://flickr.com/groups/unschooling/

And you could join and upload some of your own!

I'm collecting links to photos of my kids here:
http://sandradodd.com/kids

If you go to my blog (or just about anyone's blog) there's a blogring
link and you can randomly (or methodically) read other unschoolers'
blogs. I have a link to a general homeschooling blog, but sometimes
those can be less... peaceful and uplifting.
http://sandradodd.blogspot.com

Feel free to jump in and tell a story here and stir it up, but for a
few days I figure people with children have lots of things to keep
them busy.

And just in case there's someone here who's unaware of it, there are
many things to read and see starting at one of these entries:
http://sandradodd.com/unschooling
http://sandradodd.com/life
