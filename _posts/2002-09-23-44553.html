Hi Shannon,
Does your daughter attend some kind of school now? If not, then you are
already unschooling her. What TV shows does she enjoy now? Watch those
with her and see if you can find shows/videos that are about things she has
shown interest in. Also look for stuff about your interests "Field trips"
are a school-term, along with educational vs. junk TV, videos, books,etc.
and curriculum. When you go places now with your daughter, what do you call
it then? Going to the zoo, playground, park,etc. Just keep calling it that
and save the school-terms for any reports you have to give to the school
district.
As for places to go, take her places she likes and that you think she might
like. Active/ hands-on is much better than don't touch places.
Have you been to unschooling.com? The message boards are wonderful.

Mary-NY (mom to 3 dd-12,9,4 and to 2ds-7,2)
