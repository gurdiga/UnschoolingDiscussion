Subject: Homeschoolers International list

Howdy,

Okay, I've started the list.  So far there are three homeschooling families from England  who've signed up (if I'm guessing correctly by their email addresses).  I've invited people from nearly a dozen countries to join.  As the number of members increases, hopefully we'll see the list evolve into a truly international homeschoolers forum.

If you decide to join, please grant me two favours:

1. Post an introductory message right away.  It doesn't need to be long or involved.  Just say who you are, where you're from, etc.  This is very important in order to break the ice.
.
2. Make an effort at least for a few days to participate to get this group off the ground.

I believe this list has a lot of potential.  We just need to attract good people from around the world.  I know they're out there, it's only a matter of finding them.

You can join by visiting the group homepage at:
http://www.egroups.com/group/homeschoolers_international
