Now I know there's quite a bit of blogs for homeschoolers, but what about
the fair weather homeschoolers? Those, 'it's the greatest thing since sliced

I'm going to kill my invisible cat!' days. So if you've ever wondered and
wanted to discuss how the other evil half (known as public-schoolers) live, or
