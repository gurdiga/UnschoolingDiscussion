Rebecca,
I know you have had some great suggestions for making a sling, and someone
offered you a Maya Sling, but I have a wonderful neutral colored New Native
sling, sized medium, that you can have if you want. Just another option! I
just loved using it for my last baby. I wore him quite a bit and it always
calmed him. It was light, and I could move him around to the back when he
was asleep, which was really great for being able to have arms free for my
other children or for doing dishes/cooking, etc... Plus, it was even more
comfortable wearing him on my back. I've been using the Ergo Baby Carrier
since Evan was able to sit up on his own and he started to
dislike being so wrapped up, so I don't really need the sling anymore.
