I have something called "School-in-a-Box" from Simplified Learning
Products by Joyce Herzog at www.joyceherzog.com. I believe the SIAB is
under $20 & there is a small booklet ($3-$4)you can order for ideas how
to teach every subjest & multi-level it.

Homemade: I also got a bigger box of my own(Wal-Mart craft dept) &
started
collecting all sorts of objects: fake coins, samll animals, pony-tails
holder pieces, clothes pins,nail, screw, nut bolt, toothpick(careful
here),cubes,dice,old game pieces..............the possiblities are
endless. Then you let your child chose objects & ask them to use their
imagination to make a story, do math, ect.....But I liked the ideas in
her booklet-maybe I'm not that creative esp to put it in my ed. journal
:o)
I thought this might help. My kids LOVE it. Use for all ages & all
learning types & it's good in the car for trips if you can keep it all
together :o)
