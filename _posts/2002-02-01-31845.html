joanna514

1 Feb 2002, at 10:53am

Lunch bags work well.
Also, my kids use popsicle sticks and make flat paper people or
animals and glue them to the sticks and use the sticks as holders.
TP tubes work great. My dd just made some using the top 1/3 of the
tube for the head/face, the middle for a shirt or dress top and the
bottom as the legs/skirt. They look really cool. Feathers and yarn
are a must for my dd.
You could ask moms to bring unmatched socks they have at their house,
and any thing else they would want to donate to make puppets.
I would happily oblige that request from someone.
Have fun!
Joanna
