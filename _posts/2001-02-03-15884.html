For anyone who doesn't want to have to mess with the website, you can handle
your subscription entirely by email.

To change your subscription to digest (25 messages per email instead of one
at a time) you can send an email to:

Unschooling-dotcom-digest@yahoogroups.com

You don't need anything in the subject or body of the email.

To return to individual emails send to:

Unschooling-dotcom-normal@yahoogroups.com

To temporarily stop receiving mail without leaving the group send email to:

Unschooling-dotcom-nomail@yahoogroups.com

To unsubscribe:

Unschooling-dotcom-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com

To come on back <g>

Unschooling-dotcom-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

address. You can click the back button to go back and order other stuff
