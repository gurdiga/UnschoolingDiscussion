Thank you to all of those that responded to my questions and even read
my posts!
I know they were long.
I have learned one thing from this list, diversity is good! I will leave
here knowing that unschooling is most certainly different from family to
family, and that there should be freedom of expression.
I can see too that there are a few ladies here that feel it's their
calling to define unschooling in only their terms and to reject loudly
the valid opinions of another's method of unschooling. This is sad,
since unschooling is so diverse and in my personal research I have found
that it has MANY different meanings to many different people.
I hope that this list evolves in such a way that tolerance is the
foundation and that dogma, when it comes to an individual family choice
in education, is discarded.
THERE IS NO ONE WAY TO UNSCHOOL! :O) OR TO EDUCATE FOR THAT MATTER:O)
Unfortunately this list is WAY too busy for me and I dont think that I
can even go to the site and read all of it.
I'm amazed at those women who can hang out with their kids, take care of
their husbands and homes and still be so active here! WOW! That's
something I haven't mastered as of yet.
The messages just keep pouring in even as I write this!
So I'm moving on to a slower list.
Take care!
