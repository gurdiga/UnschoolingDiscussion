Unschooling.com Newsletter:

To Subscribe: Subscriptions to Unschooling.com's Online Newsletter are free
by request. Send an email to majordomo@... with the words
"subscribe unschooling-newsletter" followed by your email address in the
body of the email. (Example: subscribe unschooling-newsletter
YourName@...) To Unsubscribe send an email to
majordomo@... with the words "unsubscribe
unschooling-newsletter" followed by your email address in the body of the
email.(Example: unsubscribe unschooling-newsletter Yourname@...)

Unschooling-dotcom list:

To unsubscribe send a blank email message to
Unschooling-dotcom-unsubscribe@onelist.com

For more information about the Unschooling-dotcom List, or to join or leave
the list, visit this website:
http://www.onelist.com/community/Unschooling-dotcom
