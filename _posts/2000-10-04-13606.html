The book is Discover Your Child's Learning Style
Here is the web site at Amazon about it....

http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0761520139/o/qid=970617695/sr=8-1/ref=aps_sr_b_1_1/103-4681666-7218245

I loved it!! It really helped me with my son. There is recommendations

each family member. It really helped me see my children and their
personalities more clearly. I will probably buy it to have around all the
time.
