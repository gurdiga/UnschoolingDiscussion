I have a funny story! I have already sent it out to be published, so
please dont call it your own. I have seen alot of this on the net, no
I'm not nuts, well, yes, I am but.....Oh nevermind, heres the story!

When my son, Jay , was born we received a piggy bank as a gift. As he
grew, he enjoyed putting the spare change he would find into it. He
asked me what he was saving for when he was2 and when I told
him "college", he never questioned it.
Shortly after we returned from a Cape Cod vacation a year later, Jay
was dropping his pennies in the bank and told me he couldnt wait to
have enough money for cottage.
"College", I corrected.
"Whats that?" he asked.
I explained to him that college was school you go to after you finish
high school. The look on his face was priceless as he cried "Oh no! I
don't want to go to school! I want to go to the beach!"
All along he had thought he was saving for a cottage at the beach!


I didn't give it much thought at the time, but I guess he has always
been an unschooler at heart!
