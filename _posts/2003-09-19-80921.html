For instance in the US you can use WIC if you are poor or not. One family can
make 40,000 a year and still qualify and wala formula free but paid to the
companies. They still make money poor or not in the US.
I have received free formula with all three of my children. I was given
diaper bags at the hospital, medicine feeders with formula manufactures names on
them and cups. The first baby I had the hospital pushed the formula and finger
geed my son without my permission. Nursing was very hard after that. With my
daughter they did the same while I slept. Both times they sent home formula
with me.
They dont push as much now and I actually choose a hospital that does not
push formula at all. They have someone come in twice a day to assist, they offer
creams, breast pads and even a foot massage its heaven to have a baby there.

Im babbling but my point is that in the US WIC is offered to Moms with
children under 5 and if the child is under or over weight they qualify at times no
matter what the income.
