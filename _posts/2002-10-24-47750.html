That may be it! Iguanas will get huge eventually given enough room and a good diet, but if you keep them in smaller cages they will stay smaller longer, sometimes forever. For example, the one we had was 5 years old and only about 18 inches long, he had been kept in a 30 gallon aquarium for his entire life. My uncle has a 2 yr old one that is now well over 3 feet long, he built a 6 ft high cage that was 3'x3', and put in 4 levels I believe, and a tree branch.

I'm not sure what you'd call the cage. I got the bird-cage part when we bought the hamsters, then added the other pieces on from stuff bought at Wal-Mart.
