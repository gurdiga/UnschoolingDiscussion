Here is a link for the person who posted that they might move to the U.S. or
Canada to help narrow it down, and for others, either for fun or if you are
longing for a different place to live.
It's a quiz that takes about 10 minutes and they give you a list of the 24 most
compatible places in the U.S. for you to live:
http://www.findyourspot.com/default.asp?NewQ=3
Then you could narrow it down to fewer places and repost your question
about homeschooling laws and such, since they varry from state to state.
Have fun!
