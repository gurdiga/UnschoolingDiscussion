I have come to accept it. I've always know I'm an introvert. I love having a
nice conversation with 1 or 2 people. Get a whole group of people together
and I start feeling tired really quickly. This has led me to cut of ties with
people for months at a time just to get back to myself. I find I like to
actually listen to what people are saying and many people talking at once
limits my ability to focus on (and understand) one person. I have a friend on
this list who thrives on lots of people being around. I think that's cool
too. If everyone was an introvert there'd be less progress. If everyone was
an extrovert there'd be chaos.
