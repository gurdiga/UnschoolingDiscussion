Tami,

Sounds a great time to get away just the two of you. Have you heard of
Marriage Encounter? It's a weekend designed to help good marriages into
great ones. The weekend focuses on just the two of you and improving
communication between you. It's not a retreat, nor a marriage clinic, nor
group sensitivity. It's a unique approach aimed at revitalizing Christian
marriage. Sounds like you might enjoy a weekend alone with your dh.

We went 3 years ago (married 16 yrs) and it helped us a lot. I don't think
we would have even started homeschooling this year if we could not discuss
the ideas and be more open to each others feelings.

Think about it. It was great for us.

Sites with more information:

National site: http://www.wwme.org/

Section 6 covers Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, and West Virginia.
http://www.rc.net/org/me6/index.html

Section 8 covers Minnesota, North & South Dakota
http://studio.cloudnet.com/wwme/

Hope you get a chance to get away. It sounds like you guys deserve to get
away and make a weekend after all the stresses you guys have had.
