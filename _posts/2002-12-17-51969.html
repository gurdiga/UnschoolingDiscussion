Hi Lisa,

This is a Canadian film made by Zacharias Kunuk, an Inuit filmaker. It's
called "Atanarjuat, The Fast Runner" and is in Inuktitut, subtitled in
English and runs about three hours. I have not (to my dismay) seen it yet,
but know folks who have liked it very much. It is based on an Inuit legend
and won Canadian film awards, plus the Cannes Palme d'or for Best First
Feature Film. It's available in DVD, but not sure about VHS.

You can Google it, too. This review looked good:
www.scifinoir.com/reviews/atanarjuat.html

Robin B., in White Rock, BC
