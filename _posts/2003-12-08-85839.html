Yes, "quiver full" is a term in some Christian circles regarding how many children a family has.

Basically the theology is that birth control of any kind is wrong, and that God will provide the means to take care of any children He may bless you with. Some see it as wrong even where health concerns make it difficult for the mother to be pregnant, because to them it means you must not be trusting in Him fully.

So by admitting that my husband had a vasectomy, even for reasons to us that are biblical (his role as husband to protect me from dangerous situations) others felt they had the right to condemn us for because as Christians we are told to speak to one another if we see that another is doing something "wrong."
