"Creating Learning Communities" is finally off the presses. I have my first
copy in my hand. And will be mailing copies to everyone who preordered
later today. If you don't get it within a week let me know.

For those who don't know. "Creating Learning Communities" was composed
online by 'A Coaltion for Self Learning (CfSL). CfSL is an ad hoc collection
of homeschoolers, futurists, ecucational professionals and other who can
"envision a world without schools -- a world of learning communities." They
see a new learning system as necessary, possible and happening. It will be
based on new social needs, new brain research, and new techniques and
technologies.

Publication of the book is only the kickoff for a campaign to help create
learning communities. Everyone is invited to read the book join the
campaign.
The book is now available from any of the 30 authors for $21.95. It will be
in book stores on amazon.com with in week or so. And can be read online at
<http://www.CreatingLearningCommunites.org>
