Hello everybody,

I'm Stefanie Mohsennia, German and the webmaster of a web site promoting
respectful parenting and natural learning in my country where
unfortunately, we have compulsory schooling laws and homeschooling is
illegal.

I am working on a book about unschooling in German in order to get
people acquainted with its wonderful possibilities and outcomes and
maybe get them interested enough to fight for the legalization of
homeschooling. I have lots of good material already, but I'm still
collecting real life accounts of the lives of unschoolers.

I wonder if you and especially your kids and teens would like to help me
and answer one or several or all of the following questions:

- What do you do all day? What are your favorite pastimes?
- How would you describe yourself as a person? Do you think
homeschooling had an influence on you becoming the kind of person you
are today?
- Do you ever wish you had chosen the school path?
- What do you like best about homeschooling? What do you consider to be
its greatest advantages?
- Do you work (volunteering or paid work)? Do you like it? What have you
learnt through your work experience?
- What are your plans or hopes for the future? And how do you work
towards your goals?

* *

Please give your first name, age and place of residence and tell me if
you have been homeschooled all your life or if you have spent time in
school. I would translate interesting answers and contributions and use
them for my chapter in which children talk about their unschooling lives.

I would love to hear from you!

Best wishes,
Stefanie
www.unschooling.de

P.S. I only have Internet access Monday thru Friday afternoon, so don't
be upset if you don't hear from me immediately.
