Please don't forward these "alerts" to mailing lists, especially without checking to see if the information is accurate. This Wobbler thing is a hoax. For more information: http://www.symantec.com/avcenter/venc/data/wobbler-hoax.html

Forwarding this misinformation just clogs up the net, makes mailing lists a real drag, and unnecessarily alarms unsuspecting readers. It also rewards the hoaxers with publicity.

Check for hoaxes at http://www.symantec.com/avcenter/hoax.html (there are other sites as well; but this is the one I have bookmarked).
