About the Ocarina discussion...
I bought a really great little soprano ocarina from songbird ocarinas (online). They make raku ocarinas that are very high quality, a little more money too, but worth it.
If you buy an ocarina from them, it comes with a couple fingering/song books including the zelda songs. If you get a sweet potato ocarina (like Sandra mentioned) you can play all the Zelda songs, which is a great way to connect music for those gamers. :)
