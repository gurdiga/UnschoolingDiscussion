This is why we don't put anything out before Christmas, except maybe a few,
just to look pretty.
I think it's just too hard for young children to avoid the temptation.
I also figure that until I choose to give the gift to someone, I still own
it. So since it's technically mine, I can say NO. hehe
But in our house we know they can't handle the temptation, so they all stay
hidden until everyone is asleep Christmas eve.
It would kinda spoil Christmas for me if they didn't have anything left to
open.
I do buy an advent calender for each of them, which they get a treat from
every day (Trevor has decided he doesn't like chocolate through this
experience and gives his to the wee babsy man) which takes the edge off a
little.
And they always open one on Christmas Eve.

We give a small gift or two and candy for St. Nicholas Day (12/6), the
stockings are left on the couch on Christmas morning and the kids can play
with those when they wake up.(I fill them as much as possible with things

morning. This year we'll need to cover the big gifts with a sheet on
Christmas Eve or my 2yods will open everything.

Well this year the cat and the kitten were driving me crazy making their own
little place under the tree. My velour tree skirt was covered in hair and I

shaking or anything. She understands now that on Christmas she can open
some. Of course she has no idea when that is so every day she asks. She also
goes and shows everyone how we have Merry Christmas under the tree! Aren't
kids just the best??!!!!
