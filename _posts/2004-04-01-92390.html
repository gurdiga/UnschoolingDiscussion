I went last year and found it very frustrating.

time. The only thing that slightly interested me was a workshop on
multiple intelligences. I almost walked out on the keynote
speaker. The woman who wrote "The Well Trained Mind". She
literally repulsed me with the things she had to say about children

separate her from her other children, because she didn't thrive
quite as well with her methods. Obviously being biracial and
adopted had everything to do with it. :-P
After going to the live and learn conference, I would never waste my
time or money on a "regular" conference again.
