Welcome.
I have two pieces of advice for you as the mother of a seven-month-old.
First, don't bother to find out the requirements, because by the time she's
"school age," if you're still in Florida, they will probably have changed the
requirements anyway. And the other thing is to let you know (you might already
know) that there's another group you might want to join (in addition, I mean;
not instead of this one) called AlwaysUnschooled and it's linked here:
http://sandradodd.com/lists/other

and Unschooling Basics is another busy and enthusiastic list. Some of the
people here are on both of those and others are on one or another or just this
one. <g>

Sandra
