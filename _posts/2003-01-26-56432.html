Have a Nice Day!

26 Jan 2003, at 11:31am
I was telling some friends from work about how someone tried to hack us the other night and how we found the guy, and are pursuing it with his ISP, etc. We were so proud of ourselves for catching the guy (at least we *think* we have).
I was sharing an exciting story.

Guess what the first thing they asked me was?

"What is an 11 year old doing chatting on the internet anyway? You can't just let kids chat with ANYONE!"

UGH!!!!!

They completely missed the point. No harm was done and we all learned A LOT. I bet I can say with 100% certainty that my kids will never make that mistake again BECAUSE of this experience...not because of any advice I gave them before. And NOW they know how to track someone who does this stuff.

UGH again.

Kristen
