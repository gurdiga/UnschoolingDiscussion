Stay as far away as possible from the Disney magazines. I am not anti-Disney
at all, but the children's magazines are awful. My daughter had a
subscription and I couldn't wait for it to expire. The magazine is basically
a promotional vehicle for the Disney related TV shows, movies, and pop
groups. It is filled with advertisements for expensive toys as well.

Have you looked at Cricket and the other magazines published by the same
company? No advertisements and lots of fun stories and poems. National
Geographic also makes a nice magazine for the younger set.
