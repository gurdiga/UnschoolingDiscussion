Announcing a new email list - Learning in Tennessee!!

[LearnTN] http://www.egroups.com/group/learntn/info.html

This is a list to share information about learning resources, learning centers, learning
facilitators, contact people, classes, workshops, camps, mentors, apprenticeships,
events, field trips, etc. in and near Tennessee. We will have a strong focus on sharing
information among homeschoolers and those interested in creating cooperative
community-based, life-long, inter-generational learning environments.

Please help spread the word on-line and off-line.

Thanks!
