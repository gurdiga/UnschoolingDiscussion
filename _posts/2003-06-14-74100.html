Oh we've done that before!! I was supposed to visit with a friend in Phili. But her whole family fell ill while we were in the city. We were close enough to home that we could have gone home but somehow, I don't even remember how, we ended up staying with another family that we had met "online". We even had an offer from yet *another* family that we knew online. (I think we had all agreed to meet at the Franklin Institute because that is where we met).

We slept on her living room floor. I have to confess, it was quite the adventure, staying at a stranger's house, sleeping on their floor!!!

Someday I'll have to write a book about untraveling!!
