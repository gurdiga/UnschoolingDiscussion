>Wednesday April 3, 2002 there will be a meeting of the like-minds!
>Unschoolers from around the area (DC) will converge upon the National mall
>by the thousands...er.. well, a few unschooling families from the national
>unschooling email loops (Always Learning and Unschooling-dotcom and
>homesteading homeschoolers) are meeting in front of the Museum of Natural
>History at 11:00 am. Some families are driving a couple hours to meet other
>unschoolers.
>Let's get down there and show our support to those who may have no other
>unschooling families in their area. Pack snacks, we'll sit under a cherry

>something, we will !
>Montgomery and Frederick county folks may want to meet at the Shady Grove
>satellite parking lot at 10:00. Parking is free and a shuttle bus (also
>free) will take us right to the station.

29 Mar 2002, at 7:20am
Oh, how I wish it were possible for us!! I am in a real r-u-t with lack of
unschooling support. I have persevered this far ("school year") with a
'field trip' and 'gym class' group.....it's more wearing than I expected -
the constant discussions of curriculum. Why taching our children Latin is
*essential* (though none of the moms took it save one with 2 yrs in high
school)! And the askance looks when I respond to questions about our
curriculum usage. ( I usually say something along the lines.....we don't
use a specific curriculum, we draw from a large variety of resources.) Of
course, the fact that my 10 yr old is a late reader has clinched for them
that my lack of specific curriculum causes failure.
Susan, venting over for the day!!!
