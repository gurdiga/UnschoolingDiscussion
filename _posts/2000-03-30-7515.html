Thanks for your concern. I have used homeopathy successfully for several
health issues in myself and my children. I believe that it can be an
valuable part of our health care. It is not a cure for my lupus. This
disease did not begin as a result of emotional stress or trauma and
unfortunately it won't be cured by homeopathy. I don't mean to sound too
prickly about it, but believe me - if it were that simple, I and many many
others with lupus and other serious conditions would all be well by now. It
is hard for those of us with incurable illnesses to hear someone say that our
emotions brought it on and that there is an answer out there. It makes our
suffering seem less valid. I would not be surprised if homeopathy eased some
of the discomfort that many sufferers feel, however.

I appreciate the kindness behind your post.
