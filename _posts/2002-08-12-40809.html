I LOVE the Power Glide Spanish. Bought the adult one for myself.

use it at appropriate times when speaking with them.

And even if they never learn Spanish as another language, I think the
exposure to the idea of teaching oneself a language and hearing the
foreign sounds is a wonderful benefit.
