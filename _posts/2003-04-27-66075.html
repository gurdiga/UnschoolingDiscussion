There was a small unschooling conference last year in Watertown, MA. (I
went, and also live in MA.) It was not as large as the GWS camp. I put a

for others who want to pull together a N.E. camp or conference. Inquire on
the MHLA email list: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/masshomelearningassoc/
It would be great to have such a conference or gathering in the area.
