You can put a towel over the area and another under, and then use the
hottest iron for the cloth and it should lift the crayon out. It depends
on the type of crayon, though, if you will be able to get the color
stain out, Crayolas usually come out pretty easily (yes, I've had this
experience before!!;)

Good luck,

Lisa F
