I wonder if anyone on this list is familiar with "The Indigo
Children" by Lee Carroll and Jan Tober. I myself don't agree with

out), but I still found the book fascinating to read, esp. with
regard to ADD and other such labels and kids not accepting "no" for
an answer. Their website is www.indigochild.com. I would be really
interested to hear what you people think about it.

I think it was on this list where someone else mentioned
www.awareparenting.com, which also happens to be one of my favourite
sites.
