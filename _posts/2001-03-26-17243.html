We have been struggling these last two weeks. Naomi has been watching far too much television, not doing much reading or study work. Her attitudes towards me and others has degenerated into surly and mean and disrespectful.
 
We had a family pow-wow last night and discussed all this. Decided to limit television watching to one hour a day, computer to one hour a day, spend more time reading God's word, prayer, reading the books from the library, do more science experiments, etc. We will also be moving within the next month...at least by May 1st we should be in a different apartment than this one. And as a part of the move cable will be shut off and not reinstated for a looooooooong time. (unspecified time limit) I would appreciate your support and prayers in this.
