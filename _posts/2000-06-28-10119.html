or believes in either, but it sounds t me like some of you are tarring and
feathering her because you don't like her views. What happened to "don't be

Dr. Laura is an observant Jew, so it stands to reason that she would call
homosexuality a sin. She believes in the Old Testament, and homosexuality
is called a sin there. I have never heard her condemn homosexuals, just the
behavior. I have heard her support adoption rights for homosexual couples,
so I have difficulty believing that she hates all homosexuals.

Some of you have condemned her for "not living what she preaches" because

and she works during the hours he is in school only. Her husband is a full
