Try pretending you are going to send a reply or forward the message. On

Hey! My computer geek dh just helped me figure out why I couldn't "see" people's email
addresses. For some reason this is the only list I'm on that sends out messages in RTF
format (my husband says that using RTF is not very good because it makes the messages bigger
than they need to be). Well, when I changed my options on Outlook Express to that I would
reply in Plain Text as opposed to the same format that the message was sent in - Voila! I
can now see people's email addresses!
