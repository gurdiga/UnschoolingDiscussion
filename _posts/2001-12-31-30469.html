No they won't. We try out all car seats in
each car before buying the seat. They are
used to it, and even commented to me that few
people do this, but enough do that they won't
look at you funny. Do take out each model,
and try it in your car, in each of the cars
you will be using. Also, try it in different
seats, middle seat, etc. it's really worth
it! But don't expect them to help you, they
can carry but they have no idea how to put
them in.
