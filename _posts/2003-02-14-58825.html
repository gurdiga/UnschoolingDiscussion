"Can you talk more about the resource day. Sounds real interesting. "

We have use of a nice community center every Wed. from 10a-1p. There is a
large Gymn with Basketball hoops, a stage, a room with Tv/VCR and long tables
where the Yugio/Mage Knight fans congregate. A dance room has space for
anything else they want to do, we bring blocks, brio train track, plastic
bugs/lizards/bats etc... for the younger ones, have parties, play games,
share interests, play music on their sound system, barter, do art/craft
projects (the floor is tile, so easy clean up) cook etc..
Anything goes.
And the best part is the community center has a wonderful park with picnic
benches and it's right on the BAY. A HUGE dock is right next to the center
and the kids run up and down chasing seagulls and often end up splashing at
the waters edge or mucking about for fish, watching fishermen etc...
It's really awesome and I think we've influenced a few folks to really think
about unschooling in a whole new way.
We are the only truly inclusive group that I know of and we get all the most
interesting and open minded folks in Pensacola!! For the most part anyway....
:)
AND, we're hosting the first ever LIFE (Learning In Freedom Everyday)
FunFest. It's a mini-conference with speakers (local only), vendors and
workshops all day.
We're really excited!
