Hi,
I am writing, because I was spanked as a child. I was taken in the yard
and told to find a stick so my sweet little mom could hit my legs with it.
Is all sounds so ridiculously funny to me now. I don't remember it actually
scaring me out of doing something, because I was determined to do what I
wanted anyway. Emotionally damaged? I don't think so, although I did use it
to my advantage to instill guilt when I was an obnoxious teenager, and was
getting all the school psychology stuff. People would come in and ask
students how they would feel? did this or that happen? I did buy into that
and started to really take self pity on myself, and scream at my parents
about their poor skills as parents. What a brat! Now, I am too busy to even
worry about woes of my childhood. I think spanking is very ineffective. It
only makes the parent feel worse than even the child,my dad always
apologized for days after he would spank me. I don't think it does anything
to modify behavior. Maybe it stops a behavior short term by humiliating the
child into a quiet state.
Wendy wew99@...

the boundary or the affections.
