I am dyslexic. Never heard the term until age 23. My
older daughter is dyslexic. We homeschooled, then she
took her GED, ACT, attended community college --
mainly to prove to herself that she could -- has
worked full-time since age 16, makes twice as much
money as I do. If there is interest on this list I
will write at length about my perception, research,
and learning/teaching techniques.
