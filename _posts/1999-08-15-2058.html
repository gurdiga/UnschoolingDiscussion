For those asking for more information about adoption:

After a little research concerning the adoption links, and phone numbers.
Adoptive Families of America is still working on their website, their toll
free number is 800-372-3300. They provide wonderful service to adoptive
families and lots of support. Children Awaiting Parents can be accessed
thru their website at adopt.org this is connected to other photo
listing organizations. This one website will give you acess to 1300
children in need of homes. There are other photo listing services online,
you will just need to spend sometime browsing and searching to get exactly
where you want to go. There is also a lot of good information about
adopting an older child (any child past newborn) on the web. Please be sure
to read any and all suggested reading concerning adoption and fostercare.
The history that these children bring into your family will play a huge part
in everyone's lives and you must be ready to deal with it.

Good luck on your search. The joys and challenges of adopting are wonderful
and life long.
