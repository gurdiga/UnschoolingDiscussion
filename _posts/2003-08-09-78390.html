Yes. Ask at any nursing home for the supply clerk. They have large boxes
that diapers came in that is good for blankets and clothes. They have
smaller, sturdier boxes that latex gloves came in for packing your heavier
items. If anyone in your household is allergic to latex, ask for only the
boxes vynal gloves come in because the latex dust gets on everything. Ask
them what day their shipment comes in because they probably will need the
boxes picked up right away. There is never enough room to store them.
