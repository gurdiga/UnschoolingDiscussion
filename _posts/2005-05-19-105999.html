2 weeks now. I have also learned so much. I homeschooled my 11 yo son

but it makes sense to me after all these years of preferring to be
alone reading or hating attention. Please don't think I am thinking
any of your children have this. I would never assume that! Thanks for
all the info I get from these posts. You guys are great! Mary
