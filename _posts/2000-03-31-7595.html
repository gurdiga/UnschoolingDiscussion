jackie,

I had my first (and only) child when I was 40. I never paid much attention
to all the huplah about age and pregnancy, just trusted myself and my body.
I am the natural as possible type so didn't get any of the tests, (amnio
etc.) In fact did not see a medical doctor, just an at home mid-wife. I
did have moments of fear when well meaning friends would tell stories of
worst case scenario's, but decided I wasn't going to create a dreaded
experience, and if I did have something happen it was for the highest and
best.

Anyhow, I never felt better in my life during month's 4 to 9 of the
pregnancy, I did have to go to the emergency room of the hospital for the
delivery after 4 days of labor, but left after 4 hours with a healthy baby.
The hospital story and process had some difficulty, but had much meaning and
learning for me.

Everything is so individual, and I do not like to buy into statistics and
fear based decisions and was glad I didn't in this case.
