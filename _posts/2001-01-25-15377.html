When Holly asks me "what do I say if people ask what religion I am?" I say
"Why do they want to know?"

Sometimes the answer IS "Christian" for us. If the person asking is Jewish,
or Moslem, we're Christian. If someone from Madagascar asks where I live, I
live in the U.S.

If someone who's Christian is asking, we're agnostic. If their purpose is to
know what denomination, we are none. That's like someone in Albuquerque
asking where I live. I live near Juan Tabo and Candelaria. It's relative to
their position, and the information they need.

If their purpose is to ask whether we're born again so they know whether to
proselytize or not, we're agnostic. But if the question is what is your
family, culturally and historically, then in that context we're Christian.
