While the Bear Sleeps Retold by Caitlin Matthews
Is a picture story book. Includes stories about
Christmas, Hanukka, Winters Soltice, Kwanza, Native
American etc. The book begins with a young girl
meeting a bear who takes her on a journey through
different times and cultures celbrating the winter
holidays. Very, Very lovely!
