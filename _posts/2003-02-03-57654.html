Thanks for asking.

We had a great trip. Stayed at an inn in Burnsville. We had snow all Saturday
morning---SO COOL! Then the sun came up, and it was lovely the rest of the
weekend---but the mountaintops remained dusted while we were there.

We looked at many possible pieces of land through a realtor and had about
given up. ;-( Then we saw an ad---for sale by owner---in a local newspaper.
Ten acres---12 years pesticide free, so seven years organic! It was an old
dairy farm. A bubbly creek runs through it (potable!). Five acres flat with
five acres rolling almost all pasture. A few evergreens and some hardwoods on
the hills. With views, in a little cove with really cool neighbors (one
single mom with a 10 year old son she homeschools). 18 miles west of
Asheville, NC.

I'm taking the boys up this weekend to get their opinions. (Duncan will be
all for it as there are horses that live next door! <g>)

Asheville's also a beautiful place for a conference! <G>

~Kelly
