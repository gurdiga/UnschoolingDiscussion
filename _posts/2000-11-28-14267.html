Dear all,

This is to inform you that the BBC World Service should be
broadcasting a programme on infant feeding in Pakistan on
Wednesday
29th November (tomorrow).

This should include an interview with Tracey Wagner-Rizvi of the
Pakistan IBFAN group, The Network for Consumer Protection in
Pakistan,
and Niels Christiansen, Vice-President of Nestle.

The programme is called "Outlook". It will be broadcast in the UK
at
12.05, 18.05 and 00.05 on 29th November and at 08.05 on 30th
November.
In the UK, you can hear the programme on 648 MW.

For times in other countries see the website
http://www.bbc.co.uk/worldservice/schedules/index.shtml

The BBC website also gives information on the frequency of the
programme.

Tracey presented evidence to the European Development
Committee Public
Hearing on Nestle last week. Nestle boycotted the event. See the
press release on the Baby Milk Action website at
http://www.babymilkaction.org/press/press23nov00.html

Best wishes,

Mike.
