Maybe Winnie the Pooh, but note to everybody here:

In movies they use slang and idioms, and so not every phrase everyone uses
came from a movie first. The other day I used a variation on a phrase which
I'd never seen or heard used in that exact variation, and people were sure it
was from a movie.

Some quotes do come from movies, but many more movies use everyday phrases.

"Who? What?" and variations thereon are hundreds of years older than movies
are.
