If you need a great sensory activity - I suggest a ball pit - (can be fancy store bought kind or as simple as a frig box filled w/ a bunch of soft plastic balls (the kind in the ball pits). My dd's all had severe integration issues when they were very little - the ball pit proved a sure way to "unload" the overloaded sensory system - or as a tool to prevent sensory overload before it starts. And most kids love playing in the balls - regardless of age!
