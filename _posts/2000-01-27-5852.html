I greet all of you gardening mavens with a deep bow and stand in awe of your green
conversation. I am, (drum roll, please) a gardener-wanna-be. I am a bread-making
wanna-be as well, but thats off the topic. Last year my beloved dug me a garden plt
in the backyard, but, alas, nothing was ever planted in it, because I am a notorious
murderer of houseplants and outside box blooms as well, and I just didnt know the
first thing about what to plant. I want desperately to plant something, anything that
will grow and give my kids a thrill(they vote for pumpkins) Can anyone offer the
simplest, easiest advice about where to start? Many thanks and blessings.
Also, having three daughters, the big thing around here is pretending in all its
shapes and forms, both with dressup clothes and also toys. My mother in law
gaace them a big trunk with dress up stuff for Christmas last year and it was a
big hit. They can turn any kind of action figure into an extended character for as
long as I don't interrupt them. They also love books, videos, and soundtracks to
movies they have seen, so they can put them on and act them out. I am jealous that
they have each other for this, when I was little, I had to be all the characters by
myself and it got very exhausting! (You try being the entire cast of "The Wizard of Oz") Anyway, congrats on all that and best wishes and prayers.
Take care.
Teresa
mom to jolie, lucy and mara
