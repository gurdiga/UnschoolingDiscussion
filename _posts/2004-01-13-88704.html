HUGS!!! Wendy, I SOOOOO understand!!! I grew up military (in fact, I spent time at the now closed 19th Army General Hospital in Frankfurt). Best thing I can suggest is to re-up and be with him, look at it as an adventure and enjoy it!!

Wow...7 days?! That's some SERIOUS short-timing!!! Had there been a possibility of this transfer or is it truly out of the blue? I'm more familiar with the Navy and Coast Guard, so I'm just wondering.

By the way..when my mother was offered the job in Bermuda (working civil-service for the Navy's fire department as a fire inspector)..they called on Friday, gave her 24 hours to decide and 48 hours to get there. She needed to start work on Monday. We got home from camp that day, she asked our opinions...we were all for it...and she called my dad and said "Come get the kids, I'll call you when I have a house for us." Saturday afternoon we were living in Virginia Beach, Sunday the movers packed our stuff and put it in storage.

Elizabeth
