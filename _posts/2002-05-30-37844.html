> Can someone tell me what a "cover school" is? Just curious ...

Well I'm sure other's replies will be better since we don't have them where
I live, but in some states one of the homeschooling options is to operate
under a "cover school". It's sort of like a private school that you don't
have to attend. The cover school abides by whatever regulations there are
for private schools (or cover schools) in that state and offers its services
to the homeschooling parents. Some offer (or require) oversight. Some offer
services. Some just offer their name for the state forms.
