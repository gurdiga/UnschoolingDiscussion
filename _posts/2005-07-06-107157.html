One idea I thought of is to look into
www.freecycle.org. Its a Yahoo group. They have groups
in many areas around the country/world. See if there
is one in your area. Its about recycling things you
have (OFFER) or posting things you're looking for
(WANTED). No exchange of money. Very cool!
