If one is lucky, a solitary fantasy can totally transform one
million realities.
~Maya Angelou~

<snip>

John Holt's fantasy transformed schools, still inspires radical
teachers and reformers, and helps thousands and thousands of
unschoolers find inspiration and confidence.

-=-=--=-=-

And some of those millions *he* were inspired were inspired enough to
transform their own unschooling millions: Sandra and Joyce inspire
others everyday with their websites. The Live and Learn and other
conferences touch whole families for days at a time. We have Life
Learning, Life Free, Learn Free, and Connections e-zine.There are those
that own and moderate and participate on the message boards and elists!
And even something as simple (although not to *ME* <g>)---all the
incredible bloggers who share their lives and thoughts with us.

SOOO many realities have been changed and are changing every day due to
John Holt's fantasy. But it certainly could have died wih him---if not
for those of us who choose to continue his work.

I'm grateful for John Holt's solitary fantasy, but I'm more grateful
still for those who continue to transform realities!
