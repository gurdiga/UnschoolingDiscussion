ok, my turn ...

Why do you un/homeschool:

we took our dss out of school @ Christmas break our oldest ds (3rd gr) was
invited to be part of a *gang* and he was ABSOLUTELY TERRIFIED b/c he didn't
KNOW the boys but was THREATENED if he didn't join their *gang* he'd be beat
up ... dh and I had a conference w/ his teacher and b/c a school official
wasn't present, they had no way of knowing if this was true/not and their
hands were tied!!! B~U~T, the teacher said "DS NEEDS TO BE ON RITALIN!!!"
*HUH*??? dh, normally very quiet spoken mild mannered person went BALLISTIC
and asked the teacher "HOW in the name of heavens does DOPING a child up
RESOLVE THE GANG PROB and WHEN DID THE STATE OF TEXAS LICENSE YOU TO
PRESCRIBE MEDICINE!!!???" ... the teacher responded w/ "uhhhhhhhh, Mr. F we
DO NOT HAVE THOSE TYPES OF PROBS (gang) IN *OUR* SCHOOL SYST!!!" my dh
turned to me and said "go and collect dss' belongings and I'm going to the
office to WITHDRAW BOTH BOYS FROM SCHOOL" ... he proceeded to tell dt (dear
teacher), "you and the ps system have done QUITE ENOUGH DAMAGE, my child is
THROWING UP, CRYING b/c he's TERRIFIED TO COME TO SCHOOL b/c of these
threats, UHHHHHH THANKS, we'll TEACH THEM @ HOME!!!" I almost had a heart
attack right on the spot b/c I KNEW NOTHING ABOUT H-S'ING, I didn't even
know if it was LEGAL, etc, etc!!! ... I always say, we're the first h-s'rs
we ever knew!!! butttttttttt, fast forward three yrs later and we're
having the time of our lives and both dss are doing beautifully and my ONLY
regret is we didn't h-s our older dch, too :)

How many children do you have?

we have two generations of dch @ our house ... Sarah (24 yo), is married to
Matt, a wonderful fella, and we're expecting our 1st grandbaby (Kinsey
McLane or Samuel Alexander) in mid-Sept ... Matthew (21 yo) is a senior in
college (YES, *ONLY* 2 more years of college tuition; he's going to work on
his masters); our older dch weren't h-s'd ... THEN our 2nd generation of
dch, ds Taylor is 11 yo and ds Nash is 9 yo were adopted @ birth and both
are an absolute joy ...

What are your children's most recent interests? ds 11 is quite an artist,
he's drawn our Christmas cards since he was 4 yo, he'll read anything he can
get his hands on, is taking piano and violin lessons, too ... our *baby*
(9) enjoys piano, will begin saxophone lessons in the fall and enjoys
Lego's, k-nix (sp?) and working in the garden w/ his mama ... both boys are
basically fluent in sign language (I took ASL in college as my foreign
language) and they are involved in church activities ... just think, if we
were still STUCK in the system, they wouldn't have time after school for all
these NEAT things!!! both boys live and and breathe baseball, soccer,
basketball, swim team, and biking ... yes, sports season runs all year long
@ our house but I wouldn't have it any other way!!!

What area does your family live in?

in the suburbs of a very HOT south, TX ...

Do you have any pets?

... three dogs; two Dalmatians Charlie (4yo) and Dottie (11mths), Lady (13
yo), a very old Manchester Doberman who thinks like a BIG DOBIE ... two
cats; Simon (6yrs) and Ms. Wiggins (6 yrs), both felines just ADORE my dh
and *tolerate* the rest of us; give me a break!!! lol!!! ... we have
tropical fish galore ...

Anything else you would like to add?

we've h-s'd for 3 yrs (wow, time flies when you're having fun) ... we're
going to unschool for the first time this year b/c we're all REALLY SICK OF
WORKBOOKS and replicating *SCHOOL* ... it also really grates my last nerve
that MOST of the h-s curriculum available was written by h-s'ing moms but
it's taken me 3 yrs to finally figure this out; yes, I'm SLOW!!! what I
mean is, these people don't have anymore credentials than I do (I have a BA
in anthropology) and they're supplementing their income by writing h-s
curriculum; SORRY, I hope I'm not offending anyone!!! a h-s friend of
mine unschools her ds and once I saw what they were doing, I was HOOKED and
KNEW this would be perfect for my boys ... this spring I've been
reading/learning about Charlotte Mason and PUO (Parent's Union
Organization)... even though I don't agree w/ everything the CM and PUO
suggests, I'm going to modify some of their suggestions and use living books
which will suit our fm's needs ... isn't h-s'ing *grand*??? <G> ...

uhhhhhhhh ... dh and I will be married for 28 yrs (ACK, NO I'M NOT THAT OLD,
he robbed the cradle!!!) ... dh, Tim is an rr engineer and I'm a sahm ... we
cared for foster children for yrs but it b/c too difficult to say our
*good-byes*, so we decided it was time to give up that part of our lives ...
our fm is Southern Baptist and dh and I take care of the newborn nursery so
we can get our baby fix every Sunday ... we LOVE camping, esp in the spring
and winter (mind you, our winters are 50-60*s, lol) ... dh and I were the
first couple to have dad in the delivery room of the hospital we used and
when my babies were little I was very involved in the LLL and I also b-fed
our adopted babies, too ...

soooooo, that's about it, hope I didn't bore anyone w/ my novel <G> ...

Why do you un/homeschool:

We believe that children learn best if they are directing their own
learning. I know that no one knows my children better than we do and that
all we need to do is encourage them in pursuing their interests. I believe
we are al learning all the time and we don't need to separate learning from
life. Plus I don't want them exposed to the idea that "boys will be boys"
and that you have to fit into certain roles to be accepted in life.

How many children do you have?
2
Morgan who will be 6 on Wednesday
Colin who was 2 in March

What are your children's most recent interests?
Morgan is into all things Oz. He is reading the Oz series of books and
thinking of ways to convert them to plays, he is asking to learn to spell,
and wants to learn about the constitution so he can be president. He says
his first law will be that the $$$ has to be split equally :) He
especially loves Glinda and Ozma. He loves math and logic puzzles too.
Colin loves to draw and paint. He loves being read to and pretending to
cut the lawn.

What area does your family live in?
New York (60 miles from NYC)

Do you have any pets?

a frog

Anything else you would like to add?

I love this group, but I mostly lurk. I hope I'll have more to add soon.
We had to begin reporting to the state this year and I am trying my best
not to have that influence my outlook and actions wrt unschooling
