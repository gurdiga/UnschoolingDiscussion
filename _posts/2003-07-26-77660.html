We had dinner with my sister tonight, who later sent our parents and me
the following comment about Lexie.

"Lexie was very cute in her dress and shoes tonight. She was even
walking with some grace, like a young lady. Then it was too funny when

commented that they were also easier to get in and out of in order to
play in the mud!"

I thought this was not only relevant to our previous conversation
regarding boys/girls but also pretty darn cute.
