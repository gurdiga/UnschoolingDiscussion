Gluhwein:
1 bottle (24 oz.) red wine
1 cup water
1 cinnamon stick
2 whole cloves
1/2 lemon sliced
1/3 cup sugar
mix ingredients in a large pan and heat until almost boiling but do not boil,
serve hot
