Hello all!

My name is Nina and I have been lurking on the board for a few days now, and
am really enjoying the conversations, and the different posts and
information that all of you have been contributing.

My son 7 and daughter 3 have been unschooling all along... just sort of fell
into it via the way we began parenting and the books that led one to
another. It just made sense.

We recently moved to Washington state with it's yearly testing laws which
threw me into a tizzy and I almost blew everything, coming into contact for
the first time with the anxiety over making sure kids are up for passing the
test. And then, fortunately a wonderful book was recommended - Learning at
Home by Marty Layne and I feel that I've come out of the fog again, found my
footing again. And I've found you all!

Well, I thought it was time I came out of the bushes and introduced myself.
Thanks to all who have posted insights, personal experiences and opinions...
and the great stuff about mexican beans and newspaper hats!!!

Nina (who will use just about anything as an excuse not to mop the kitchen
floor!)
