I know it is frustrating to get alot of emails and not being able to
sort them easily. I get a lot of emails for various projects and
have sorted them on yahoo. (I love yahoo.)

What I did to resolve this: Go to the section for your group
settings and click on the part that says no emails. Then once a day,
or once a week, or whenever, you can just to to the group as it is
saved in your section in yahoo called "my groups." I love this
feature because it lowers my incoming emails. The downside is that
you might miss something. but these days, being so busy, it is good
for me to miss something.
