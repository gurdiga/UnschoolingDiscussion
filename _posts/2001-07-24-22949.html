This one isn't a hard-drive wiping-out virus. A friend cleaned his out last
night pretty easily. I have the link to the cleanup instructions if anyone
comes to need it, but the virus will probably come from someone you know and
have a title of something you've corresponded about before (because it uses
old e-mail trails somehow), but the text will be this:

<< > Hi! How are you?
>
> I send you this file in order to have your advice
>
> See you later. Thanks >>

I wouldn't just delete it. I would tell them they have a virus. You can
respond to the e-mail without it hurting your computer. They won't know
unless someone who got the e-mail tells them.
