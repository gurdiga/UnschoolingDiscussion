Our favorite search engine has a new service in beta. Not only does Google
let you search web sites, it is now letting you search print catalogs. This
is cool because they've included our new catalog that was only printed a few
weeks ago!

You can jump to pages, zoom in, or do searches. Just go to:
http://catalog.google.com/catalogs?hl=en&lr=&ie=UTF-8&safe=off&issue=14696&c
atpage=cover&catview=thumb

The URL is long so you may need to cut and paste if your mail reader wraps
it to two lines. As an alternative, you can also go to:
http://www.fun-books.com/catalog.htm and click on the picture of the catalog
cover.

Sorry, just had to share!

Billy
www.FUN-Books.com
