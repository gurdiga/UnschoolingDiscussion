Welcome Faith!
Many factors go into the sibling relationship, but I notice that
schooling families I know tend to often say "the children need time apart
from each other". Now mine are young, so I may change my views later,
but... It seems that as children grow and develop interests and friends
that time away from their siblings will occur as a result of that. I
don't think I need to put a lot of energy into artificially creating
separate spaces for each child.
My $.02!
