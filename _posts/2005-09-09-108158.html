For reasons of discipline and distraction.
If kids are having too much fun or irritating one another, or looking at one
another, or showing off for one another, they pay less attention to the
teacher.

That's one reason.

Another is that the same way they seem to think people have to get up and get
ready 180 times a year or more as practice for getting up and getting ready
the first day one has a real job, they claim that practice dressing acceptably
will help people dress acceptably for work when they go to work.
