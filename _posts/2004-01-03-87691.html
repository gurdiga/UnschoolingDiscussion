We finally closed up the house and turned on the ac. Don't see turning it
off anytime soon. Figures. I made sure I was ready for the cold weather this
year. Every year I don't worry about cold weather clothes for the kids. And
every year when it gets cold for awhile, I'm stuck in finding anything for
them to wear. Usually by that time, the stores start having spring fashions
out. So this year I prepared early. Each kid is set with long pants and
sweaters and even gloves this year. The kids complained last year at sports
camp that it was hard to run with their hands in their pockets. So far it's
all just been sitting in the closets.
