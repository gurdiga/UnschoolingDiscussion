our hospital has one of the 9 (I think) milk banks in the UK, yet my daughter received formula as her first meal.  IMO, if she was poorly enough to be on SCBU, she was poorly enough to receive donor milk, otherwise she could ahve been with me on the ward and we could have got on with our lives.  Just my experience.  <g>
 
Tracy
 
PS  I think all mothers who are capable of breastfeeding should at least TRY it for 4 to 6 weeks, since this is the general amount of time it takes for nursing to settle down, and it can take this amout of time to become established after any problems such as nipple confusion are resolved.  After all, no-one knows what they're missing unless they try it :-)  Doesn't mean I expect it to happen, mind you...
