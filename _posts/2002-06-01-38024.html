Hi Angi,

We are in Texas as well. You are free in Texas. The requirement means that
IF someone comes to your door and IF that person questions what kind of
education your son is getting and IF that person has the proper credentials,
you need to be able to show that y'all are doing something.

Written curriculum could mean library books, computer manuals, magazines,
cook books, instructions for the GameCube.... You do NOT have to have some
scope and sequence written out. You do NOT have to have lesson plans.

Julie
