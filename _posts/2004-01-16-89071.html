Netflix hands down. They have like 20 distribution centers to Mentura's one.
I'm over lapping right now and the service from Netflix is clearly winning
out. The overlap was due to frustration and a two week free trial. I think if
you live in California, Mentura would be ok, that's where their distribution
center is. Otherwise it can take a week to get a dvd shipped from Chicago and
checked into their system in order to get another back. I"m getting two day
turnaround from Netflix.

Also, Netflix has more movies. Mentura's bent is educational, Superman isn't
educational to them.

YMMV.

My guess is there might be a Canadian version, but I have not had good luck
with mail in and out of Canada. It's expensive and take way longer than U.S.
mail.

Those are sent a sort of first-class bulk, where Netflix pays the post office
by the lot as they're scanned in and out. That kind of mail can't go out of
the country. The mailer doesn't pay for it, and Netflix is probably paying
per thousand, from an account they have with the post office.
