I think a mother who firmly decides to breastfeed should do
so. I have 3 children and nursed all of them. Some people
were very uncomfortable with it and couldn't be in the same
room while I was doing it. Go with your gut feeling. No
matter what you do, people will question the decision you
make either way. AS IF it is their decision to make! My
husband was uncomfortable at first (his whole family was
that way!) But he realized what a great thing it was and
supported me when he saw the weight gain and money saved!
The rest of his family never came around but who cares, it
was our baby.
Good luck and stand firm, whatever you decide!
