A New Jersey homeschoolers' prom will be held at the Somerset Hills
Hotel in Warren, NJ, on April 25, 2003. All homeschoolers aged 13 or
older are invited, along with their guests. The prom will include
dinner, dancing with music provided by a professional DJ, and plenty
of adult chaperones. Dress is formal.

For more information, ticket sales, and a link to an online
discussion, head to: http://mywebpages.comcast.net/nuvakitty
