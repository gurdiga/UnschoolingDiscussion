NICKI,
Water really will help. It takes a while to get into the habit of
drinking that much. I have suffered from joint pain which is now almost
completely controlled when I drink enough water. It also does wonders
for your skin. I fill up water bottles, the required amount, and drink
them all day long.
Adding a zinc supplement to your diet a few days a week helps your skin
too. It's especially good for acne.
I have used kava kava for sleeplessness with great success. Check for
drug interactions if you're taking prescriptions.
Have you tried ear plugs at night? Some people can't stand them. Might
not be an option if there is no one else to get up with baby, or if baby
is too little still to come to you.
We used a noise machine when my husband needed to sleep during the
daytime. It creates a fuzzy, soft kind of sound which muffles outside
noise. It really worked.
A fan would work too but sometimes it's too cold for a fan.
I also use St. John's wort. I have heard some people can't take it, but
it really works for me. I had trouble with worry while taking care of an
ill family member. St. John's wort with kava kava has been a huge help.

Calcium can sometimes help with pain, and aid in sleep. If you wanted to
start simply you could try this. ( with lots of water!)

I hope you can find some relief. Maybe you would consider a visit to a
naturopath? There may also be acupuncture or acupressure treatments that
can help.
Hang in there.
Deb L
