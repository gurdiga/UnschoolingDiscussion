About the conference:

Do you have any of the following questions or concerns? How do children
learn? How do you unschool? Why does unschooling work? Can Christians
unschool? Who can't unschool? How do you get rid of the school mindset? What
about math? What about college? What about "special needs" children? What
happens when unschoolers grow up? How do you "trust" your child? What is
meant by "strewing their paths"? TV, computers. video games: are these
educational? All these questions and more will be answered!

There will be magicians, storytellers, artists, and more to entertain and
inspire you and your children! There will be no age-discrimination: all ages
may participate in any and all presentations/funshops. Bring the whole family
for a joy-filled weekend!

Featured presenters: Sandra Dodd, Anne Ohman, Lisa Bugg, & Joyce Fetteroll
from the unschooling.com message boards, Ned Vare & Luz Shosie, parents of a
grown unschooler; and Fiona Hutchison, herself unschooled and daytime star of
"One Life to Live".

For more information, visit our website, http://www.schoolsoutsupport.org

I hope you all can join us!
