The California State Education Department lists on their website the big
fancy learning standards that they have adopted for every grade level up
to 12th. They are pretty intimidating -- I certainly don't believe that

love over your grandson is going to be a lot more important than
stuffing knowledge in him.

The website:

http://www.cde.ca.gov/standards/

The standards are in PDF documents, arranged by grade.
