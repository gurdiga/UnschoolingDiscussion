I've recently stopped giving my son mini quizzes, such as asking him things
just to see if he 'got it'. On a trip to the zoo with his gma and gpa, they
were quizzing him. Which animal has the long neck, or trunk, etc. I'm sitting
uncomfortably, not knowing quite how to intervene, but my son did great. They
asked him a couple of questions about animals, then he quizzed them! Asked
them which animal had the hump, then when they answered he said, 'Good!' It
was a laugh for everyone and an eye opener too!
