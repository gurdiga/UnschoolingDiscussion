Katheryn Culwell

18 Feb 2011, at 1:05am

My boys lives revolve around BMX! They ride every day and have a really
cool BMX
community of friends that they ride with. Sometimes they go to
skateparks, sometimes dirt jumps, sometimes our house. This last fall the
boys built
a full size half-pipe in our yard (there is an unschooling story in there
too one day).

My boys subscribe to the magazine RideBMX.com . They watch youtube videos
and cable tv shows like Firsthand (fuel tv channel). Mostly they learn from
doing
and from riding with guys that show them how to do certain stunts.

The bike shop will be a great place to find out more info., find out where
the boys
can ride. Not all skateparks allow bikes. Some are bike only.

Have fun!

Katheryn
