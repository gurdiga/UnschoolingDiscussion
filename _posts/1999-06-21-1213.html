Re the current topic of conversation regarding unschooling high
schoolers, transcripts, diplomas, etc. ~ I would highly recommend
reading Grace Llewellyn's "The Teenage Liberation Handbook: How to Quit
School and Get a Real Life and Education." Before I read this book, I,
too, was "worried" about my unschooled high schooler's subjects, grades,
transcripts, and diploma; by the time I finished reading it, none of
that stuff mattered. A very powerful and *empowering* read.
