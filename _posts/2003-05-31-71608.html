I wouldn't worry too much about showing progress. We've only had one
portfolio review so far, but it was with an unschooling friendly person. The
portfolio was just a box full of miscellaneous from the year: literature from any
programs we attended, art work, stories she wrote, a book my daughter had just
finished reading. This year the progress is obvious to me. My daughter went
from a tentative reader to a "just about can read anything" reader. So I'll
mention that to the reviewer, but I won't prepare anything to quantify it.
