======================================

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UnschoolingDiscussion/

That's the URL of the yahoogroups page for this list. For me, to get
to yahoogroups, I just put in the URL box of my browser yahoogroups.com

That was their old address, and it still forwards.

One can go to that page and sign on. (And read messages, and look at
files, and lots of things.)

At the bottom of this, these addresses appear:

Group Email Addresses
Related Link:
http://sandradodd.com/lists/info
Post message:
UnschoolingDiscussion@yahoogroups.com
Subscribe:
UnschoolingDiscussion-subscribe@yahoogroups.com
Unsubscribe:
UnschoolingDiscussion-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com
List owner:
UnschoolingDiscussion-owner@yahoogroups.com
That middle one is something you could pass on to your friends. But
tell them we ask people to read a couple of weeks first. Someone
just left the list and wrote us a long complaint about having been
asked not to post the day she joined, and not to have sigline
advertising a how-to-teach-reading-and-you-need-phonics webpage. So
she quit the day after she joined. But an e-mail goes to all new
members explaining WHY we want people to read a while.

If there's anyone here who's been on this list a ong time, before
that e-mail went to new members, or who has just forgotten, please
review this before you you invite lots of other people, because we
don't want to make your friends grumpy. (That info is at the top
link above, "Related link")
