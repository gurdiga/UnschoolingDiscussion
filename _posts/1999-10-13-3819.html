Hi all

Following on from a piece of humour received recently: "Girls' Prayer"
and "Boys Prayer" (basically about shopping and beer respectively, and
in the form of the Lord's Prayer - if anyone hasn't seen this and
would like to, please email me for it privately), I have been tossing
this one over for the last couple of days - thought you'd enjoy it. I
do not mean to be derisive to the Lord's Prayer, or to cause anyone
offense by this - I am simply copying it's form.

Our Inner Wisdom, which art in our hearts
Hallowed be our Unschooling.
Thy peace shall come
Thy will be done in home as it is in NZUnschoolers 8-)
Give us this day our dose of sanity
And forgive us our stuff-ups
As we forgive those who send theirs to school 8-)
And lead us not into temptation to send ours to school
And deliver us from school-at-home.
For ours is the knowledge, the power and the glory
Forever and ever
UNSCHOOLING!

Cheers
Mandy Tyler from New Zealand
