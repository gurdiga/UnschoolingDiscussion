I'm curious why sugar is prohibited. I assume it's for the obvious reasons,
the same reasons anyone would ban or limit sugar, until I get to the part
that honey is ok. Honey and sugar are the SAME as far as the body's
concerned. So I'm just curious if what sugar does to the body, is the reason

know there's really no difference (as far as what it does in/to your body)
between the two. Lara..........
