We are in NE OH. Our hs coop involves around 20 families right now--last time there were about 10-12. Most classes are facilitated by parents who want to share and explore a passion. Some are taught by kids. We are not a legal entity, although since we use a church, we do sign a liability waiver.  Classes include watercolor, cool art stuff, Spanish, German, chemistry, creative writing, theater for kids, swing/ballroom dance, baseball skills, ancient history (Greek), human body, games, young scientists (for the littles).
 It's about as much structure as I can handle...
