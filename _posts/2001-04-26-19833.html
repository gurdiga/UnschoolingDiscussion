Dear Fellow Homeschooler,

Admit it! Homeschooling does have its funny moments. And
Homeschooling Humor is the topic for an upcoming segment in NHEN's
website. I'm gathering humor pieces for the HS Humor POV. I have
several that have crossed my screen over the years and have kept for
posterity, but I know there's more out there - some of which hasn't
even been written yet. We're hoping this project will inspire
homeschool humorists and pundits across the country to share their
wit.

If you've collected any of the many homeschooling jokes that have
flown through cyberspace over the years, please forward them to me at
<mailto:cmoxley@...>cmoxley@.... I will make every attempt
to credit the original author of the joke or anecdote if that person
can be found.

If you yourself have created a joke, humorous essay or anecdote that
shows the lighter side of homeschooling, we'd love to include it.
Again, just send to <mailto:cmoxley@...>cmoxley@...

Jokes, limericks, anecdotes, essays . . . if it's funny, send it in!

So! Once again . . .How many homeschoolers does it take to change a
light bulb? (We'd love to have MANY punch lines in answer to this!)
And don't stop there . . . Why did the homeschooler cross the road?
What do you get when you cross a homeschooler with an elephant? What
time is it when a homeschooler sits on a fence? Silly, creative,
funny . . . your joke could end up on the pages of NHEN!!!

Please forward this to your homeschooling friends.

Carol
