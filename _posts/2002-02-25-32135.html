> a simple explanation to why dark clothes absorb more heat than light
> colored :o) I can't explain it in kid language hehehehe

White light is made up of all the colors. When white light hits a colored
object, it reflects the light of the color of the object and absorbs the
rest, turning it into heat. Since white is made up of all the colors a white
object reflects back all the light that hits it and absorbs none of it.
Black relfects back none of it and absorbs all of it.
