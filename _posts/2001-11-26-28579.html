I used goldenseal to get rid of a sinus infection a few years ago.
It's probably not a good choice if you have high blood pressure. Ask at
your health food store for a good organic, cultivated variety as the wild
plant is endangered. You can buy it in capsule form. It burns a little
in the tummy,so eat first. Also, have you tried a Netti pot? (sp) I've
never used one but have heard good things about them.
Sinus infections hurt like heck! I hope you feel better soon.

I'm not at all into natural healing, but I get sinus infections with
charming regularity about four times a year, so I know how to deal with them
sans antibiotics.

I sometimes use sudafed and tylenol for a few days if the pain is bad, and
after that I drink a LOT of hot tea and hot chocolate to "break things up".

They always cure themselves this way after about five or six days, about two
of those days are feverish enough that I don't drive, etc.
