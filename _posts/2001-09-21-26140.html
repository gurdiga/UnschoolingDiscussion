Cheesy Cockpot Ham and Potatoes
Need:
2c cooked cubed ham
4c potatoes, peeled and sliced thinly
2c shredded cheddar
diced onion (optional and to taste)
1 can cream of chicken or cream of celery soup, undiluted

Layer all ingredients except for soup in the crockpot in this order: ham,
(onion) potatoes, cheese, making 3 layers. Spread undiluted soup over all and
cook 4-5 hours on high or 8-10 hours on low, stirring occasionally. Makes
great leftovers and my kids love this!!
