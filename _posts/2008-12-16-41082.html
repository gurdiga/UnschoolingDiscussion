I think more options to public school are better than less! So, in
general, I think umbrella schools are a wonderful thing.

Ideally, there should be no need of such a thing because, in my own
little ideal world scenario, there would be no laws regulating
homeschooling, therefore, no need to jump through the hoops of an
umbrella school to avoid intrusive testing and oversite.

So, all in all, the disadvantage that I see of using an umbrella school,
is perpetuating the need of it.
