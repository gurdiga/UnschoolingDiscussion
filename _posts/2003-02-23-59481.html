Have a Nice Day!

23 Feb 2003, at 4:23pm
I just had a thought the other day.

I wonder how much of the conflict between parents and children really has to do with how a child's behavior is assumed to be a reflection on his/her parents and their parenting.

I am guessing 90% of the "rules" that abound in most homes comes directly from parents worrying about their own image.

Kristen

Today is even more important than tomorrow because "today" is a gift, and "tomorrow" might never come.

Today is where hope lives because today is when we can make things better than yesterday.

The only thing we can be sure of is today and life isn't worth living if it isn't lived in joy for as many moments of today as we can manage.
