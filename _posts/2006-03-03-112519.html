Just a reminder -- the 9th nonsectarian InHome (formerly HOUSE)
Homeschool Conference will kick off one week from tomorrow, March 10
and 11 at the Sheraton Chicago Northwest in Arlington Heights,
northwest of Chicago.

"This is always my yearly shot in the arm to help me know I'm doing
the right thing and am still on track."
-- Past conference attendee

The conference will include over 150 children's and 70 adult
workshops. Featured speakers this year will be Patrick Farenga
(Growing Without Schooling), Penny Gardner (Charlotte Mason Study
Guide), and Robin Schneider (Teaching Science Skills at Home without
being a Rocket Scientist).

The Vendors Hall (over 50 Vendors), as always, is open to the
public. Check their websites at:

http://www.homeeducatorsconference.org/vendorlist.html

New this year:

- Tapes/CDs of workshops available for purchase
- Parent Chat Room
- Teen Poetry Slam
- Talent Show

Not to mention the ever-popular Science Fair, Children's Craft and
Flea Market, Family Dance with dj and a special performance by the
Muntu Dance Theatre of Chicago, showcasing the art of West African
dance.

At this time, hotel rooms are still available at a discounted rate.

We hope you can make it -- it won't be the same without you!

For information please go to www.homeeducatorsconference.org.

Maureen O'Grady
