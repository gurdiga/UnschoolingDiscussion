SCHOOL’S OUT SUPPORT

presents

DAVID ALBERT
author of
And The Skylark Sings With Me


April 25, 2003

St. Michael and All Angels’ Episcopal Church
Corner North Trenholm and Bridgewood Roads
Columbia, South Carolina

SOS is delighted to host David Albert, the author of And the Skylark Sings
with Me~Adventures in Homeschooling and Community Based Education and a new
homeschooling book, Homeschooling and the Voyage of Self-Discovery: A Journey
of Original Seeking . He is also editor of the new two-volume set : The
Healing Heart~Families Storytelling to Encourage Caring and Healthy Families
and The Healing Heart Communities: Storytelling to Build Strong and Healthy
Communities. He writes a regular column, My Word! for "Home Education
Magazine" and is well known in the homeschooling world. Signed and inscribed
copies of his books will be available at the presentation.

There will be two inspirational talks with an optional supper:
4:00 "Don't Worry, Be Happy" (also called "The Curriculum of Happiness")
6:00 Spaghetti Supper (vegetarian option available)
7:00 "Learning About Learning, Conversations with my Violin"

Fees:
$12.00 for one talk
$16.00 for one talk and supper

$22.00 for both talks and supper

Proceeds will benefit the Scholarship Fund for the Live & Learn Unschooling
Conference, August 22-24, 2003.

For more information, contact Kelly Lovejoy @ (803)776-4849 or visit our
website - http://www.schoolsoutsupport.org
