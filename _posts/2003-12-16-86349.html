Zoombinis is awesome! My 4 yr. old plays it all the time as do I. We bought
ours from a college student selling bundled games and books this summer. I
think we also got Might Math Numbers with it also.

BUT, today I was at Scholastic Books Warehouse sale and spotted them. I think
regular price was 14.00 so discount price for HSers and teachers is 7.00. Go
to www.scholastic.com and click teachers and then the link on the side for
the warehouse sales near you.

For those who do not know the scholastic sales we HSers/Unschoolers can
attend. Some ask for HS paperwork while others (here in Maine) never ask.
Everything on the shelves is 50% off and here there are like 8 double sided large
isles of books, pens pencils and some toys.

This is where we bought Dustin's Harry Potter books 1/2 off! Captain
Underpants, goosebumps, Box Car Kids and they also have Judy Bloom, books on America
etc....
Our sale has been for almost two weeks and ends this Friday.
