April 26
The current issue of Splash Magazine for kids, a SAM's Club publication,
includes their regular column on homeschooling. This quarter's topic is what
homeschoolers do during the summer. The article includes a mention of NHEN,
and quotes from NHEN interview volunteers from across the country. Splash is
not available online, but you may be able to pick up a copy in your local
club.
