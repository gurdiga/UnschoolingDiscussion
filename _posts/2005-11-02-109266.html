2 Nov 2005, at 10:52am

On Halloween night my dd met up with one of her friends from
school. At first it was kind of awkward. This girl started
bragging about all the "cool" stuff in junior high. At first I was
thinking (OMG), my dd will want to return to school, (she's been
gone for 3yrs now). My dd turned to her friend and said that was
nice and smiled.

Then in the car on the way home from the party she told me she is so
happy that she is home. "They just don't know how good I have it."
Apparently through out the night this girl wouldn't stop going on
and on how great school is. My dd said "she was trying to make me
jealous because I'm homeschooled."

I was impressed with my dd(11 yo) and how she handled the whole
thing. If it were me I would have gone on and on about how great I
had it at home and how what a waste school is.

Kay
