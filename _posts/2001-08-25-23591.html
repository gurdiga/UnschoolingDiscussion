Hi All:

Dawn here back from the beautiful Coronado Island in California. We thought
we were in heaven. Our first family vacation in 7 years and the kids loved
it. I couldn't help noticing how many people we met kept on saying to Zak
"so your going to be 6 this month, you must be going into Kindergarten or 1st
Grade this year" I would say "actually we are thinking about homeschooling
him".... after about the 10th person had said this to Zak he said "actually
mummy and daddy are thinking about me having school at home".... Steve and I
just smiled....

Not happy to be back in the heat of Arizona but happy to be home in order to
put our house on the market and move to either Washington State, Oregon or
California. Knowing that there is support for our lifestyle choices wherever
we go.

Dawn
