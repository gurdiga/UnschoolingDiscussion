We don't watch commercial tv. The only thing we have
watched in the last few months is some of the Stanley
Cup playoff games on ESPN, Playhouse Disney in the
morning for the kids, or Nick Jr. On some occassions
we enjoy catching shows on A&E, The History Channel,
Animal Planet, Discovery and The Learning Channel.
Most of the time if we, or they, want to watch tv, we
put in videos. That gives us a lot more control of
what they are watching, which I think at their age is
important, and leaves out the ads, which I think are
really bad.

We are getting rid of cable pretty soon, as it is a
lot of money we are spending for a service that we
hardly utilize. Since our tv is in the big family
room in the basement, that menas we probably will not
get any reception of channels after the cable is gone.
