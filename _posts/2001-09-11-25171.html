Hi Kimberly,

2 of my kids attended school after we moved back to an old neighborhood
where they had gone to school prior to coming home. (did that make any
sense?) They would be attending school with all their old friends and
wanted to go. Very sadly, I agreed. My son even had to get shots to go and
still wanted to.

The school was kind of jerky about it, very clearly assuming that the kids
would be way behind (in 2nd grade and K, if you can believe that). They
tested the 2nd grader and she did fine. The kids had a ball....initially.
They bounced right out of bed....initially. They even did their
homework....initially. Within 3 months, they both asked to come home and
have never mentioned going to school again.
