I just had to speak up. My girls are very big fans of Harry Potter. They have spent a great deal of time online finding the best sites on Harry Potter. This one is so much fun. If you haven't been to it, you've missed on a great chance to hear the words pronounced. Follow this link, then under Harry's head there is a link for Pronunciations, go to that, scroll down to find the word you want... click on the word, and like magic, it says the word for you. : )

http://www.scholastic.com/harrypotter/books/index.htm
