I believe that unschooling is more then a way to "school" it is a
philosophy and a way of life. I am not lerning just because i do not pay to
go to a college anymore. and I am not done learning just cus i have a
masters. I will continue to learn. I love learning and I believe that I
know best how to learn and i know what I want to learn.

yes there are some things kids need to learn but that is up to us as
parents to do. and many of them do not have to be in an exact time frame.

Unschooling says alot about my priorities in life. I believe that I am in
charge of my family and my life not the govt. or even a religion.
