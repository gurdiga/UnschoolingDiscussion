Grandma and us had the same phone number when we moved here.

I think technically this one would be correct. You check with the
verbs. Grandma had. Us had. Nope. Doesn't work. Grandma had. We
had. Yep. Works. But We had the same phone number as Grandma when we
moved here sounds a lot better.

Same rule applies, as when you to other sentences. John and me or John and
I. Is it correct if you leave off "Grandma and" So would "us" had the same
phone number be right or would "we" had the same phone number be correct.
(it is "we") The third choice should be "We had," not "all of us."
