This is one of my favorite unschooling quotes because Laura lived into
the 1950's and there's no way her parents could have forecast accurately
what she would need to know in her adult life.

Her Pa says:" I wouldn't wonder if you'll live to see a time, Laura,
when pretty nearly everybody'll ride on railroads and there'll hardly be
a covered wagon left."

Imagine.
