Starhawk and Margot Adler are two good authors to try, as others have been
suggesting. Try Starhawk's "Sprial Dance" and Adler's "Drawing Down the
Moon." Also look for books by Scott Cunningham, Janet and Stewart Farrar
and Gerina Dunwich. Look up Pagan books at any book store (online or in
person) or they an be found under categories such as Neo-Paganism,
Witchcraft (a much maligned and misunderstood classification that many
Pagans avoid and others cherish) Shamanism, etc. Pagan refers to a whole
host of belefs from many different cultural origins and is not specific to
any ONE set of beliefs. My advice would be to sample some authors until
you find material that you are comfortable with and connect with, and look
for authors connected with the same beleif system.
