Hail Brian Jacques! The author of the Redwall books. My son Nicholas, 9
years old, reads them for hours on end. You would be able to purchase them
anywhere. Or at least you should be able to. Some of the book titles are
Redwall, Marlfox, Pearls of Lutra, Legend of Luke, Mossflower, Mattimeo,
Mariel of Redwall, Salamandastrom, Martin the Warrior, The Bellmaker,
Outcast of Redwall, The long Patrol, The Great Redwall Feast (this is a
short BEAUTIFUL book, that even my 5 year old enjoys). There are probably
more, but he has read all but two of these books. They range anywhere from
about maybe 350 to 450 or so pages, except for the Redwall Feast, it's only
about 30 pages or so, with gorgeous illustrations. The books are just
fantastic. There are parts in them with some of the characters getting
killed or whatnot, but I feel Nicholas has dealt with the stories fine, and
they fuel his imagination tremendously.

We have checked most of them out at the library, however they would be nice
to own and we are planning on getting his Brian Jacques library expanded
soon.

Have fun! I'm sure your son/daughter will love them!

Also, just a quick note, we also ADORE Jane Yolen, good books, full of
imagination and magical ideas and lore. She's written many books, just do a
search....
