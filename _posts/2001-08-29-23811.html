alcohol was most prominent either 30 or 90 minutes after intake and then
evaporated fairly quickly. so i always thought timing was the most important.
just what i remember, but not too sure. ds is 3 1/2 and we just quit nursing

strawberry tofu popsicles
1 1/2 c. firm silken tofu
1 1/2 c. fresh ripe strawberries
1/2 c. honey or other sweetener
1/4 c. oil
1 T. lemon juice
1 t. vanilla
pinch of salt

this is actually a recipe for pudding. the recipe says to blend and let sit
overnight, which i did and it wasn't firm enough. so we made popsicles and i
was impressed! they tasted great and had the texture of an ice cream bar.
