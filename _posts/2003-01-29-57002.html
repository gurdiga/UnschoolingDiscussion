You don't need a book. The evidence is all around you. I posted about my
brother the chemist who flunked out of college. I have a graduate degree
but haven't used it in years. I teach karate and raise goats (both of which
I love).

Johnny Depp dropped out of high school and when he decided he was crazy and
tried to go back, the principal told him "No really, please don't." And
encouraged him to follow his passions.

If you follow your heart and are willing to work hard, you can make a
living. Perhaps not a fortune, but a living.
