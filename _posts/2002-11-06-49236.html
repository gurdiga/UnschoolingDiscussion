I just have to tell you all about a really fun thing that we've been
doing lately: geocaching. Check out: http://www.geocaching.com

You need a GPS, a vehicle, and a whole lot of patience :). There are
various "caches" around the world. Some are virtual (where you find the
spot and note what you were supposed to find) and some have actual
treasure hidden. This is like the ultimate treasure hunt and a whole
lot of fun. I'm sounding like a commercial. You can look at the site
and do a search for how many are in your zipcode. We are planning on
putting out our own cache sometime soon. There are lots of rural and
urban ones. We've done several that were in city parks and one that was
just a sign in the middle of a bunch of businesses.

-Lisa in AZ
