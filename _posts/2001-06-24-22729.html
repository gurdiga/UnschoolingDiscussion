Linda -

Thank you so much for the book "The First Year of Homeschooling Your
child"!! And thank you so much for letting me be a part of it!

I had just arrived in Lake George, NY (where I'll be spending the
summer), after a two-week trip from Florida. My husband was already
here, and had brought up the mail that had piled up over the two
weeks. When I saw the book package, I was surprised, as I have been
trying to be good about not buying so many books (my addiction). And
imagine my surprise when I opened it, and realized it was the book to
which I had emailed a contribution over a year ago!

I turned to my husband and said "Did you see the book package?" He
frowned and said he had, and I was pleased to tell him that not only
didn't I buy it, but that I/we were in it!

It looks like a wonderful book (with all our unpacking I haven't had
a change to read it yet)! I actually "counsel" homeschool moms
through our church, and I look forward to sharing it with them and
many other homeschool moms!

Thank you!
Robin
Orlando, FL/Lake George, NY
