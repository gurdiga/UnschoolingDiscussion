One of my main sources of info on Praise is "Punished By Rewards" as I've no doubt said before. A fellow unschooler used it to transform a small bank branch into a behemoth of a sales organization by stopping all the Corporate "praise" devices (contests, commissions, etc.) within his branch. People (and kids are people too) can smell a phony concept a mile away. "Turd in punchbowl" obvious!

Same with praise after the kids figure it out. "Mom doles out small amounts so I expect that I can make her do it maore often." It creates an upward spiralling cycle of manipulation. Action, praise, action again, gold star, action again, candy, ... action again, sports car. And a hollow feeling inside because the rewardee has no idea WHAT THEY want! Reward breeds unhappiness!

Or if Mom won't praise, I'll withdraw and not play. I'm no rat in a cage!" Reward breeds sloth and apathy!

My (radical) thots
