I have two boys...birthdays...10/27/90 and 7/31/92...

My oldest being born on the even month gets to have control of the TV remote
and control of the nintendo on even days...but he is also assigned some of
the more unpleasent and intensive chores on the even days as well...On Odd
days...he gets unlimited to limited access to the computer depending on if I
am using it...or if we need to go out and about...

My younger son being born during the odd month gets the TV and nintendo on
odd days as well as the chores that the oldest has on even days and gets the
computer on Even days...

So far that is working out to a degree...the rules are that if they choose
not to be on the computer, the tv, or the nintendo on their given days then
the other child gets to use it...but the other child has to give way when the
primary user wants thier time back...does that make sense...?

That was the easiest way for me cuz I could never remember who used what
last....lol...I can see this mite work for more children if one thinks about
days divisible by three and four and five...
