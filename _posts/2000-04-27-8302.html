With all my good intentions of disseminating information about Debbie and her Uncle, and requesting recipe submissions for Debbie's fundraising cookbook, I wasn't aware that a kak virus was also being sent to everyone.  Thanks to a couple of eGroup members who alerted me to the fact that this virus had been transmitted with my e-mail, I was able to use my Norton AntiVirus program and eventually remove the kak virus from my computer.  It was found lurking in the signature part of my Outlook Express. 
 
I, therefore, apologize for any problems or inconvenience this has caused others.  I do want everyone to know about this problem, so steps can be taken immediately to remove the virus from your systems.
 
'Sorry!
 
Linda
