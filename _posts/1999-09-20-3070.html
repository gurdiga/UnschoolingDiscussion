Print this up real purty' and have it laminated. Then you can show it to
those who question you.

"This card entitles the bearer to:
Homeschool their children to the best of their knowledge and ability;
Live a full, happy and enriching family life complete with love,
laughter and learning;
Not have to apologize for their lifestyle or beliefs;
Disregard the judgements and pronouncements of uninformed persons;
Not be a mouthpiece or mobile public educator about the subject of
Homeschooling, unless they WANT to
Not have to prove themselves worthy as parents or Homeschoolers to every
(well meaning or otherwise) person
who questions them.
