Hello Everybody!
My daughters and I were going through my old stamp collection I
happened to come across at my mothers. I was telling them about my
penpals from Japan and China I once had as a child. It got us to
thinking...would anyone like to be an "old fashioned penpal?" aka
snail mail.
My daughters are 10 and 7. Too many interest to really list. The
basics...LOVE art, reading & writing, anything outdoors, computers
and did I mention ART?! That's very big in our house.
If anyone is interested please contact us at our email address and we
can exchange addresses. Be sure to place *PenPals* in the subject
line to avoid being deleted by mistake.
**Mom's & Dad's....this is open to you as well if you would like to
write also. I love to write letters just as much as I like to receive
them. It's nice to find a friendly letter amoungst all the bills!
*grin*. I enjoy meeting new people and learning from their lives.
Excited to meet people from all over the world. I love playing games
with our children. Working with Stained Glass, Clay, Copper and all
other art forms. I love photography and scrapbooking. I would love to
learn about stamping. Love reading, writing and anything involving
nature for the most part. I love traveling, just can't afford to do
it as often as I would like. I enjoy working with our children as we
find and sell items on ebay. It has been such fun. check us
out...ebay seller name: online_annie
I hope to hear from you! You can write just about anything*, day to
day thoughts or experiences, life in general. (*except religion.
Sorry, not a religious person.)
~Annie
