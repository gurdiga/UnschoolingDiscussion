Did anyone watch "Shackleton" on A&E?

I had read his book "South" so I was pumped. My older kids (11 and 8) got
into it as well. We ended up staying up late, eating popcorn, having out
the globe to follow the exploits. (Our globe even has Shackleton's base
camp denoted which was cool). I showed the kids some of the pictures in
"South" and we saw the scenes that matched the pictures exactly (like the
officers washing the galley floor). It made it all seem very real.

Dh taped the second night for us because we had karate and so now even the
little guys are watching the daring do over and over. Everyone is running
around saying "Bloody hell" in our best English accents which sent us off
laughing over other English sayings we have heard friends from the UK use.
It has been fun.

Julie
