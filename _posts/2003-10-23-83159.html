Hello from a silent reader,

a groups of Dutch Home-educators just started an email action to our
government. As you might know (or not) home education, let alone
unschooling, is not a legal possibility in our country as such. We ourselves
e.g. are still in court after almost 4 years. Next week a meeting will take
place between the Minister of education and a educational committee of
members of parliament where this subject will discussed. International input
is very much appreciated!

Background information, sample mails and emailadresses can be found at
http://www.thuisonderwijs.net/support-nl
and at a mirror at http://www.nvvto.nl/support.html

Thank you in advance for your time,

greetings,
hartelijke groeten,
Bernadette van Zuidam
unschooling mom of two in The Netherlands
