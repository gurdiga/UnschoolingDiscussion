I've been reading 'Punished by Rewards' by Alfie Kohn and this
passage was so apropo to some of the discussion I thought I'd share
it:

From the subchapter "Tighter Control: The Case of Special Education"

"Consider the fate of students who are said to be 'learning
disabled,' a category so elastic that virtually all of us could be
diagnosed that way in one situation or another."


I think this sums up my feelings on disabilities.
