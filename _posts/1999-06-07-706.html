For math anxious parents I highly recommend the book Math: Facing an
American Phobia by Marilyn Burns (also the author of books for kids--The I
Hate Mathematics Book and Math for Smarty Pants). Burns writes well about
math phobia and offers concrete ways to teach math so as to avoid
perpetuating the anxiety in our children. She's not writing from a
homeschooling point of view, but I found a lot of encouragement for our
math-in-context approach and she helped me relax about paper-and-pencil math
skills (which are not as important as schools might have us believe).
