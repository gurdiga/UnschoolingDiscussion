I don't know his website, but I've shared the podium with Gordon Neufeld up
in British Columbia, and he is wonderful. He has brought attachment
parenting to a largely very conservative Christian audience, some of whom
had been tempted to embrace "Ezzo-related" practices (which I personally
believe are a form of child abuse.)

Unfortunately, (and I know him personally), Gordon has been afflicted with
severe writer's block for years, and so you'll see very little in print.
But you can find similar material on the Natural Child Project website
(www.naturalchild.org) run by my old friend Jan Hunt up in British Columbia.

David
