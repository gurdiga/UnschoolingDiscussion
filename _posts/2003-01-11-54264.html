Brenda Rose

10 Jan 2003, at 6:45pm

Have them "seek beauty":

In people - discuss difference between physical and personality/spiritual
In nature
In man-made objects -discuss form vs. utility
In architecture
Your meeting place, neighborhood, community
In their homes - houses vs. family (what makes a 'home'?)
In words - stories, poetry

Tell about "most beautiful" character from a story, movie, tv show, etc. -
again, look for different types of beauty.

They can draw, write/dictate, create nature collages/ other ideas that have
been shared...

Have a wonderful morning with this!

Brenda R.
