Attention, teenage explorers, pioneers, and globetrotters!



After a six-week journey through Argentina this fall, Unschool Adventures is pleased to
announce our next adventure on the Hawaiian island of Maui for the entire month of April,
2009!

Argentina was a thrilling adventure for our nine unschooled teen students (ages 15-19),
and we are excited to continue the Unschool Adventures approach to unbridled learning
and exploration. Grab your swimsuit and a pair of flip flops and join Not Back To School
Camp advisors, Blake Boles and Abbi Miller, for a healthful and challenging experience of a
lifetime. The theme of this trip is "Health is Wealth": learning how to care for your body.
Students will learn to plan and prepare nutritious meals, keep their bodies active and
healthy, and experience a full month of overall mind-body wellness. Other activities
include biking, hiking, daily yoga on the beach, and possibly surfing & windsurfing
lessons. You'll also get the chance to experience authentic Hawaiian culture, complete the
world's longest down-hill bike ride (down a volcano!), devour fresh coconuts and papayas,
and discover lush tropical beaches! The trip will consist of 10-12 students, ages 15-19
(flexible), and two trip leaders. The group will stay in a rented house near the town of Paia
for the duration of the course.

The total program fee is $3,999, which includes lodging, activities, transportation in Maui,
and all-you-can-eat delicious & healthful food. Students are responsible for covering
personal incidentals, traveler's insurance, and roundtrip airfare to Maui.

To view the full details of the trip and to learn how to apply, please visit our website:
http://www.unschooladventures.com
Enrollment begins January 1, 2009. The Argentina trip filled up quickly, so don't wait long
to send your application!

Thanks, and we hope to see you on sunny Maui!

-Abbi & Blake, Unschool Adventures founders & trip leaders
