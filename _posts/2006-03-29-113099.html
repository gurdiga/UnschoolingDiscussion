Have to share this sweet moment.

I drove my 13yod to her play rehearsal. It's a fairly small building, she
is acquainted with many of the friendly people who spend a lot of time
there, and there is no way that she could get lost on her way to the class.

As we got close to the parking lot, she asked, "Are you going to walk me
in?" I said, "Do you want me to?" She replied, "Yes".

I walked with her to the sign-in sheet and the door of the rehearsal. She
blew me a kiss and said, "Love ya."
