What county are you moving too?  We live in Eastpointe which is Macomb county.  If you look at the palm of your right hand, we are at the bottom thumb knuckle <g>.  Our city borders Detroit - we "eastsiders" love this part of town, low traffic, excellent freeway system, all in all nice folk*s*.  Better than the other suburbs of Motown - horrible traffic, not "as" nice folks.
 
Anywho - I am involved in a group called Michigan-MacombCountyHomeschoolers.  We have an email loop on yahoogroups.  We do great stuff, bowl once a week, have parties, go to the youththeatre, museums, parkdays, you name it.  There is only one other unschooling family in the group - but the other families (approx 20) are very welcoming and supportive.  Let me know where you are moving too, and hopefully we can come up with some contacts.
